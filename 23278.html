Yossi Itzkovich <Yossi.Itzkovich_at_ecitele.com> writes:
> Hi,
>
> After just loading fvwm2 with pager , I see that 150 colors were allocated !!
> !
> in .fvwm2rc I see only 2 references of colors, and I have added
> ColorLimit 60
>
> So, where did all the color go?
> What can I do to reduce the colors of fvwm?

You need to put "ColorLimit 60" before your first use of any pixmap
in your .fvwm2rc. If you have done this, how many colors is fvwm2 using
now?

You want to make sure you are not using gradients anywhere.
