Hi,

the Open source and free Software Developers' European Meeting will take
place in Brussels the 3rd and 4th february 2001, with speakers including
fyodor (nmap), richard Stallman (FSF), Rasmus Lerdorf (PHP), Richard Morell
(Smoothwall), Axel Hecht (Mozilla), Johan Vos (Blackdown), Jeremy Allison
(Samba), Amnon Barak (Mosix), Philippe Biondi (LIDS), REnaud Deraison
(Nessus), David Faure (KDE), Mathieu Lacage (Gnome), Werner Koch (GnuPG
internals), Kevin Lawton (Plex86), Ami Ganguli (Midgard), and others......

It will take place in Brussels, at the Universite Libre de Bruxelles, from
9:30am to 6:30pm. Information will be distributed about activities in
Brussels for non technical spouses/husbands/children.
We have two auditoria of 250 people each + 8 classes (capacities from 25 to
50 people) all with network connection, projectors,.....

At midday, sandwiches and drinks will be sold by the Cercle Informatique of
the ULB students. T-shirts will be printed for the occasion. But to know how
much t-shirts we have to print, please register to help us evaluate the
number of visitors....

We also set up a mailing list so visitors can have contacts before the event,
possibly to arrange transportation and stay for people coming from the same
region. Entrance is free as this is organised on a voluntary basis by people
from the community, with the strong support of VA Linux.

All info, such as speakers, program, location is available at
http://www.osdem.org . Don't forget to sign up to be sure we print enough
t-shirts ;-)

Thanks

Raph
