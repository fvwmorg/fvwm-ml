On Wed, Sep 01, 2004 at 10:51:38AM +0530, Subbiah wrote:
> Hi All,
>
> I am having fvwm 2.4.3 with me. I am using fvwm95 style in my PC.
>
> In my mozilla browser (ver 1.3), The popup menus are coming with out close
> button at the right corner of the window.
> Similar way for the Preference selection window, I am getting only minimize
> button.
>
> How to get all the three buttons for the mozilla windows?

Try

  Style * NoFuncHint, NoDeconHint

This tells fvwm to ignore the hints that disable these buttons
(actually, one of these styles is sufficient, but I don't know
which one).

> In Mozilla in Fullscreen mode hides my Taskbar. How to prevent this also?

You can't make the mozilla window smaller because fullscreen mode
means the whole screen, regardless of what's on it. You can put
the Taskbar on a higher layer, e.g. with

  Style FvwmTaskBar Layer 20

In 2.5.x, the EwmhBaseStruts command defines aread near the screen
edges that are not used by maximized windows, but that does not
affect fullscreen windows.
