Walter Dnes <waltdnes_at_waltdnes.org> writes:
> The subject says it all. How do I uninstall 2.4.4 to upgrade to
> 2.4.5 ? Or can I simply install over top ? Will installing over top
> leave ~/.fvwm alone ?

In every case I know about you don't have to uninstall anything,
you can install right over what you have.

Installation will not change what you have in ~/.fvwm.
