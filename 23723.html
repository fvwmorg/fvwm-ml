On 25 February 1999, William Denton wrote:

: And, in fact, section 3.3 of the FAQ is "How do I get Alt-Tab behaviour
: like another GUI?" Sorry, I thought I'd read it.

Sorry to send out three messages in a row, but I now realize the best
place to look is the WindowList section of the fvwm2 man page. There's
better information there than in the FAQ.

The man page says to use

Key Tab A M WindowList "Prev FlipFocus"

which, when you hit Alt-Tab twice in quick succession, flips you back to
the window you were just in. However, it wasn't raising that window, so I
created a function named Raise-and-FlipFocus:

AddToFunc "Raise-and-FlipFocus" "I" Raise
+ "I" FlipFocus

and then defined Tab with

Key Tab A M WindowList "Prev Raise-and-FlipFocus"

and now it's peachy. If I missed an easier way of doing this, please let
me know.
