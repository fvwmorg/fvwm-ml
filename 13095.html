Here is an example from my .fvwmrc

Style "*" Button 1, Button 2, Button 4, Button 6, GrabFocusOff
.
.
Style XTerm* EWMHMiniIconOverride, ..., GrabFocus

In the first example, none of the windows will grab (or steal) focus..
In the second example, only the terminal window is allowed to do so.
Hope thats what you were looking for.
