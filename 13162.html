On 23 Aug 2004 16:14:27 +0800, Ding Lei wrote:
>
> 1. I wonder how to do the same as in MS Windows Win-D to show
> desktop. And press the combo again will restore to the previous
> state.

If you install fvwm-themes 0.7.0, it has such feature in the panel of the
RedmondXP theme. It plays with State flag to be able to restore the
original state of windows.

> 2. I found that lots of Window titles in FVWM'S WindowList is different
> from KDE/GNOME's taskbar. Those titles in FVWM's taskbar are usually
> not that friendly, or uneasy to recognize. any way to fix that?

Please show examples of such difference. There are at least 4 names that
may be used to identify the window, name (title name), icon name, class
and resource. Apply FvwmIdent or xprop on your window to see its names.

You can pass UseIconName argument to WindowList to show icon names
instead of title names, but in most of applications these are the same.
In some modules, like FvwmIconMan, FvwmPager and FvwmWindowMenu, you may
configure any combination of the 4 window names, see their man pages and
"format" options.
