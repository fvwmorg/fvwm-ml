If the icon label is wider than the icon, FVWM truncates it whenever
the icon does not have the focus. This is fine, but I'd like to
have it show the rightmost characters of the title instead of the
leftmost. (Alternatively, it could make the title two lines high
and wrap the text.) Is something of the sort possible? I couldn't
find anything in the manpage or FAQ.

I don't want to just use a shorter icon title, since I want to be
able to see the full title by moving the mouse over the icon.
