I'd like to propose a name for FVWM - "Fresh Vision Window Manager." I think
this name is appropriate for the following reasons:

1) The developers of FVWM certainly have a new approach to window managers. I
   like the "Do it your way" attitude they have.

2) Since each individual who uses FVWM configures it the way they want, it could
   be said that each has a "Fresh Vision" of how a window manager should be set
   up.
