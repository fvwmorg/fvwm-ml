Hm. My opinion is that there are some things that ctwm does better,
and that there are some things that fvwm does better. I would

That's just a simple typo in the .fvwm2rc provided: Search for the
string "Restart fvwm" and replace it with "Restart fvwm2". This is
the one spot where the renaming didn't seem to work...

  Mike> What I'm asking therefore is whether I should persevere with
  Mike> the fvwm2 series or stick to 1 until it is stable.
