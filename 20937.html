Curious...
I'm using fvwm 2.0.46 on HPUX 9. Before I upgraded to this version,
using 2.0.45, when I minimized a window, the icon would set to the bottom
left of my screen.

Here's the icon region of my .fvwm2rc:
############################################################################
# ICON Setup

Style "*" IconBox +0-64-1-1

Style "*" StickyIcon

############################################################################

Now, however, with version 2.0.46, the icon places at the top right of
where the window existed; that is, until I do a "Restart fvwm", then it
places it at the bottom left.
I would like it to always place at the bottom left.

Is there a style definition that I am overlooking? Anyone have a quick and
easy solution to my little problem?
