Jan- Hendrik Palic <palic_at_billgotchy.de> writes:
> Hi to all...
>
> I turned over from SuSE to debian. Under SuSE, the fvwm2 had a console,
> which was titles "xmessages", and I want this in my selfcompiled fvwm2 under
> debian, too.
>
> How can I realize that?

If you are trying to get fvwm2 to launch "xmessages", you might try
adding:

AddToFunc InitFunction Exec xmessages

to your $HOME/.fvwm2rc

sorry, but I'm not familiar with Debian, SuSE, or xmessages.
Hope this is what you are looking for.
