On Thu, Nov 08, 2001 at 10:25:51AM -0800, J C Lawrence wrote:
>
> I recently upgraded to 2.4.x and one of the changes is driving me
> nuts:
>
> (Almost) every time an application pops a transient window it
> moves to the back of the current z-order, sometimes taking the
> transient with it (eg xless), or sometimes leaving the transient
> at the top of the z-order and putting the parent at the bottom.
>
> Even more annoying is that when the transient closes it doesn't
> always bring the parent back to its original Z-order placement.
>
> So far I've been unable to see what might be in my RC that's causing
> this, or something in the man page to turn it off.

You should try if

  Style * DontStackTransientParent, DontRaiseTransient, DontLowerTransient

works better for you.
