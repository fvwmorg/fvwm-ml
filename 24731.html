On Wed, Sep 01, 1999 at 06:28:42PM -0400, Pablo Osinaga wrote:
> Hello.
> I am using fvwm2 and I do have the usual mouse binding to resize windows
> Now, I need to have a window Style that let me have some windows with border
> but without title and that this window can not be resized.
>
> It should be somethilng like that:
>
> Style "name" NoTitle, NoHandle, BorderWidth 5
                        ^^^^^^^^

It's 'NoHandles'. Note the 's' at the end. However, disabling the
handles only switches off the cursors over the border. You can still
resize the window.
