>
> I wanna use FvwmButtons to popup root menu this way:
> When I push the button, pop up menu.
> And the button stay pushed until the menu pop down.
>
> Here is my config:
> *FvwmButtons: (2x1 Frame 1 Padding 2 0 Title(side) "Start " ,Left, Icon \
> linux.png, Action(Mouse 1) 'Menu RootMenu Rectangle +$left+$top 0 -100m')
>
> Any ideas?
>
> BTW: I use fvwm-snap-20040516.

Please, add commas, make the life of everyone (including yourself) easier.

  *FvwmButtons: (2x1, Frame 1, Padding 2 0, Title(side) "Start ", ...)

Searching for "popup" in FvwmButtons man page returns the answer.

In the example above, you may just replace "Menu" with "Popup" and
it will work as in fvwm itself, or you may add ", ActionOnPress" to
get an alternative behaviour.
