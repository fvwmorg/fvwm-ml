> > Would it be possible to do this, without affecting the behavior of

>
> addtofunc XtermCreateGrow
> + i exec exec xterm
> + i wait xterm
> + i next (xterm) maximize grow grow
>
> addtofunc Create4XTerms
> + I XtermCreateGrow
> + I XtermCreateGrow
