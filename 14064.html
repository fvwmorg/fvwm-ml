"Fletcher Mattox" <fletcher_at_cs.utexas.edu> writes:
> I want to be able to click any part of a window and have it RaiseLower
> the window (using focus-follows mouse mode). So my first, naive attempt
> was the obvious:
>
> Mouse 1 W A RaiseLower
>
> But I quickly learned that this breaks cut-n-paste by usurping
> the left button. Similar for the middle button. The right button
> is a little better, but I find that it still usurps an application's
> use of the right button (e.g. firefox).
>
> Am I asking fvwm to do something impossible?

Not at all.
You have to be using one of the later 2.5.x releases,
(I'm not sure when this was implemented).
Look in the man page for the substring 'clickraise'.
