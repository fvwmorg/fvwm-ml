On Sun, 16 Mar 1997, Jim Nicholson wrote:

> Sorry for a slightly off-topic post. I'd like to map the dead keys on
> my new "natural" keyboard to fvwm actions. I understand the "key"
> directive, and I have xkeycaps to help, but I can't seem to come up
> with a xmodmap that will use the keys w/o some modifier (Alt, shift,
> etc.) I remember a post about this, and I've been searching the web'd
> archives for a while, but I can't seem to find this. Can someone help
> out?

Well, I know that under X (on my machine), the key with the arrow
pointing at a menu is called "Menu", and the Windows Icon keys are called
"Meta-R" and "Meta-L" depending on Left or Right... I have "Menu" bound to
bring up a list of window-ops if pressed within a window... And Although
this prolly isn't too great of an idea, I have Meta-L bound to a
RaiseLower if pressed within a window.. Hope this helps...
