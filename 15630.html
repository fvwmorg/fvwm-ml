>>>>> "Ted" == Ted Serreyn <tserreyn_at_uucom.com> writes:

    Ted> I have a microsoft Natural keyboad, and would like to bind
    Ted> the window keys under X11. Is this possible? I have the
    Ted> following information from xev. Any ideas? I would like to
    Ted> at least bind the windows keys to the different menus for
    Ted> fvwm. Note that neither of these keys is defined in
    Ted> /usr/include/X11/xkeysyms.def anyone know how to define new
    Ted> keys?

Use xmodmap to rebind the keycodes for those special keys to keysyms
that exist. Then in your .fvwmrc, provide bindings to modules,
functions, or actions for those keysyms.
