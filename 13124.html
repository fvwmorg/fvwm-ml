Hey there!
I've got fvwm 2.5.10, and starting the pager with FvwmConsole doesn't
make it better. Same problem.

find -name '*' | xargs grep -i pager in fvwm-dir: (Maybe this could
help...)

./fvwm2rc:read "pager.fvwm2rc"
./fvwm2rc:+ I Module FvwmPager * 3
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: Geometry 71x53+0-0
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: Font none
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: Balloons
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: BalloonColorset * 9
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: HilightColorset * 9
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: WindowColorsets * 10
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: Window3DBorders
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: UseSkipList
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: BalloonFont
"xft:Sans:medium:pixelsize=10:minspace=True"
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: NoSeparators
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: MiniIcons
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: ShapeLabels
./pager.fvwm2rc:*FvwmPager: Colorset * 10
./WindowStyle.fvwm2rc:Style FvwmPager NoTitle, NoButton 1, NoButton 2,
NoButton 4, BorderWidth 1, Sticky, WindowListSkip, NeverFocus,
CirculateSkip
