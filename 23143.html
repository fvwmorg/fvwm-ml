On Fri, Dec 11, 1998 at 04:01:45AM -0700, dhaley_at_infobeat.com wrote:
> Hello,
>
> I am using the fvwm2 that came with RH5.1.
> How do I turn on mouse autofocus so that when my
> mouse is over a window, I can type in it?
>
> Someone told me to uncomment this line in my $HOME/.fvwm2 file:
> Style "*" MouseFocus
>
> I did this and hit start>Exit FVWM>Restart on the menubar, but nothing
> has changed. Do I need to exit X for FVWM to recognize changes?
> Let me know if there are other files I need to change or load new stuff
> into the xrdb database.

No, restarting is more than enough. Basically the line above is
ok (although you might prefer SloppyFocus instead of MouseFocus).
Perhaps you have another line setting the focus style? I'd
search through the .fvwm2rc and see if something overrides you
setting (watch out for any line containing 'focus'). Please
have a look at the manpage too (Style command).
