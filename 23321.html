> Perhaps somebody can explain what I am doing wrong.. When I use the attached

> sr/local/libexec/fvwm/2.1.5/'

I'd bet the build/installation failed somewhere. Try rebuilding
fvwm in a clean directory. Watch closely what configure says.

to specify this by hand. After installing make sure the right
executables are first in your PATH.
