On Mon, Sep 04, 2000 at 04:03:52PM +0200, Wolfgang Fritzsche wrote:
> Hi,
>
> after a short time of inactivity (5min) my screen gets blank.
> I searched the .fvwm2rc file but didnt find anything that looks like a
> screensaver.
> is it the reason of using xdm as login ?

No, this is a setting of the X server. You can deactivate it
with this command:

  xset s off

You can put this in your .xinitrc file, or if you like in
your .fvwm2rc:

  AddToFunc InitFunction
  + I Exec xset s off
