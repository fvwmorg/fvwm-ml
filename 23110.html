On Mon, Dec 07, 1998 at 11:31:43AM -0330, nzanella_at_cs.mun.ca wrote:
> Toggle command1 command2
> Executes command1 when invoked, then command2 when invoked the next time,
> then command1 when invked the next time and so on...
> Commands containing spaces must be quoted.
>
> Examples:
>
> AddToMenu Modules "FvwmButtons" Toggle "Module FvwmButtons" "KillModule FvwmButtons"
> + "FvwmPager" Toggle "Module FvwmPager" "KillModule FvwmPager"
> etc...

I need that myself quite often. What I do is (on Linux):

  AddToMenu Modules "FvwmPager" Exec "killall FvwmPager || FvwmCommand FvwmPager"

That's not nice and requires a special line for each of my applications,
but it works (as long as you have killall or sth. equivalent that returns
if processes have been killed). Actually I don't use it in menus but
in FvwmButtons. So I can pop FvwmCommand, netscape or a console window
up and down with a single button.
