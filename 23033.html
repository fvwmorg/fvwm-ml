Quoting AlanK_at_aol.com (AlanK_at_aol.com):
> I have installed a fresh copy of FVWM2. It doesn't recognize some of the
> commands in my .fvwm2rc file. The unrecognized commands are:
>
> AddTitleStyle
> BorderStyle
> MiniIcon in the Style command
>
> Any idea what is wrong?

Was it a binary package of fvwm2 you installed? fvwm2 has the option to omit
many features at compile time, to save space. I have heard that the stock
RedHat fvwm2 has had feature trimming that could cause these errors. I am
afraid your only recourse is either to install a full-featured fvwm2 or stop
using those features.
