On Wed, 28 May 1997 eudes_at_gat.univ-lille1.fr wrote:

-> Hi all, I've been reading this list for a while now, and it's time
->to send you my first question :)
->- Does any of you knows about a way-cool buttons-like module WITH
->kind of submenus ? I mean when you click on a button called, say Games,
->then some buttons, say xtetris, xlander, and xvier, just appear over
->the Games button; and they disappear if I click on the Games button
->again. Er... popup mechanism might be better than 'submenus'... :)
->I know Xf-Panel but that's not exactly what I'm looking for.
->I do not like TkGoodStuff too much for some reasons even if I reckon
->that this is a good one ;)
->WHAT I WANT is a FvwmButtons just a little bit improved !! Thanks

One of my buttons is defined with the following action

Action `Popup Rxvts`

The pop-up defined in my .fvwmrc is as follows

AddToMenu Rxvts "Rxvts"*rxvt_but.xpm* Title
+ "&QTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/qdigi.xpm
-scale T -title "$HOST : QuakeTerm" -n QTerm -geometry 80x45 &
+ "&AlienTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/alien01.xpm
-scale T -title "$HOST : AlienTerm" -n AlienTerm -geometry 80x25 &
+ "D&MTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/dmlogo.xpm
-scale T -title "$HOST : DMTerm" -n DM -geometry 80x32 &
+ "&DeadTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/dead01.xpm
-scale T -title "$HOST : DeadTerm" -n Deadlock -geometry 80x32 &
+ "&TestTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/tcf.xpm -scale
T -title "$HOST : TestTerm" -n TestTerm -geometry 80x32 &
+ "&EliteTerm"%x_mico.xpm% Exec
/home/imc/bin/rxvt -pixmap /home/imc/nwrm/pics/pixmaps/Elite.xpm
-scale T -title "$HOST : EliteTerm" -n EliteTerm -geometry 80x32 &

(though obviously this has wrapped around a bit in the mail)

So when I click on the rxvt button a menu pops up. When I select an
rxvt or I click elsewhere it goes away again.

If you get rid of the text you can just have a menu full of pixmaps
which will look a lot like a button bar.
