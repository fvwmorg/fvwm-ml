Hi everybody,

I've solved it:

> On 4 Apr 1997, Kai Grossjohann wrote:
>
> > >>>>> Thomas Leitner writes:
> >
> > Thomas> No. This does not work either. Apparently the Purify window
> > Thomas> explicitly turns off its decorations or something.
> >
> > Thomas> Any more ideas anyone?
> >
> > There is stuff about MWM specific settings in Fvwm. Try those?

This was the problem. I had the following setting:

Style "*" MWMDecor

because I thought it would make Fvwm more compatible to MWM which does
not seem to be the case. After commenting it out, Purify comes up with a
standard window decoration as well.
