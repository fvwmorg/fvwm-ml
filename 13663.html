Hi,

tried to create my first script using FvwmScript today and ran into a
problem with receiving signals/messages. I followed the examples on the
FvwmScript manpage and it just didn't work. After having a close look at
the examples shipped with fvwm I realized that there's a difference
between the manpage and the examples.
On the Fvwm manpage: Case Message of
In the examples: Case message of
Notice that the 'm' of message is lower case in the second line. With
the uppercase 'M' the script fails.
I'm using fvwm 2.5.12.
