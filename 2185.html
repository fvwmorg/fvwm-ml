You can assign Mozilla and all its windows to, for instance, a specific
desktop.

  Style "Mozilla-bin" StartsOnDesk 4

You can also specify a specific page, or a specific desktop and page.

As for having the pager "look like multiple desktops", I'm not sure what
you mean here. The pager draws little delimiters between desktops, which
I think you can specify as being solid instead of dashed. No "page" titles,
of course. And you can specify different backgrounds for each page these
days...
