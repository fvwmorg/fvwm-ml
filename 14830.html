Hm. Since I've never used this function (I only use 1 desktop), I
just tried it out. I entered WindowsDesk 1 into FvwmTalk, and the
cursor changed to prompt me to select a window, which I did, and it
moved to desk 1.

Andy> I tried to bind it to Shift-Meta-MouseButton1 like this:
Andy> Mouse 1 WTSFI SM WindowsDesk 1
Andy> and like this:
Andy> Mouse 1 WTSFI SM WindowsDesk Desk_1
Andy> but neither seem to work.

I tried binding it like so:

Mouse 3 A C WindowsDesk 1

And that worked fine for me. I don't see any problems with your
lines, but perhaps your Mouse definition above conflicts with another
one you have set up? Also, the 2nd wouldn't work (Desks are numbers).
