>
> First, I have not benn sucessful in getting FVWM buttons to swallow a
> digital clock (month/day/date - red on black background). Second, I

I used to use this, before I switched to asclock (available from
http://rif3.iiic.ethz.ch:1313/spiff/asclock/):

*FvwmButtons(5x1, Frame 0, Swallow "xclock" "Exec xclock -d -update 1
-bg Black -fg LimeGreen -padding 4
-fn '-adobe-helvetica-medium-r-*-*-12-*-*-*-*-*-*-*' &")

Note: I've inserted carriage returns for readability ... remove them before
use.

> haven't been able to get buttons to swallow a pager (any
> configuration). These pager function has been included in several
> example .fvwm2rc's I have found - unfortunately none of these work on my
> machine.

How about something like this:

*FvwmButtons(3x2, Frame -2, Back #908090, Swallow "FvwmPager" "FvwmPager 0 0")

> One last problem. I keep getting messages to the effect that the
> colormap is full. I've tried using the command for colormap focus
> following the mouse and the focus - no positive results. I'm running a

Something's eating all your colours. Netscape is a frequent offender.
Try 'netscape -install'. It could also be lots of multicoloured icons
being used by fvwm2.

> cheap 2 meg trident tgui card at 1024 x 768. When I try to remove the 8
> bit depth entry from XF86Config - X complains that the default depth
> entry is missing and refuses to engage.

I don't know about the capabilities of your card. You can try replacing
the 8 with 16 (or even 24). You may need to reduce the resolution to
get it to work. Try to find out what resolutions and depths are
supported by your card.
