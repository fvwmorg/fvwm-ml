You could try something like:

Style * HandleWidth 3
Style * ForeColor White, BackColor #22aa99
Style * HilightFore White, HilightBack #22aa99
Style * Font -adobe-helvetica-bold-r-*-*-14-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
TitleStyle TiledPixmap pattern.xpm -- Flat
TitleStyle LeftJustified Height 20
ButtonStyle 1 Pixmap button.xpm -- Top Left Flat
ButtonStyle 2 Pixmap button2.xpm -- Top Right Flat
BorderStyle -- HiddenHandles NoInset Flat
Mouse 0 1 Nop
Mouse 0 2 Nop

the only problem is, I dont think its possible to make the
tiledpixmap avoid the title text.

good luck,
