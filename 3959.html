On Mon, 7 Jan 2002, Mikhael Goikhman wrote:

> Well, if you read the man page (you did, right?), you should find that
> StartFunction is executed on both start-up and restart.

Yeah, I did...

> The modules are part of FVWM and they are killed on quit or restart.

Oh. That explains why the modules weren't started twice. That confused
me, as did the StartFunction being executed both at start-up and
restart. It kinda makes RestartFunction seem a bit redundant if that's
the case. The man pages don't really give much in the way of examples and
typical usage, and the sample system.fvwm2rc doesn't even have a
StartFunction section at all.
I learn best by example.

> So, if you don't want Eterm to be launched twice, don't request this.
>
> AddToFunc StartFunction
> + I Module FvwmButtons
> + I Module FvwmTaskBar
>
> AddToFunc InitFunction
> + I Exec Esetroot -f /usr/share/pixmaps/vpm_wp_01_1024.jpg
> + I Exec Eterm -g 26x21+0+552 -F 5x7 -O -T XMessages -e tail -f $HOME/.X.err

This worked.
Thanks.
