On Fri, Feb 21, 2003 at 04:57:29PM +0100, Thomas Glanzmann wrote:
> Hi FVWMers!
> I have a problem with mplayer. Always I tell mplayer to go fullscreen it changes
> his layer from 4 to 10 ... I don't like this. Is there a way to tell FVWM to
> forbid mplayer to change its layer?
>
> I tried the following which doesn't work.
>
> Style "MPlayer" BorderWidth 0, HandleWidth 0, Layer 4


Try

  Style MPlayer GnomeIgnoreHints, EWMHIgnoreStackingOrderHints

One of those two styles should work. You need 2.5.x for the
EWMH... style.
