Hi!

I think Ive tackled the problem down to the UseDecor Style. Consider this
fvwm2rc:

DestroyDecor defaultdec
AddToDecor defaultdec
+ ButtonStyle Reset

#Style * UseDecor defaultdec

Style * DecorateTransient, MWMButtons, MWMFunctions

ButtonStyle 1 - Clear MwmDecorMenu
ButtonStyle 2 - Clear
ButtonStyle 4 - Clear MwmDecorMax
ButtonStyle 6 - Clear MwmDecorMin

Mouse 0 1 A Menu MenuFvwmRoot
Mouse 0 2 A Close
Mouse 0 4 A Maximize
Mouse 0 6 A Iconify


If you comment the line with UseDecor out youll get the strange button
behaviour. Or at least _I_ get it on my system ;)
