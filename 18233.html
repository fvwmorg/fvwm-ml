A new patch which lets one alter their borderstyle is at:

http://www.rpi.edu/~veliaa/projects/fvwm_patches/fvwm_border_picture.html

If you want textured borders, this should be for you. Although a
separate patch from the title-bar patch , it *requires* the title-bar
patch (version 6 or greater). See the new manpage entry for
Borderstyle. A quick example of how it works is:

Borderstyle Active (Pixmap texture.xpm)

Only Pixmap is available now, but one can also specify Simple to
remove the decoration. The HiddenHandles style which was present in
the experimental title-bar patch has been removed (pending
reimplementation).

Enjoy,
