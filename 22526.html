        Now, if a little advertising is allowed ...
        (not very off-topic, I like and use fvwm2 ;)

        Check http://nether.tky.hut.fi/iconstore/

        I've collected 48x48 icons from the net, and you
        can preview them, pick them one at a time or a
        pack at a time. Copyrights belong to the respective
        authors, but as far as I know, all the icons there
        are at least freely distributable.

        Format is jpg, tif or xpm, and when converting, use
        something like GIMP or ImageMagick, XV leaves the
        transparency information out of the saved xpm:s.
        If you use that you must edit the background color
        to transparency color by hand (which isn't terribly
        hard if you take a look in a xpm where transparency
        info exists).

        All the best,

        Tomi
