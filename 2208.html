On Sun, Mar 04, 2001 at 08:53:58PM -0500, Rick Hatton wrote:
> How can I change the inactive window colors, and can I have different colors
> for different apps>
>
see
man fvwm2
read up on all the style commands "/STYLE".

in my .fvwm2rc (fvwm2.3.30) i have:
Style * HilightFore White, HilightBack grey55,\
                ForeColor grey80, BackColor Black

Style rxvt HilightFore Ivory, HilightBack grey70,\
                ForeColor Black, BackColor grey50

etc...
you might also want to read up on decors
