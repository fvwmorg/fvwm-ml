Just noticed my menus weren't acting quite right after killing the
font out of Menustyle (it looked funny)

I use the mwm style of menus and after removing the font specification,
they reverted to fvwm style.

Maybe it should be changed so that a missing specification (ie: the font)
doesn't screw up the rest of the line.

This works:

This doesn't:

MenuStyle black SlateBlue1 RoyalBlue4 mwm


I don't see why both can't work =)
