I am running fvwm 2.0.40 and 2.0.41 under Solaris 2.3. I can specify "-0"
when exec'ing a program, as Robert showed above, and the window is
positioned just fine. But, I know of 1 little "geometry" problem with the
IconBox style:

  Style "*" IconBox 5 -80 -140 -1

positions the icon box along the bottom of the screen (bottom edge of box
is 1 pixel above bottom edge), but

  Style "*" IconBox 5 -80 -140 -0
                               ^^
doesn't. Icons show up just anywhere on the screen. So, in this
instance, fvwm does not like a '-0'. I thought I had sent this to Chuck
Hines a while back, but I must have spaced it out since it's not
anywhere in my mail log. Sorry.

Is it something like this that you're seeing?
