Ok, after some tricky search I finally found what you have:

  http://www.suse.de/de/private/products/suse_linux/i386/packages_professional/index_all.html

They ship very old fvwm-themes-0.4.2, but it may work ok with fvwm-2.4.8
probably, I don't know.

You may install fvwm-themes-0.6.1 from sources, it works well with 2.4.8.
2.4.8 has both executable names fvwm and fvwm2, so don't worry about this.

Now, to your problem. Do you start fvwm-themes-start instead of startx or
xinit? See /usr/share/doc/packages/fvwm-themes/README .
