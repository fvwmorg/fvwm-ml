On Tue, 15 Feb 2000, Michael Han wrote:

> Previously from Kendrick Vargas
> >This works fine on the first page (the left most). If I switch to another
> >page, x2vnc stops responding unless I move a window on the new page. Then,
> >I can flip around between the non-first pages without issue. If I switch
> >back into the first page OR if I manually click a window's decorations OR
> >if I bring another window into the foreground, x2vnc once again stops
> >responding.
>
> It looks like the PanFrames are getting the stacking order out of
> whack. I don't remember how (or if you can) fiddle with the PanFrames
> anymore, but I know some work was done on this a while back. Maybe
> there's a runtime option now pertaining to the PanFrames? I assume
> you've setup EdgeResistance or the other EdgeMumble option are set not
> to change pages at the screen edge. Basically, try to turn the
> PanFrames off if you don't use the mouse to pan the display across the
> virtual screen.

Thanx much!

I never wanted the Edge scrolling stuff to work in my configuration (I
found it annoying) so I set the EdgeResistance to some ridiculously high
number. Your post got me to looking at the Edge* stuff in the man page and
I noticed the EdgeThickness option. I set EdgeThinkness to 0 and all is
now good!

Thanx :-)
                        -peace
