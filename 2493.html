%% "Ripalda-Marin, Miguel-Angel" <maripalda_at_elasa.siemens.es> writes:

  rm> how can I set the color of my root window? I don't want images on my
  rm> background, just one plain RGB color...

This isn't a window manager question (the window manager doesn't control
what goes on your root window), but...

You can use "xsetroot -solid Blue" or whatever color you want.

Put it into your ~/.xinitrc file (if you're running startx or similar),
or your ~/.Xsession (or sometimes ~/.xsession) of you're using XDM or
similar.
