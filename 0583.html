On Sun, Apr 02, 2000 at 08:42:57AM -0500, Douglas Straub wrote:
> Hi,
> I can't seem to get rid of the bevels that go around the buttons on
> window decorations. I am using
> pixmaps for the buttons, and the buttons are rectangular(not square).
> Is this what -- flat is supposed to do?

Yes. The order in which you add the individual ButtonStyles may
have an effect if it works or not. Try the ButtonStyle commands
in sample.fvwmrc/system.fvwm2rc-sample-95 as a starting point:

  TitleStyle ActiveUp (solid DarkBlue -- Flat)
  TitleStyle ActiveDown (solid DarkBlue -- Flat)
  TitleStyle Inactive (solid grey51 -- Flat)
  ButtonStyle all -- UseTitleStyle Flat
  AddButtonStyle 2 Pixmap mini.winXX-close.xpm
  ...
