Yaron wrote:
-> I got fvwm2.0.46 running on an SGI Indy, which has a 24-bit display. Thing
-> is, it gives a Cannot Allocate Colormap Entry error quite a bit, xterms
-> and gradients get colourless, etc.
-> isn't using the right one. Does anyone know how to tell it it's supposed
-> to use TrueColor?

Change /var/X11/xdm/Xservers to
:0 secure /usr/bin/X11/X -bs -nobitscale -c -class TrueColor -depth 24
and then restart X (either reboot, or hit Shift + Control + F12 + Keypad '/').
Works fine for me on IRIX 5.3, 6.2, and 6.3...
