On Tue, 26 Nov 1996, Carl Payne wrote:

> I have two questions. First, Is there a way that I can make a button(on the
> new fvwm2rc95-fvwm95-2.0.41) that goes directly to a telnet location? By this
> I mean: I want to click on a button that takes me automatically takes me to a
> location, let's say info.rutgers.edu. or 150.333.3435.554 whatever. Is this
> possible?

I've not tried it with telnet, but it should work in of these two ways:
Either you let the button start up an xterm which automatically runs
telnet:

*FvwmButtons(Title "Info", Action 'Exec xterm -e telnet info.rutgers.edu
&' )

Or you use an X-based telnet like xtelnet:

*FvwmButtons( Title "Info", Action 'Exec xtelnet info.rutgers.edu &')


> My second question is how do I get the Autoraise function to NOT be
> executed at startup. I hate having to turn it off each time I enter. Thank
> you for your time.

Just remove or comment the call to the Autoraise module in your
.fvwm2rc95 file (the module is called FvwmAuto).
