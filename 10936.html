On 04 Jan 2004 09:56:04 +0530, Kingsly John wrote:
>
> I have the following style for gkrellm ...
>
> Style "gkrellm" Sticky, WindowListSkip, StaysOnTop, CirculateSkip, BorderWidth 0, NoTitle, NoHandles, ClickToFocus
>
> Under 2.5.7 both mplayer and tvtime went to fullscreen perfectly okay.(ie.
> fullscreen and on top of all other windows)
>
> Under 2.5.8 the gkrellm window ends up on top of tvtime even when it's on
> fullscreen. But mplayer works perfectly as before.
>
> I've not made any changes to the config.. and the only layer related note in
> the changelog is about the !Layer condition.
>
> What could be the issue and how do I get tvtime to go fullscreen ?

I can't reproduce your problem.

When I run "mplayer" and press "f", it puts itself in the fullscreen mode
and into layer 10. This is higher that the StaysOnTop layer of gkrellm,
that is 6 by default unless you redefine DefaultLayers.

The man page of mplayer contains some information about the layer used
in fullscreen mode, you may want to read the mplayer man page.

In any case, issue FvwmIdent on both gkrellm and fullscreen-ed mplayer
and report their layer. (I hope you may bind FvwmIdent to some key.)
