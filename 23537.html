%% Sam Krishnabhat <samk_at_cisco.com> writes:

  sk> I tried with the following, but still with no luck.

  sk> ./configure --with-xpm-library=/usr/openwin/lib
  sk> --with-xpm-includes=/usr/openwin/include/X11
                                              ^^^^

This is incorrect; you just want /usr/openwin/include. All X headers
are always accessed as "#include <X11/xpm.h>", so you don't want the X11
on the pathname.
