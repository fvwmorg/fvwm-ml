<alaric_at_caerllewys.net> wrote:
> - A button can only have a single vector chain. This isn't
> necessarily a problem, because vector segments can be made

I have several chains per button in my configuration, one draws the emblem,
another draws a shadow for it and another draws a border.

for example:

AddButtonStyle 8 Active Vector 9 40x40_at_2 45x35_at_2 55x35_at_2 60x40_at_2 60x45_at_2
50x50_at_2 50x55_at_2 50x65_at_4 50x65_at_2 -- Flat
AddButtonStyle 8 InActive Vector 9 40x40_at_3 45x35_at_3 55x35_at_3 60x40_at_3 60x45_at_3
50x50_at_3 50x55_at_3 50x65_at_4 50x65_at_3 -- Flat
AddButtonStyle 8 AllUp Vector 7 40x45_at_1 45x40_at_1 55x40_at_1 60x50_at_4 50x60_at_1
50x75_at_4 50x75_at_1 -- Flat
AddButtonStyle All Active Vector 9 80x60_at_4 80x40_at_2 60x20_at_2 40x20_at_2 20x40_at_2
20x60_at_2 40x80_at_2 60x80_at_2 80x60_at_2 -- Flat
AddButtonStyle All Inactive Vector 9 80x60_at_4 80x40_at_2 60x20_at_2 40x20_at_2
20x40_at_2 20x60_at_2 40x80_at_2 60x80_at_2 80x60_at_2 -- Flat

(This is supposed to be a Question mark :)

my fvwm2rc is here if you would like to see it in context,
http://dev.gentoo.org/~taviso/fvwm2rc.html
