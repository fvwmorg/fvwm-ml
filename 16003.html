>>>>> "Mirai" == Mirai <mirai_at_mirai.com> writes:

    Mirai> how do I get a screendump of the screen WHILE the root
    Mirai> window menu is popped up? I'd like to show off the menus.

In an xterm, do

        sleep 10; xwd -root

then you have 10 seconds to popup your menu. Keep it open 'til you
hear the beeps.

    Mirai> Also, anyone have a patch or is working on something that
    Mirai> can embedd xpm's into the title bar,

That's the one thing ctwm still has over fvwm: pixmaps for the title
bar buttons ... pixmaps are also heck a of a lot easier to edit than
``45x45_at_1 55x45_at_1 55x55_at_0 ...''
