>>>>> "Mark" == Mark Borges <mdb_at_cdc.noaa.gov> writes:

Mark> In fvwm-2.0.41, this style line:
Mark> Style "xbuffy" NoTitle,NoHandles,Sticky,WindowListSkip,RandomPlacement

Mark> and this resource:

Mark> *FvwmButtons(2x2) - NULL Swallow "xbuffy" Exec xbuffy -priority 16

Mark> would make the transient pop-up windows (i.e., those created by xbuffy
Mark> when a mailbox button is pushed) come up undecorated. This fails in
Mark> fvwm-2.0.42, i.e., they come up with a titlebar.

Mark> Anyone know what could have changed? How can I get the old
Mark> (undecorated transient pop-ups) behavior back?

Do you have 'Style "*" DecorateTransients' in your .fvwm2rc by any
chance? I just checked to make sure I wasn't putting that in as a
default, and I'm not, so I'm not sure what could have caused this
change in behavior.

You could try adding 'Style "xbuffy" NakedTransients' and see if it
helps.
