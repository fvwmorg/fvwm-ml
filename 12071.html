It has nothing to do with the WMs. .xinitrc and .xsession are usually sh
scripts. Think about what exec does in sh. Check the sh man page if
you don't know.

This script will only ever say "hello":

--->
#!/bin/sh
exec echo "hello"
exec echo "goodbye"
<---
