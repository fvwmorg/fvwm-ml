One other fvwm 2.0.41 compile-time problem I've noticed... if Fvwm.tmpl
says:
  #define FvwmName fvwm2
then the resulting "make install" command line for fvwm will look like:
  bsdinst -c fvwm2 /usr/local/bin fvwm2
Getting rid of the additional spaces in the #define statements in Fvwm.tmpl
seems to fix this problem.

I'm not an imake guru, and I'm running a beta-test OS, so... is this
a bug in my imake, or is it incorrect to put those additional spaces
in the "#define" line in a template file? Thanks -
