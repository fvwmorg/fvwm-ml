It is, however, sufficiently close to X11 for the purposes of this
discussion. And the answer is Yes, fvwm does run under Sun's

just need to add the appropriate line to your .xinitrc file .


Note, however that some of the UI extensions in openwindows don't
trasnlate very well to fvwm. In particular the pinable windows. But
that is a small price to pay for a REAL window manager.
