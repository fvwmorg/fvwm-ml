On Fri, Jul 31, 1998 at 02:56:02PM -0400, Dan Espen wrote:

> Vikas Agnihotri <VikasA_at_cagw1.att.com> writes:
> > I have 'FvwmPager 0 0' all by itself, not part of FvwmButtons in the lower
> > right corner of my screen.

> > Why am I unable to move the pager by click-dragging on the title? Something
> > special I need to do?

> Style "Fvwm*" NoTitle,Sticky,WindowListSkip

> which would suppress the titlebar on the pager.
> The pager has its own "title" which is not a titlebar.

> You should be able to tell the difference.

(grin) Obviously not. I was mistaking the pager title for the title bar. I
gave Style FvwmPager Title and indeed got a title bar using which I was
able to click-drag move the pager.

> If you don't have a titlebar on the pager, you should be able to move it
> a number of different ways. My favorite:

> # Button 2 with Meta (diamond), moves window: dje
> Mouse 2 W M Move

Cool. Works like a charm.
