I've recently switched displays and now I have less screen real estate
(I've gone from 1400x1050 back to 1280x1024 for reasons that don't
bear going into).

roughly like the standard gnome panel. I think I could get the results
I want with FvwmButtons (though I'm open to suggestions if there's a
better module). But it has to auto-hide to be maximally useful.
