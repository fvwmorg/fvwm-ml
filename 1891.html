FvwmEvent is powerful when configured propertly, but it seem your goal is
simplier (you don't want to move your mouse pointer) and may be achieved
without it.

  Key Escape A M Prev Focus
  Key Escape A SM Next Focus

  Style * GrabFocus, GrabFocusTransient # supported only in 2.3

You may add Raise as well (when you switch focus) if you want.
