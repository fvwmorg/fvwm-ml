>>>>> "Joel" == Joel Young <jdyoung_at_erinet.com> writes:

    Joel> When I try to compile fvwm-2.0.43, I get Makefile:683: ***
    Joel> missing separator.

 Missing separator means that a tab got converted to spaces, probably
in the Imakefile.

target: <tab> dependancy
<tab> commandline _at_\\
<tab> commandline _at_\\

not

target: <spaces> dependancy
<spaces> commandline _at_\\
<spaces> commandline _at_\\


 Convert the spaces back to tabs and it should work again.
