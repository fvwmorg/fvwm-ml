> Can someone give me an example of how flipfocus is used? I tried
> binding it to a key, but it seems to be a no-op that way.

Hello. I wrote FlipFocus, and this is my primary use of it:

Key F8 A N Prev [CurrentPage] FlipFocus

When I hit F8, I go to the previous window. When I hit it again, I go
back to the first window. This is the easiest way to switch back and
forth between 2 windows.
