Hi,

I am the maintainer of the Fvwm modules for the Dotfile Generator. I
didn't include the PixmapPath in the Fvwm2 module, because i read
somewhere (I don't remember where...) that it was obsolete, and IconPath
would be searched for pixmaps as well.

I have checked my own setup (2.0.43 - no patches), and pixmap _icons_
cannot be found in the IconPath, but pixmap _buttons_ and _menu_ pixmaps
can!

Of course I could easily generate both IconPath and PixmapPath, but if
PixmapPath is really going to be obsolete, I won't do that.

Does anybody know if PixmapPath will be required in 2.0.44 and later
(Chuck, perhaps;)

BTW: I played around with mini icons, FvwmButtons and the recent genscheme
     package the last couple of days. I just *LOVE* Fvwm-2!
