Hi,

how can I hide / change color of the border of the IconMan when NOTHING
is in it? In other words: As long as I have some windows open or
minimized the IconMan doesn't show a border (since my background is
black, too), but if there is NO window the icon man shows a 2 pixel
border in brown color...

Here is how I configure man IconMan:

Style FvwmIconMan BorderWidth 0, NoTitle, NoHandles NeverFocus,
 FixedPosition, IgnoreRestack, StaysOnBottom

Style FvwmIconMan Color black/black

*FvwmIconMan: title ""


Bye
 Felix
