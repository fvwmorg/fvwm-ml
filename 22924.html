wrote concerning 'FVWM: Title Bar Styles':
> I'm using fvwm2 2.0.45 and I'm having problems getting my titlebars to come
> out nicely rounded in a 3D effect. The snippet I'm using at the moment is:
>
> TitleStyle Centered
> AddTitleStyle ActiveUp VGradient 128 cyan3 white Raised
> AddTitleStyle ActiveDown VGradient 128 cyan3 white Raised
> AddTitleStyle Inactive VGradient 128 #c06077 white Raised
>
> This leaves the titlebars in a flat appearing bar. Anybody got any clues?


I do something like this:

  AddToDecor foo
  ...
  + TitleStyle Inactive (VGradient 32 Cyan Black -- Raised)
  + TitleStyle ActiveUp (VGradient 32 Cyan Black -- Raised)
  + TitleStyle ActiveDown (VGradient 32 Black Cyan -- Flat)
  ...

The decor syntax is somewhat ... strange ... :-)

You can see what it looks like at
