Most likely the window title does not match. There may be
invisible spaces. Use FvwmIdent to find to get the window's
resource and use that instead. If this still does not help,
verify that the function is called at all by adding

  AddToFunc WarpToFind I Beep

FvwmEvent may not be running. Are there any error messages on the
sonsole?
