> Dominik,
>
> That's great news! Can you tell me the details of the fix for version
> 2.2.2 and later? I tried one suggestion you'd made before to no avail.
> I'd like to incorporate the inprovements to make the code more robust.

There were two events missing at the end of the list (before the builtin
events). These events are defined in fvwm/mnodule.h (at least in 2.2).
When you make a typo, FvwmEvent searches past the end of the list since
the lenght of the list is defined in module.h. Just add entries for the
missing two events.
