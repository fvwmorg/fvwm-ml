  Actually it works really well on some applications (and is very useful
as well). I'm thinking of things like text editors where, for example,
you have long lines and you want to make the window JUST big enough
without having to make it too big first and then shrink it back down.
And I don't consider my hardware especially high-end (it is good though)
but OpaqueResize worked quite well on it.

  If added as a Style option (if possible) then that would be really
ideal since I could easily limit it to those applications where it made
sense.
