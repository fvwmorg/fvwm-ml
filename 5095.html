Just upgraded from 2.4.5 to 2.4.7 (Solaris). I have TitleStyle's
set up to display HGradients for ActiveUp, ActiveDown and Inactive
windows (all with -- Flat). The window titles seem to be repainting
themselves more than in 2.4.5, causing them to "flash" whenever
their window gains or loses focus; on a mouse button 1 down; and
ClickTime milliseconds after a mouse button 1 up. The behavior -
especially the latter - is a bit disturbing. Developers - what say
you? Am I seeing things?
