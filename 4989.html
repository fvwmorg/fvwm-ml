On April 29, 2002 at 15:23 +0100, Ripalda-Marin, Miguel-Angel wrote:
> I have included all the suggestions made by Uwe, and now my .fvwm2rc look
> like this:
>
> ImagePath $HOME/.fvwm_icons
>
> AddToFunc StartFunction
> + "I" Module FvwmButtons
> *FvwmButtons:Back black
> *FvwmButtons:BoxSize smart
> *FvwmButtons:Font fixed
> *FvwmButtons:Fore white
> *FvwmButtons:Frame 0
> *FvwmButtons:Geometry +0+0
> *FvwmButtons:Padding 2 2
> *FvwmButtons:Pixmap none
> *FvwmButtons:Rows 1
> *FvwmButtons:(Title "Spanish layout",Icon bomb.xpm,Center,`Exec loadkeys
> es`)
>
> But it still keeps on saying the 'No buttons defined. Quitting'... I have
> browsed the whole man page for FvwmButtons and cannot understand what's
> missing.
>

Are you sure, you are using the right module version and fvwm version?
I have tried the following (even without the Action keyword) and it works
with version 2.4.3. At least the button appears:

DestroyModuleConfig FvwmButtonsTest
*FvwmButtonsTest:Back black
*FvwmButtonsTest:BoxSize smart
*FvwmButtonsTest:Font fixed
*FvwmButtonsTest:Fore white
*FvwmButtonsTest:Frame 0
*FvwmButtonsTest:Geometry +0+0
*FvwmButtonsTest:Padding 2 2
*FvwmButtonsTest:Pixmap none
*FvwmButtonsTest:Rows 1
*FvwmButtonsTest:(Title "Spanish layout",Icon bomb.xpm,Center,`Exec loadkeys es`)
Module FvwmButtons FvwmButtonsTest
  
You can copy the previous lines in FvwmConsole. The button should
appear immediately.
