There's a small buglet in the fvwm man page; ~ line 624:

  Executes \fIcommand\fP. Exec doesn't require an additional 'exec' or
  '&' in the command.

you can't put a "'" in column one in nroff, this causes the rest of the
line to be ignored.

Change to:

  Executes \fIcommand\fP. Exec doesn't require an additional 'exec' or
  \'&' in the command.

and it looks fine...
