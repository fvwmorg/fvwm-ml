Hi Chuck. Thanks so much for putting out the fvwm WEB page. I have
been using fvwm 1.24 for about a year and don't know how I ever got
by without it. About 3 months ago I tried one of the 2.x beta
releases. I eventually decided not to use it because it seemed
to me that:

Iconified stick xterm clients were the same color as non-sticky ones.
I couldn't change this. Is there now a way?

Iconified clients in 1.24 tended to get put on unused parts of the root
window. I made this the upper right corner. In the 2.x version I looked
at, I could iconify something and it would sometimes disappear, under
some other client, and I couldn't easily find it.

Deiconifying clients resulted in their being placed on the bottom of the
stacking order, instead of the top in 2.x. Has this behavior changed?
I would like deiconified clients to appear on the top of the stack.


I confess, I'm not even close to an fvwm expert. I did read some
directions, and try to get the desired behavior, and failed.
So, either I can't get what I want becuase it is no longer possible
or, I just didn't try enough things.

If you have any brief comments about this, I would really appreciate
it. I'd like to move on to version 2.x, but I really need the
above features first.
