Well, if you think about it, adding a little more customization to the
pixmaps in the title would be a functional decision rather than a "looks"
decision.

Being able to use a different pixmap for the area immediately below the
title would help a designer in using pixmaps for the background. As it is,
I imagine pixmaps for backgrounds on any window are pretty much pointless
unless you've found the magic pixmap that lets your window look good while
not killing the visibility of your title. If you could change the pixmap
for the background area behind the title, it'd aid in making visual
identification of a window easier.

I think an addition like this would be a functional one. Just being able
to change the pixmap immediately behind the title, as well as another two
settings for transitions into the other pixmaps.

Would that be so hard? (I'm being serious, not sarcastic)
