Philipp Frank <Philipp.Frank_at_web.de> writes:
> > Do you see any gkrellm when you press Alt-Tab or when you press
> > Alt-Shift-Tab with the following binding:
> >=20
> > Key Tab A SM WindowList Root c c OnlyListSkip, NoDeskSort, SelectOnRele=
> ase
> >=20
> > Choose gkrellm in WindowList and it will be moved to the current page
> > even if it has large coordinates.
>
> Heck, you're right :) I can choose both instances from the WindowList
> and get them back...
>
> So, what's the problem here? I don't have the "Remember Position"
> Option enabled in gkrellm, so that's not the problem. I tried quitting
> gkrellm directly, but at the next start the same problem would happen
> (I usually don't quit gkrellm, I just end Fvwm via the Mouse Menu and
> so gkrellm ends as well...). The geometry of the windows is something
> like 0x0+3840+0, so that's where they are, but how can I make them
> stay on the right page?

Try changing the Remember Position and then saving your config.
In my:

~/.gkrellm/user_config

I have a line that reads:

save_position 0

I think that is what you need.
