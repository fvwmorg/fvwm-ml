I think this is an excellent idea. I mean, AutoRaise is not supposed
to have any effect on the functioning of the pager, but having the
pager changing desks as it gets mouse focus on the appropriate spot,

replace MOUSE_CLICK event with GOT_FOCUS (yes, now you know I'm
completely illiterate about X programming) and will add a configurable
wait duration functionality could not be so difficult.

As about pager tooltips, somebody has already published here a series
