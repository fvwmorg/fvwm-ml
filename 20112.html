I use on my FvwmButtons. They all span
30 pixels wide and 30 or less pixels
high. Most of these were snarfed and
sized via XV then hand edited to clean
them up.

I hope this is the right place to send
this. No flames, please, but if there
is a better place to send these, let me
know. Also, if anyone has an ftp site that
has a collection of small pixmaps, I would
appreciate knowing where that is.
