>
> I have 1.x fvwm, and I have problems with setting up colors for
> non-selected windows. I know it should be StdForeColor and StdBackColor,
> but it doesn't work. HiFOreColor and HiBackColor work, and others, but
> whatever I do, Std*Color doesnt change (its blue). I'm wondering if anyone
> can tell me where is it comming from? Some default file I don't know
> about? I have .fvwmrc in my dir, and I got rid of system.fvwmrc file. I
> also have .xinitrc, .Xdefaults and so on. What am I missing? I have
> XF86_S3G server.
> Thanks.
>
> Bruno

Yes. I had the same problem. It turned out to be a "style" definition in the
.fvwmrc that overrode the Std*Color settings. Look for I line like

Style "*" BorderWidth 5, HandleWidth 5, Color Black/#60a0c0,Icon unknown1.xpm
                                        ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
