> In fvwm, I get 5 areas for each button. In fvwm2, I get Desk 0 only.
> Selecting another button removes highlight from Desk 0. Clicking on
> any of the 5 areas for Desk 0 returns to button zero.
>
> In FvwmPager, if I use 0 9 rather than * *, then the pager becomes 9
> wide with 10 desktops always shown.
>
> I have tried all combinations that I can think of to circumvent. Is
> this the expected behavior of fvwm2?

Which fvwm version do you speak about, 2.2.4?

What you are describing happens when you start FvwmPager without
parameters or with 0 as its parameter. If you start it using * (or * *),
it should show the current desk, as you may expect.

When you are at desk 0 open FvwmTalk or FvwmConsole and enter commands:

  FvwmPager * *
  Desk 0 3

If the pager started by the first command does not show desk 3 after the
second command, there is a bug in your fvwm version. Upgrade.

> Can the "Desk 0, Desk 1, etx." be removed?

You can specify your own desk labels, see the FvwmPager man page.
