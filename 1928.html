>
> Are any of you using 2.3.27 with RH7? I'm thinking about giving it a shot, and
> would like your comments.

If you decide to use a beta, you should first go to RH7 Updates and
upgrade glibc to glibc-2.2 or greater, this is needed anyway for RH7.

You may either compile yourself, or get fvwm-2.3.27 rpm for RH6, which may
work for other distributions as well.

The way our rpms are created is that they do not conflict with RH or SuSE
fvwm2-2.2.4 rpm, so you may have both and switch between /usr/X11/bin/fvwm2
(2.2.4) and /usr/bin/fvwm2 (2.3.27). Remove ModulePath if you have any.

If you have problems, it is wise to stay with the stable version, or to
help with beta by investigating and reporting problems to fvwm-workers_at_.
