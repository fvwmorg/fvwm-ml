The endcaps are being combined with the main portion of the title bar,
making them appear about twice as bright. This is inconsistent
behavior, since the button pngs are only combined with the background
color. I think the behavior you're after is more useful, but I could
see both ways (combining with previous image and combining with color
only) being useful--perhaps there should be a MultiPixmap flag to
control this.
