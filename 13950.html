which works finte too. But you can do:

  __( '~/.xsession' )___________________________________________________
 /
| HOSTNAME=`cat /etc/hostname`
|
| if [ $HOSTNAME = "multimedia" ] ; then
| fvwm -f $HOME/.fvwm/config.multimedia
| else
| fvwm -f $HOME/.fvwm/config.default
| fi
 \______________________________________________________________________

and then put the specific configs in Subdirectories

like:
  _____________________________________________________________________
 /
| ~/.fvwm/config.default
| ~/.fvwm/config.multimedia
| ~/.fvwm/config.default
| ~/.fvwm/key.default
| ~/.fvwm/icons
|
| ~/.fvwm/default/keys
| ~/.fvwm/default/global
| ~/.fvwm/default/FvwmButton
| ~/.fvwm/default/FvwmTaskbar
|
| ~/.fvwm/multimedia/keys
| ~/.fvwm/multimedia/global
| ~/.fvwm/multimedia/FvwmButton
| ~/.fvwm/multimedia/FvwmTaskbar
