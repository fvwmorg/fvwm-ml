Yes, it is the case that Allegro uses Mainsoft. What a shame for an
application originally developed on Unix! Anyway, the suggestion in the
Fvwm FAQ was helpful. I spent some time with the Mainsoft pages. They
don't really want to support Unix it seems, which is rather strange.
Anyway, they suggest with reluctance defining the environment variable:

            setenv MWWM allwm

This I did and have not experienced crashing since. I still do experience
the placement of the application window with the title bar off screen. I
did try a variety of style options (DumbPlacement, ActivePlacement,
Random...) to no avail.
