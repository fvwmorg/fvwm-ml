On 8 Aug 97 at 14:06, Jesus Medina wrote:
 
> Warning: Cannot allocate default colormap entry
>
> And Netscape's colors are gone, that is, I only see a somewhat black and white
> Netscape interface. I commented the AddTitleStyle lines in my rc file and
> Netscape comes out normal, no warnings.
>
> Can anyone explains why is this? or how I can possibly fix this problem.

Netscape allocates a very big colour map all to itself when it starts up.
You have taken 64 of those colours into your title bars. Unless you can
support 16k colours or more, I would avoid the greedy title bars. If you
have an 8 bit colour depth (256 max. colours), there is a FAQ somewhere -
sorry don't have the URL - that will help you tidy up that colour map. I
think there is something in the Afterstep distribution too.

You could also try invoking netscape with the -install switch to install
its own colour map - but that might give you more psychedelia than a mere
mortal can survive..:-)
