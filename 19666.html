Charles Hines pointed out a solution.

##################################################
Style "FvwmPager" Lenience
Style "xclock" Lenience
# or
Style "*" Lenience
##################################################

This makes my xclock and FvwmPager both AutoRaise - which suggests that
both are not quite ICCCM compliant.
