> I do so, but
> Maximize 100 -24p doesn't gives me the correct result.
> When invoking with -20p there is a hole between the window and the
> FvwmTasBar, with -19p the two applications overlap ;(

Seems like you maximize a window that does not allow any given size in
pixels. For example, xterm or other terminal emulators allow only certain
sizes, i.e. with 7x13 font: 200 on 200 pixels, 213 on 207 pixels etc.
Try to resize such window manually and you will notice it jumps in size.
