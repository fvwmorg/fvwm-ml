David Wren-Hardin <dwrenhar_at_u.washington.edu> writes:

>
> In the console I get:
>
> FvwmIconMan: Bad action: sendcommand Focus, sendcommand "Iconify -1"
> FvwmIconMan: Offending line: *FvwmIconMan*action Mouse 1 N sendcommand Focus, sendcommand "
> FvwmIconMan: Bad action
> FvwmIconMan: Bad button: Iconify
> FvwmIconMan: sendcommand: too few arguments
> FvwmIconMan: Bad action: sendcommand Iconify
> FvwmIconMan: Offending line: *FvwmIconMan*action Mouse 2 N sendcommand Iconify
> FvwmIconMan: Bad action
>
>
> Any ideas what could be going wrong? Fvwm2.0.45 on an UltraSparc running
> Solaris 2.5.1.
>

This looks like a version mismatch for FvwmIconMan. You're using the new
syntax for actions, and those error messages sound like you're trying to give
them to an older version of FvwmIconMan.

I tried out your lines on my copy, and it works fine. You might change that
action for mouse button 1 to send the iconify -1 first and then the focus
though if your intent is to have the uniconified window receive the focus.
