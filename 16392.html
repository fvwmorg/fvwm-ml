> >The fix is to say:

> >Style "*" SmartPlacement, ActivePlacement
>
> Why would you want to have the WM pick a place for the window and then
> have it force you to move it around with the mouse and then click before
> you can start typing? Just curious.

SmartPlacement means that if there is room on-screen for the window,
then it will be placed there automatically.

DumbPlacement won't bother looking for space on screen.

ActivePlacement and RandomPlacement specify the policy to be used if
SmartPlacement/DumbPlacement fail.
