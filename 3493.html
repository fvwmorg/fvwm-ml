Some users that submitted screenshots also submitted
.fvwm2rc files. My original position was NOT to include
.fvwm2rc files on the screenshot page.

Since then, I've had a change of heart and will now accept
them. (Theres one there now.)

If you previously submitted a screen shot and would like to
submit an .fvwm2rc file, feel free to do so.

Try to make sure that your .fvwm2rc doesn't produce errors
and is reasonably up-to-date.

Screenshots or .fvwm2rc files can be sent to fvwm_at_fvwm.org
(as attachments).
