On Thu, 18 Apr 1996, Sergios wrote:

> I just installed fvwm 2.0.41. is it possible to bring a window to top
> (focus) without the mouse cursor moving to the top left corner of the
> window? I was thinking of the way MSWindows "circulates" windows with
> alt-tab. the mouse pointer does not move.
> circulate: with the old fvwm 1.x I could define this:
>
> Key Tab A M CirculateUp
> Key Tab A MS CirculateDown
>
> now it does not work. do I miss something...
>
> btw I read somewhere about fvwm-95 "which gives the 95 look and feel" and I
> suppose it is possible now to include pixmaps in the *title bar*? how can I
> do that?

Could this be a problem with Keyboard Modifiers in Fvwm2?

I like the sides to resize as well as the corners, but I would still
like to move windows which don't have a titlebar. So in fvwm-1 I was
able to make Control- or Meta-click move the window, but the following
does not work in fvwm-2. Any suggestions?

# Button 1 in the corner or sides, without modifiers, gives resize or raise
Mouse 1 FS A Function "Resize-or-Raise"
# Button 1 in the title, or border with modifiers, gives move or raise
Mouse 1 FS M Function "Move-or-Raise"
Mouse 1 FS C Function "Move-or-Raise"
Mouse 1 T A Function "Move-or-Raise"

For now I can use a menu bound to button two to move the window,
but it would be nice if the above could work too.

# Button 2 in the corners, sides, or title-bar gives the window ops menu
Mouse 2 FST A Function "window_ops_func"



BTW, I also see the "sticky" behavior, in which a window gets stuck in
"move" mode after clicking and releasing in the title bar. It happens
often (say, 1 of 20 clicks), and occurs even when the window is already
on top.
