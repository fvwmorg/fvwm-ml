> I have yet another problem. This time, I am trying to
> get FVWM2 to recognise a particular window, based on
> its ID. I have tried using FvwmEvent to capture this
> window ID, but so far, I am not having much luck.
>
> What I would like to happen, is once FVWM2 detects
> that this window has appeared, I would like for it to
> be
> "closed" and NOT killed, since killing this window
> closes the entire application. Has anyone got any
> ideas??
>
> I have tried to follow the examples in the manual
> pages (FVWM2 and FvwmEvent), but either I am a
> luddite, or

> I assume that the FvwmEvent modules is what I am
> supposed to be using??

See FAQ question 3.18, it explains several solutions with and without
FvwmEvent depending on your needs.

If you know window id or window title or both use one of the commands:

  Next ("My Window Title") Close
  WindowId 0x140000e Close
  WindowId $w ("My Window Title") Close
