Congratulations on the first 10 years!

I've been using fvwm since I installed my first Linux-distribution,
Yggdrasil Linux, Fall '94.

I've tried other windowmanagers for a couple of hours from time to
time, but I've always returned quickly to fvwm; it works like I expect
it to, and I can have wide borders on all sides of my windows easily.

Here's to the next 10 years, and thanks for the first 10!
