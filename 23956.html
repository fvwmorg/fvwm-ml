This is probably only of interest for those who want more
out of their .fvwm2rc configs, but either haven't the time,
luck, or resources, to find anything better.
It's the result of 'hacking up' a 3000 line .rc file into
several dozen files and directories.... then adding to it.
It needs no special installing of any libraries.
It merely is a collection of simple fvwm2 commands as
documented in the man pages.
It allows menu selction of decors, including the default
to load at Init/Restart, and menu control of FvwmEvent
configs, including individual events.
It also allows mutiple modules with individual configs.
All files are in relevant directories. Easily modifiable.
Separate configs for modules, decors etc....

Just posted awols-fvwm2config0.2g.tar.gz
and awols-fvwm2config0.2g.tar.bz2 (download either/or)
Icons included in pack. Screenshots are available.
Site is http://dove.net.au/~awol/fvwm2

I will hopefully find a way to put a save decor feature
into version 0.3
Still working on lots of other features....
Let me know if you have any problems with it.
In fact, just let me know if you are using it!
