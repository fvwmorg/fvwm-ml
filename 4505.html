John Smith wrote:

> I'm also a bit confused by the difference between handles and borders.

> I suppose handles are just the parts around the window that let you

> do some move/resize/user config thing?

> And borders are around the handles? Please explain.


Borders are the borders around undecorated windows, i.e. without titles
and resize _handles_. Handles are the borders around decorated windows.
