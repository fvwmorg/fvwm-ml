I've just installed fvwm2.0.46 on a DEC Alpha running DU4.0. So far
everything works fine except that I seem unable to get any styles for
the buttons in any titlebars for any windows.

I've tried something as simple as

ButtonStyle 1 Pixmap mini.exit.xpm

as well as

ButtonStyle 1 4 50x65_at_1 35x35_at_1 65x35_at_1 50x65_at_0
ButtonStyle 4 4 50x65_at_1 35x35_at_1 65x35_at_1 50x65_at_0
