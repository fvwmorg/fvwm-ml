On Wed, May 22, 2002 at 11:41:59PM -0400, Eduardo Gargiulo wrote:
> I'd customized .fvwm2rc, but now, when I hit F4 to show up WindowList,
> the process list disappear when i release the key and next time it
> appear in the upper left corner of the window focused. How can I freeze
> the WindowList so i could choose any process?

It's hard to say why this is happening without your config. Can
you send the lines that are responsible for said behaviour, please?
Anyway,

  Key F4 A N WindowList

should do the trick.

> Another question: how can I customize (eg: desktop #) the text of each
> process in windowlist?

There are some options that modify the displayed string, e.g.
NoGeometry, NoGeometryWithInfo, NoDeskNum, NoNumInDeskTitle,
NoCurrentDeskTitle.

For example, use

  WindowList NoGeometry

to get the bare list of window names without any additional
information. For more details, take a look at the man page.
