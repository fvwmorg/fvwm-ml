>>>>> "Alex" == Alex Iskold <iskola_at_mcsnyw72.mcsunx.gs.com> writes:

Alex> Hello, we are trying to force all of our users to use FVWM,

You shouldn't try and force anyone to use FVWM. They should see you
using it and just want to jump on the bandwagon themselves!

Alex> however, htey are resisting because of their mwm resource files.
Alex> Is there a program (script) that would convert .xxx files from
Alex> othere window managers to .fvwmrc.

Nope. The styles of rc's are all pretty different, and a conversion
script wouldn't be the easiest thing to do. But there are lots of rc
examples out there, and making your own isn't too bad.

Take a look at the official web site:

                     http://www.hpc.uh.edu/fvwm/

and it has pointers to all kinds of info, including many rc file
examples.

And if you want to make a script to convert parts of .mwmrc files to
..fvwmrc or .fvwm2rc syntax, feel free to contribute it!
