Hello,

  I just recently upgraded to fvwm2. I am as pleased with the improvement
over fvwm as I was with fvwm over mwm. It's just amazing!

  Most things from the fvwm config file mapped over nicely to the fvwm2,
albeit they became Style parameters as opposed to standalone commands.
One thing which has apparently disappeared though is OpaqueResize. I
cannot find the equivalent to this. Did it disappear? This particular
command would be an excellent thing to do on a window-by-window basis
(there are only a few things I want to resize "opaquely", so if this
were a new Style command somehow, I would be very pleased.

  Also Chuck, awhile back I reported my wish to not have the click of the
mouse which brings the window into focus (I use ClickToFocus on most
windows) be passed through to the window itself. You mentioned that it
would be an easy fix and even supplied a patch I believe. Did this ever
make it into the production code?

  Lastly, according to the pl35 man page, there is an undocumented
option that I am curious about: SkipMapping/ShowMapping. What's this?

  Thanks,

    ...Lance
