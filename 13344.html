There seems to be a bug in FvwmIdentify in 2.5.12.

When an FvwmIdentify comes up, it is completely blank;
its contents are not visible.

But if you cover the window with another window, say and xterm,
then uncover it, then the FvwmIdentify window repaints itself and
its contents become visible.
