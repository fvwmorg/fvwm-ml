        My StartsOnPage patch has been revised to correct the known
defects, and can be fetched from

        http://www.pcnet/com/~proteus/Fvwm/FvwmPatches.html

        This version corrects the misplacement of StartsOnPage windows
when the starting page is at a negative offset from the current page
and SkipMapping is also in effect, and handles the restoring of
recaptured StartsOnPage windows to their starting pages. (It could be
argued that this latter should be optional, but it's consistent with
StartsOnDesk.)

        Any comments, suggestions, or (shudder) bug reports will be
welcomed.


        Cheers,
