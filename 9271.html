On Tue, Jun 17, 2003 at 08:23:25PM -0400, Subba Rao wrote:
> Hello,
>
> I am slowly (very) getting to understand the fvwm2 configuration. My
> desktop is slowly looking the way I would like it to look like. The only
> thing missing is the wallpaper. I am using Gnome 2 on Slackware 9 with
> fvwm 2.4.16.
>
> I tried to set a wall paper using GThumb and that did not work. How do I setup
> a wallpaper when fvwm session begins?
>
> Thank you in advance.

I use:

AddToFunc InitFunction "I" Module FvwmPager 0 0
+ "I" Module FvwmAuto 500 raise
+ "I" Exec xv -root -quit -max /home/alaric/Wallpaper/aurora.jpg
