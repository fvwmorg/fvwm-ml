On 27 May 2002 12:40:21 +0530, subbiah .L wrote:
>
> I am having fvwm2 ver 2.4.3. I am having one customised Motif application,
> that will in my screen always. I want a option that , If I press a key
> combination, then my Motif application has to Come in front for my use (
> Focus + Raise ). This should be irrelevent of any page I am working.

  AddToFunc ComeHere
  + I MoveToPage
  + I Iconify false
  + I WindowShade false
  + I Raise
  + I Focus
  + I WarpToWindow 50 50

  Key M A MC Next (my_motif_app) ComeHere

Press Ctrl-Alt-M.
