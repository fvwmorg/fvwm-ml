>
> Well, no :-) I like the taskbar and pager to stay until I dismiss
> them. I am happy with every aspect of how they work now, without
> Transient and without NeverFocus, except I want them to pop up under
> the cursor. Transient itself seems to me to do to many things. Pager
> disappears as soon as any key is hit, but WinList stays around longer.

This command brings the context window into the pointer in 2.5.x:

  Move $[pointer.x]p $[pointer.y]p

Use it together with FAQ #3.18.
