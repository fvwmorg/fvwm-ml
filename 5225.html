> Hi!

> I'm using fvwm under debian/woody from packages.
>
> Now I cannot move windows outside from the screen. (expect xmms)
> How can this behaviour changed?
>
> I fire up xosview from init.hook
> How to change this programs style?
> If I wrote some styles into the post.hook or pre.hook files (which
> should be processed BEFORE anything happens), I cannot change the
> style. However if I open an xosview after fvwm has started, the style is

I don't know how the debian system works, but the commands you
need are EdgeResistance, EdgeScroll and DesktopSize. Check the
man page. Normally, you put your configuration commands in your
.fvwm2rc file.
