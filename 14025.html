> Hello all.
>
> So I have a question : I use fvwm 2.5.6 (didn't succeed to switch to
> 2.5.12 that crashes unexpectadly) and switched to Linux Debian Sarge
> from Woody, kernel 2.6.8 and the Gimp 2.2. When I tear off menus
> from the Gimp, they are no more decorated.

I've got current CVS Fvwm here, and gimp2.1.
The gimp tearoff menus are decorated.
I don't see any special settings in my config file for gimp.

> More : the menus are pinned and so are said "sticky" by fvwm.

They aren't sticky either.

> I tried customizatons in the Gimp about "windows to
> windowsmanagers",got out ewmh by recompiling fvwm without the
> support. played with some ewmh parameters, all without success. I
> browsed the FAQs and did some googling with no chance. So excuse-me
> if I was to weird to find obvious answers before disturbing the
> list.

There are 'ignorehints' settings for Gnome and Ewmh,
no need to compile out the feature.

> I have the DecorateTransient param in my Gimp windows style.

Use FvwmIdent to verify that your style matches the transient
window for the menu.
