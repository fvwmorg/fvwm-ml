On Thu, 22 Jul 1999, Dominik Vogt wrote:
>
> I'm not quite sure if you are aware that fvwm95 and fvwm (aka fvwm2)
> are different window managers.

Dominik, I'm not sure I know what you mean by this statement. In the
RedHat setup, the stript /usr/X11R6/bin/RunWM executes the statement

eval "exec fvwm${FVWMVER} ${FVWMOPTIONS}" > "$HOME"/.FVWM${FVWMVER}-errors
2>&1

where FVWMVER is either fvwm2, fvwm95 or fvwm95-2 with precedence in that
order.

and by default FVWMOPTIONS is

"-cmd 'FvwmM4 -debug /etc/X11/AnotherLevel/fvwm2rc.m4"

In fact in this file you will even find statements like:

    echo "RESUMING with Fvwm2 (95 look)" >&2
    Start_Fvwm95

For all I can tell, fvwm95 is fvwm2 with the decors set up for the "95
look". It's not my preferred decor, but...
