Andrew Nesbit <alnesbit_at_optushome.com.au> writes:
> Hi, I was just wondering if it is possible to configure fvwm in such a
> way that when I iconify an app, I /don't/ actually get an icon on the
> desktop, but I am able to access the iconified apps via a menu?

To suppress icons:

Style * NoIcon

You can still access apps using Alt-tab (Windowlist),
FvwmIconMan, FvwmIconBox, FvwmTaskBar.
