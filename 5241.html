> > I've already tried that, no result. I have
> following
> > line in my config file:
>
> > MenuStyle Wheat DimGrey Black
> lucidasanstypewriter-12
> > Mwm PopupImmediately PopdownImmediately
>
> That's not a legal MenuStyle line. You are mixing
> the old and the
> new MenuStyle syntax. Remove all your MenuStyle
> lines and try
> this instead:
>
> MenuStyle * mwm, Font lucidasanstypewriter-12
> MenuStyle * Foreground Wheat, Background DimGrey,
> Greyed Black
> MenuStyle * PopupImmediately, PopdownImmediately
>
> It's important to issue the mwm style first because
> it resets most
> of the other options, including PopupImmediately.

Thanks a lot, it worked!
