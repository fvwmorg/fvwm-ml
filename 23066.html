---Reply to mail from Bradley Owen about FVWM: Focus Question

> What I want to do is launch a program from an FvwmButton such that it
> will have the input focus without my having to move the mouse over it.
> Is that possible?
>
> Thanks.

Hello Bradley,

Make the mouse focus "sloppy". When you start a program the focus
will be on the window, unless the mouse is on a different window.

So when you click on the icon, move the cursor off of the icon onto
the background before the program comes up.

HTH,
Paul
