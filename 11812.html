Hello All.

If anybody has interest.
I have ru_RU.UTF-8 locale. FvwmTaskBar is crashes when windows title has
Russian letters and long (For example while read mail in Mozilla). May
be, some peaple have same problem with Unicode locales.

I fix it for me, but it is VERY bad solve. I'm not fvwm developer and
not specialist by fvwm. I can't understand "why", but I found "how".

In file modules/FvwmTaskBar/ButtonArray.c and ButtonNew routine, I
truncated window title length to 40 characters. After this, FvwmTaskBar
works fine.
