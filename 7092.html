William Denton wrote:
> I've just started using rdesktop to connect remotely to a Windows 2000 box
> (it's a really handy program), but I've got a problem: my control and alt
> keypresses are being passed through when sometimes I'd rather they
> weren't. I use C-arrow to move around the virtual windows, but when I
> have the rdesktop window in focus I lose that ability. I have to move the
> mouse, bring something else into focus, then do C-arrow. Is there some
> way I can stop fvwm from passing through particular keystrokes to certain
> windows? I looked through the man pages and did some searches but didn't
> see anything matching the keywords I could think of.
>
> Thanks,
>
> Bill

-K --vmkeys
       By default the keybindings of the windowmanager will be ignored
       when the rdesktop window is in focus. Use this setting to keep
       the windowmanager keybindings.

  Manual page rdesktop.1 line 53
