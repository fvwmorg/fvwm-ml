> Ok, just typed in FvwmConsole and it works without any
> Colorset 50 Transparent, bg pink3, fg gray20
> Style FvwmButtons-Trans ParentalRelativity
> DestroyModuleConfig FvwmButtons-Trans: *
> *FvwmButtons-Trans: Colorset 50
> *FvwmButtons-Trans: (Icon /some/existing/transparent/image.png)
> *FvwmButtons-Trans: (Swallow FvwmPager-Trans "FvwmPager FvwmPager-Trans")
>
> DestroyModuleConfig FvwmPager-Trans: *
> *FvwmPager-Trans: Colorset * 50
>
> FvwmButtons FvwmButtons-Trans
