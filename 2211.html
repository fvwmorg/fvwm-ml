Hello,

I need some kind of semi-sticky behaviour: I want a window to follow
me when I'm on Desk1, but when I switch to Desk0 I want it to stay on
Desk1, until I switch back to Desk1 -- then it should follow me again.

I'm running 2.2.4 on SunOS5.7, and I guess the above behaviour can be
achieved with FvwmEvent, I'm gonna work on this. But maybe this is an
enhancement request:

Why not ``blur'' the distinction between Sticky and Slippery, so you
could say, e.g.

Style Window10 Sticky(0 (0 0) (0 1) (0 3) 1 (2 2) (3 3))

(this is _very_ strange syntax, I know, but I can't figure out how to
make it better)

meaning that Window10 should be sticky on (Desk0, Pages 0 0, 0 1, and
0 3) and on (Desk1, Pages 2 2 and 3 3).

olleH,

Ulrich Fahrenberg
