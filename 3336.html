On 09 Oct 2001 16:58:08 -0500, Glen Lee Edwards wrote:
>
> This must not be what you mean, because it isn't working:
>
> TitleStyle Inactive ForeColor black
>
> Nor does this:
>
> Style "TitleBar" Inactive ForeColor black
>
> Mikhael Goikhman writes:
> > On 07 Oct 2001 20:47:08 -0500, Glen Lee Edwards wrote:
> > >
> > > How do I change the TitleBar font color on an inactive window, and on an active
> > > one? It seems that inactive defaults to white while active defaults to black.
> >
> > This color is controlled by Style options ForeColor or Colorset,
> > and HilightFore or HilightColorset.
> >
> > Note that if you use decors, I can understand your problems, you can't
> > change this color per-decor, you should change it per-style.

The correct syntax is:

  Style * ForeColor darkred, HilightFore darkgreen
