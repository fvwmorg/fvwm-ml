Alas! Peter McAlpine spake thus:
> I don't see how 16 pixels makes that much of a difference.

16 extra pixels in both the x and y direction means 1792 extra pixels
for each icon: 1792 x 11 icons = 19712 pixels of absolutely wasted space
with _nothing_ in it.

I use FvwmButtons primarily for "quicklaunch" icons for my often-used
apps, and all the icons are 48x48. It just looks bad with an 8 pixel
wide border all the way around each icon.
