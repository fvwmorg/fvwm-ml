>>>>>> "DK" == David Kennedy <D.Kennedy_at_Queens-Belfast.AC.UK> writes:

    DK> This is becoming a very FAQ...and its not directly fvwm related.

    ch> I have some info about the Sun keyboards in the FAQ already (question
    ch> 19), and I just put a reference to xev & xkeycaps as well.

    DK> I have a key on my Sun (type 5?) keyboard, beside the F keys,
    DK> with nothing on it (beside 'Help'). I'd like for it to be
    DK> 'Lock Screen'. Running 'xev' shows it up as 'NoSymbol'.

    ch> Anyone out there using a Sun box w/ a type 5 keyboard have
    ch> anything to say?

Not really, what's the problem? :)

It's certainly true that the key is unbound in "standard" X11 (I just
tried it with X11R6.1 on my Sun with a type 5 keyboard, and it's
"NoSymbol", or unbound).

But so what? Just bind it to something and it'll work fine. I used
xkeycaps to bind it to the symbol "Return" (I couldn't find a symbol
called "LockScreen" or variant anywhere), and voila! Now I have two
return keys on my keyboard.

No big deal.
