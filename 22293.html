I'm running fvwm2 on both sparc and intel solaris2 (2.6, and 2.7 beta) in
8,16,24, and 32 bpp modes -- 1280x1024 screen generally, but I tried both
larger (1600x1200) and smaller (1024x768) and tried the screen grab in xv
3.10a -- it worked just fine here. Is there anything else that could be
effecting things, like
        a) wierd permissions on devices,
        b) running your display without doing the semi-obligatory
                xhost + (whatever your hostname is),
        c) dynamic libraries updated since the build of xv, or
        d) dynamic libraries updated since the build of fvwm2 (or a binary
                distribution of unknown build status)?

Just my $0.02 worth...
