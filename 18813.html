Hi!

Here are some suggestions about features I feel missing in fvwm. Some
of them might be of inspiration to some of you :-)

- Wildcards in DestroyMenu (eg. DestroyMenu "*" doesn't work)
- a Style option allowing a window to get its icon from a {bit,pix}map
of same name (eg. "Style "*" AutoIcon" would be somewhat equivalent to
"Style foo Icon <foo.xpm OR foo>", whatever is found first, for every
possible "foo")
- I didn't find a command that would customize icon placement on
screen. I would eg. like them to be placed at the bottom of the
screen, right-to-left...
- References to standard modules' manpages in fvwm2 manpage, section
SeeAlso could be nice, for TkMan with produces hyperlinks from these..

- Perhaps an option in FvwmButtons to allow icon resizing to fit the
computed button-size ?
- A Command in FvwmButtons to reread its conf-file ? (instead of
having to ask fvwm to reread the conffile, killing and restarting the
module)

 That's all for now :-)
Keep up the good work !
