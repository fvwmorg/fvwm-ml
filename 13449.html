If you want your customary key combinations to go to the Xnest'ed fvwm try this oddity when Xnest has the focus: press the NumLock key, work in the Xnest window, unpress NumLock when finished in the Xnest window. It works with VNC - YMMV. If I needed this a lot I'd try to get the outer fvwm to press/unpress NumLock for me.

I've never got to the bottom of whether this is a bug, a feature or maybe even deliberate functionality. The outer fvwm ignores everything I do when NumLock is down because I don't have that modifier in any of my key combinations. This means the keys go through to the fvwm inside the VNC session. Somewhere along the line the NumLock modifier seems to get dropped, so by the time the keypresses reach the nested fvmw, they match my normal WM config. and do the expected operations on the windows in the nested session.
