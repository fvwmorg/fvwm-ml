I think Fvwm requires all the buttons to be in some kind of ratio. (ie,252 is
not a multiple of 68)

> *MailBox: (Swallow(UseOld,NoHints,Respawn) "gkrellm" \ `Exec exec
> gkrellm` Size 68 252)

*MailBox: (1x4, Swallow(UseOld,NoHints,Respawn) "gkrellm" \
        `Exec exec gkrellm`)

will achieve something close to what you are looking for.

> Clear 1 column (or 1 row) examples, even if they are complicated are most
> welcome and appreciated.

You can read about Containers.. maybe it'll help you achieve what you are
looking for with a couple of blank buttons ensuring that'll pad gkrellm (top
and bottom) to achieve some kind of ratio relationship.
