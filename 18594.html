Yo!

With 2.0.43 and ClickToFocus on Solaris 2.5 (OpenWindows) I notice the
following: I have something that executes

    xmessage -nearmouse -default okay -geometry +50+50 "$msg"

This makes this little window appear and I can do two things to make
it disappear:

(1) Click the `Okay' button with the mouse.
    This works fine: the window disappears and focus goes to the
    window that had focus before the xmessage window appeared.

(2) Hit the Return key.
    This also makes the xmessage window disappear, but focus *doesn't*
    return to the window that last had focus :-(

What's the story?
