On Wed, Nov 13, 2002 at 03:09:26PM -0500, Peter McAlpine wrote:
> hey. I remember some change being made to the Next and Prev commands,
> and every since then they've been annoying.
>
> my alt-tab command:
> Key Tab A M Next [CurrentPage !iconic] FocusRaise
>
> my whole .fvwm2rc file:
> www.uoguelph.ca/~pmcalpin/pub/fvwm/my.fvwm2rc
>
> Sometimes alt-tab works ok, and sometimes it leaves windows out of the
> cycle. I've found that the cycle just stops at xclock and Gaim. There
> are probably other that block the cycle, as well, but I haven't
> noticed what they are yet.

This isn't new. The xclock window does not want to be focused and
thus stops the cycle. You can either force it to accept the
focus:

  Style xterm Lenience

Or skip windows that do not want to be focused:


That problem has been around for a long time
Yeah, but I believe it's not the change I made to "Next" but the
bug with the "Key" command that causes it to work like
"Pointerkey". I have fixed this with my latest commit. If the
problem still does not go away, please report back.

  Key Tab A M Next (CurrentPage, acceptsfocus, !iconic) FocusRaise

(Note that the syntax with conditions in brackets and separated
with spaces will probably be removed in 3.0).
