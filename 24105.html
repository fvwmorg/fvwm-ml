My FvwmWinList tends to creep off the window to the left. I place it
so that it's right on the edge of the screen. Then I look later and
it has moved left and is 1/4 the way off the screen. I'm not sure
what makes it move. Maybe when there are long titles that have to get
truncated. I am supplying a min and max width. Can anyone tell me
how to fix this? My configuration is below:

*FvwmWinListBack #c0b0c0
*FvwmWinListFore Black
*FvwmWinListFont -adobe-helvetica-bold-r-*-*-10-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
*FvwmWinListAction Click1 Iconify -1,Focus,Raise
*FvwmWinListAction Click2 Iconify
*FvwmWinListAction Click3 Module "FvwmIdent" FvwmIdent
*FvwmWinListUseSkipList
*FvwmWinListGeometry +0-120
*FvwmWinListFocusBack yellow
*FvwmWinListMinWidth 120
*FvwmWinListMaxWidth 120

Oh yeah, that FvwmWinListFocusBack doesn't seem to work as advertised
either. I don't see any yellow.
