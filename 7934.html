> Ok, forget all I say about taskbar ! IconMan is really what I want ! (Fvwm
> is really the best software I ever seen ! If you want it, look, it's in
> FVWM... Is fvwm doing some coffee ?)
>
> I have a tiny problem : when no window is launched, my iconMan is an ugly
> box with large grey borders ! :(
>
> How to make IconMan disappear when no window is shown ?

How about making the background transparent and removing the
border?

  colorset 77 transparent
  *FvwmIconMan: *Colorset 77
  Style FvwmIconMan sticky, parentalrelativity, handlewidth 0, borderwidth

If that is not good enough it becomes really tricky.
