On Wed, 29 Apr 1998, Jin Ko wrote:

->Hi all,
->
-> I have three buttons on my fvwm title bar:
->(1)Function, (4)Iconify, (2)Close.
->I'd like to omit some of them for certain applications or
->modules like:
->
->Defaults: 1, 4, 2
->FvwmButtons: none
->TkDesk: 1, 4
->Xclock: 1, 2
->...
->
->How can I do this?
->

Use the Style NoButton option. Edit your .fvwmrc (or .fvwm2rc or
whatever it's called) and add/modify the following lines:

Style "FvwmButtons" NoButton 1, NoButton 2, NoButton 4
Style "TkDesk" NoButton 2
Style "XClock" NoButton 4


Cheers
