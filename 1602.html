} screen, so I want to maximise things so that they're not on that. I guess I

If you have fvwm 2.3.21 or greater, you might want to try

 Maximize 100 grow

The 'grow' argument grows the window until it hits another window (in
this case probably the panel).

I just like grow on general principles. It's been pretty useful to me.
