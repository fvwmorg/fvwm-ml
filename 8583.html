Do you mean you do not want to have icons on the screen but want
application icons displayed besides the titles of icons?

If so, RTFM (M: FvwmIconMan)...

  *FvwmIconMan: [id] DrawIcons value
        If your version of fvwm is capable of using mini icons,
        then this option determines if FvwmIconMan displays the mini
        icons. Otherwise, it generates an error message. "true"
        means that mini icons are shown for iconified windows,
        "false" that mini icons are never shown, and "always" that
        mini icons are shown for all windows.
