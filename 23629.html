%% "John R. Smith" <jrsmith_at_eng.utah.edu> writes:

  jr> Awesome! Does anyone have .fvwmrc files that give a 95/98/NT look?

Well, you should be able to take your .fvwm95rc file and copy it to
.fvwm2rc pretty much as-is and have it work.

Keep the icons, etc. from fvwm95 because they're a lot more complete
than what comes with fvwm2 at the moment.

The major differences would be in the modules; particularly the task
bar; many of the fancier features of the fvwm95 task bar aren't in
fvwm2, I don't think, like the dynamic loading modules, etc. (I honestly
have never used the fvwm2 FvwmTaskBar module, and I've only used the
fvwm95 one for about 5 seconds). Maybe fvwm2 TaskBar statically links
those instead.

Other modules, like FvwmButtons, should be fine.
