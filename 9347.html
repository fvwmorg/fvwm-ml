In a couple of previous message I asked about the possibility of
varying the titlebar button style per window. I am glad to report
that I have figured out how to do it. I use fvwm's "State" boolean
flags to keep track of each window's state. Then I change the
button's decorations according to its state. Works pretty
nicely, although the corresponding script is an ugly hack.

I have placed a screenshot in

    http://www.math.umbc.edu/~rouben/fvwm-screenshot.png

Titlebar buttons on the right side of the titlebar perform the
following actions:

   maximize to fill screen
   maximize vertically
   maximize horizontally
   window shade
   iconify

Each one (except the iconify button) acts as a toggle. When
the button is in toggled state, its color changes from green
to red.
