fvwm-2.0.45

The man page for fvwm, when describing ``Style windowname options''
lists ``IconTitle/NoIconTitle''. As indicated in the next paragraph
in the man, this implies that NoIconTitle is the default, which is
wrong. Hence the listing should be ``NoIconTitle/IconTitle''. Below
is the trivial context diff.

Thanks for fvwm-2.0.45
allan gottlieb
