Hello again,

Just two suggestions for potential additions to the "TODO" list, assuming
they're not there already:

1. a function (module?) that takes scattered icons and aligns them to a
grid within an IconBox, perhaps with the option to sort them based on the
icon title, class, etc.

2. option to turn of "3D" beveling on things swallowed by FvwmButtons,
eg. clocks and pagers. Perhaps they could even appear "sunken", the
reverse of a button. That'd be cool.

Cheers,

Dave
