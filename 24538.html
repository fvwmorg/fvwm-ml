Dominik Vogt writes:
> On Thu, Jul 08, 1999 at 11:21:20PM -0500, Nathan J Froyd wrote:
> > But to phrase it carefully: If I have a pixmap button that I want to fill
> > the entire allocated button area, how do I do that? I am running fvwm
> > 2.2.2 and my ButtonStyles are Flat. I have MWMBorder and MWMButtons set in
> > my Style lines and fvwm still insists on placing an unnecessary one or two
> > pixel relief area around the button (although the button is larger than
> > the space available; fvwm only provides the "buffer" on the top and left
> > sides).
>
> I think this will require a patch. Although tremendously complicated
> the ButtonStyle code isn't very flexible yet.

Actually, I accomplished it with the following:

ButtonStyle 2 ActiveUp Pixmap e-buttons/kill_normal.xpm -- Top Right Flat
TitleStyle LeftJustified Height 18 -- Top Left Flat

And similarly for the other buttons. I have no idea what the 'Top'
directives do...but it works!
