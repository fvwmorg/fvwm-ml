>
> Is there a way to bind something to the print-screen, Pause and
> Scoll-lock keys on a PC keyboard?

You may have to modify your Xmodmap. I can't give you a detailed
description, but use the xmodmap command to modify it. Make sure
the keys are bound to something (xkeycaps can help too). To bind
The scroll lock key may be mapped as a modifier. You can't bind
to modifiers, so you'll have to remove the corresponding entry
in the Xmodmap to make that work.

> How would I bind a key to focus the FvwmButtons? Sometimes this gets
> hidden under windows and it'd be nice to hit something to pop it to the
> top.

Key <some key> A N Next (FvwmButtons) Function RaiseAndFocus
AddToFunc RaiseAndFocus
 + I Raise
 + I Focus
