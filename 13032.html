On Tue, Aug 03, 2004 at 12:01:06PM -0700, m p wrote:
> Hi,
> I have the following setup for a menu:
>
>
> AddToMenu RootMenu "Windows" Title
> + "Move" Move
> + "Resize" Resize
> ....
>
> and
>
> Mouse 2 R A Menu RootMenu Nop
>
> Why is it that once I open the menu with middle
> mouse button and unwillingly click
> on the top (Title) a new window pops up with
> RootMenu

It's a feature that allows pinning menus to the desktop (aka "tear
off menus"). Usually - for example in gimp - there is a dashed
horizontal bar at the top of a menu that can be clicked to tear a
menu off. But fvwm supports tearing off for all menus, and
clicking with button 2 on the title bar is one way to achieve this
(pressing Backspace is another).

Unfortunately there is no way to close a tear off menu with the
mouse yet.

> and how to get rid of this?

What Tavis said. You can close tear off menus by pressing Escape
with the mouse over the menu window.
