I use point-to-focus focus style. I made a desktop with four screens:

DeskTopSize 1 1
*FvwmPagerRows 1
*FvwmPagerColumns 4

and bound C-M-right and C-M-left to cicle between these screens:

Key Left A CM Desk -1 0 3
Key Right A CM Desk 1 0 3

I made several windows sticky. I move a mouse pointer into a sticky window
to made it focused, then press C-M-right (or left) to move to another
screen (mouse pointer is still in the same window) and get in unfocused so
I have to move pointer outside of it and back inside to make is focused on
the new screen again. I want to make window under pointer to be always
focused when I switch screens. How can I do it?
