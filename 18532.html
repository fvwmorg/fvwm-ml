Carl said:
> I have two questions. First, Is there a way that I can make a button(on the
> new fvwm2rc95-fvwm95-2.0.41) that goes directly to a telnet location? By this
> I mean: I want to click on a button that takes me automatically takes me to a
> location, let's say info.rutgers.edu. or 150.333.3435.554 whatever. Is this
> possible? My second question is how do I get the Autoraise function to NOT be
> executed at startup. I hate having to turn it off each time I enter. Thank
> you for your time.

A button to go directly to a telnet location:

(1x1, Padding 0 0, Title BIDS Action `Exec "xterm" /usr/lib/X11/xt
erm -ls -sl 2500 -T "Bids" -geometry +75+200 -n Bids -e telnet bids &`)

Not execute autoraise at startup:
Go to your .fvwmr2rc95 (or whatever) and look for the InitFunction,
find a line with 'FvwmAuto' in it. Simply remove that line.

If you use a system-wide file then you need to copy it over, make
your customisation and then log out/in to see the effect.
