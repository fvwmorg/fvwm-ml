"Patrick K." <patrick_at_sid.BoXsoft.com> writes:

> I admit that I didn't do much research on this question but
> I can't see any thing in my ${HOME}/.fvwm2rc that would cause
> this.
>
> I recently noticed that when using xv to open images that are
> bigger than my Virtual desk size fvwm2 will force the xv image
> window fit this size.

Nothing to do with fvwm. You are seeing documented xv behavior. xv
will internally keep you image at the original size. However, you
need to be careful when saving an image - to retain the original size
you need to select ``Normal Size'' in the ``Save'' dialog.

> My question is: Is there anyway one could disable this behavior?

Maybe. What do the xv docs (usually ``/usr/doc/xv/xvdocs.ps'') say?
