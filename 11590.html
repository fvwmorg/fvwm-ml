Hi there,

Using framemaker I experienced a strange behaviour. When I
do a "change desk" to the current page where framemaker is
running, the framemaker window remains emtpy as if it would
need to update its contents. The contents are drawn after I
do a "change page" (switch to another page and come back) or
if I move another window over the framemaker window. It
seems if framemaker is not notified on a desk change, but
gets notified on a page change or window movement.

The behaviour is reproduceable. I have not observed this
behaviour with other applications yet.

(fvwm 2.5.7)
