Greetings,

I just upgraded my Toshiba Techra 730CDT (C&T 65550 chip) to RedHat 4.2
which includes XFree86 3.3 with various accelerator enhancements. My
fvwm2 session looks the same. Performance looks good. However, when I
move a client with the title bar, the entire screen seems to have a
severe flicker problem. I can observe various ghosted images of the
screen flickering at odd places during the move. All seems normal as
soon as I stop the motion.

I am posting here because it does NOT happen in fvwm95 session so it
seems to be related to the fvwm2 which I prefer.

I am using fvwm2 2.0.45-3 which I got from the redhat site as an RPM. I
checked the FVWM home page and that is the newest version available.

Anyone else having similar problems? Any Suggestions?
