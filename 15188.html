E> From the FvwmPager man page:

E> Dragging mouse button 2 on a miniature view of a window will
E> cause that window to be move to the location where you
E> release the mouse button, but your viewport will not change.
E> If you drag the window out of the pager and onto your desk-
E> top, a full size image of the window will appear for you to
E> place. There is no way to pick up a full size image of the
E> window and move it into the pager, however. Since some mice
E> do not have button 2, I have made provisions to drag windows
E> in the pager by using pressing modifier-1 (usually Alt) and
E> dragging with button 3.
