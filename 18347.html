Hello,

I just changed my windowmanager from mwm to fvwm2 because of some
intersting fvwm-features. Generally I am quite happy with fvwm, but I
miss some things from mwm:

With mwm I could give any X-Client special decor with *clientDecoration
(like in fvwm too) and special window-manager-functionallity with
*clientFunctions (not found in Fvwm). There are some clients I would
prefer to give them no window-manager-functionality at all...

In the Button Bindings, you have several decoration contextes: RWTS...
Why is S for the Resize-Handlers on the window-edges AND for the
window-border which is produced with Style*NoHandlers ?? This leads to
strange functionality, I can resize windows without resizehandlers...
Maybe it would be easier if F stands for the corners and the edges of a
resize-handler windowborder... (like in mwm, resizeh <-> border)

Are there solutions for those problems?

Greetings...
