Is there a way to highlight the active fg menu item without having to

MenuStyle * MenuFace DGradient 255 2 darkgray 50 MidnightBlue 50 white
MenuStyle * SidePic happy.xpm
MenuStyle * ActiveFore AntiqueWhite

but also no AntiqueWhite on the active menu item ...

What I really want is the AntiqueWhite fg & my menuface gradient to
