On 31 Dec, Alexis Rosoff wrote:
> Which path are you using? In order to use full colour pixmaps your pixmap
> path must be set (the pixmap icons don't go in the IconPath). I mention
> this because when I first saw it in FVWM it seemed slightly illogical.
> There IS a reason of course but putting pixmaps in the IconPath won't
> work.
>
> So the PixmapPath should look like this:
>
> PixmapPath /usr/X11/include/X11/pixmaps/:/usr/local/icons
>
> Or wherever you keep your pixmaps.
>

Aha! Bingo! Didier Nghia Le Tien also pointed out the same thing to me
but I didn't realize what he was talking about until now. I didn't know
you had to have an IconPath *and* a pixmap path. So it ended up being a
problem with the dotfile generator (which doesn't support PixmapPath
for some reason), not fvwm or me (well, inasmuch that I haven't yet read
the manpage from a-z, which make me unaware of these little details ;P).

Thanks Alexis and all the others who helped me out so quickly.
