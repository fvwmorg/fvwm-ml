I wouldn't agree - it's in beta state for a reason still, and beta
generally means that the whole world shouldn't know about it, just a

mess things up so it doesn't compile or link on some platforms and

(and various other things about 2.xx) on comp.windows.x.apps and
comp.os.linux.announce, and answered many a question about fvwm on the
former as well where I reference the web site (always stressing that
it is still beta code).
