On Tue, 10 Dec 1996, Randall Cone wrote:

> Hey folks,
> I'm trying to compile fvwm2.0.43, and having trouble.
> I tried xmkmf;./MakeMakefiles
> and within several lines of the compilation status there was:
> "no rule to make target /lib/libfvwm2.a: stop".
> I'm a newbie and have never compiled anything before, how should I
> proceed?

Proceed by telling on what platform you're compiling fvwm and what you've
done before you typed "make" in the dir /bla/fvwm-2.0.43/ (hope you typed
it there and not in the fvwm/ dir, first you need to build the things in
lib/). _Normally_ fvwm2 compiles straight Out Of The Box.

> Also, how do I check what version is currently running while in X
> window?

Depending on what version of fvwm (1 or 2) you're running either type in
an xterm "fvwm -version" of "fvwm2 -version".
