It's similar to something we have on our motion controllers called
"softkeys". On the far left is an up arrow to go back up a level, and
on the far right is a right arrow to display more keys on the same
level. Once a key is pressed, a new set of keys are displayed.

It might be a little overkill for xterms, but good luck implementing
it. :)
