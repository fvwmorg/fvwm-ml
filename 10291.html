Hi all,

A new version of FvwmTabs is available.

You can get it at:

http://users.tpg.com.au/users/scottie7/fvwmtabs.html

ChangeLog:
- Fixed annoying focusing race condition. (FvwmTabs would often steal
  focus when mouse was merely passing over a tab-manager to another window.)
- Bugfix: invoking NextTab/PrevTab in an empty tab-manager caused crash.
- Make FvwmTabs work with older versions of Perl.
- Created man page for FvwmTabs.
- Updates to fvwmtabrc file.

SCoTT. :)
