While reading the fvwm man page that comes with 2.4.5, I spotted
a couple of typos/omissions.

  o In the description of the "XorValue [number]", the type and
     range of acceptable numbers are not specified.

  o In the description of %Space and %Tab ItemFormat options, we
     have a garbled sentence:
               Add a gap as large as one respectively eight
               spaces are with the font used in the menu items.
