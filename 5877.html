On 14 Aug 2002 17:42:50 -0600, Gregg Dameron wrote:
>
> I would like to be able to use a key binding to simulate clicking on
> whichever TaskBar button is under the mouse cursor. Unlike
> FvwmButtons, FvwmTaskBar seems to ignore carriage returns.
> Experiments with FakeClick proved fruitless. Is there a way?
>
> Perhaps "Return" or "Enter" could be added to the list of Action
> qualifiers someday.

You may configure any mouse and key action (with optional modifiers) in
FvwmIconMan in case you want to simulate FvwmTaskBar using FvwmIconMan
swallowed inside FvwmButtons.
