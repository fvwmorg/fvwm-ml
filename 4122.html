might be not that complicated to translate the fvwm2 man pages to
DocBook SGML or DocBook XML, which are the languages used in the LDP.

http://www.linuxdoc.org/LDP/LDP-Author-Guide/index.html
(There was also a good article in the german computer magazine c't
