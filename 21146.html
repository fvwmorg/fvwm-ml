I have a problem with the IconGrid and IconFill styles, I use

Style * IconBox 55 6 314 874, IconGrid 1 96, IconFill Bottom Left

yet the first icons are placed at the left hand edge of the screen (they should
be 55 from the left edge) with a left-edge more than 55 they start to get placed
some distance from the left-edge but are off by about 50-60 pixels. I've looked
at the patch and I can't spot an error but it looks like it's placing the right
hand edge of the icons in the grid. If I change to IconFill Bottom Right it all
works fine.
