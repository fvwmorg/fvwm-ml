On 06 Apr 2002 16:39:05 -0500, Jesse L Farinacci wrote:
>
> i am using fvwm 2.4.5 and am having a problem with the FvwmIconMan. for
> some reason, after a seemingly random amount of time, the icon manager
> seems to stop responding to mouse clicks. i have no valid proof of this,
> but a friend who uses the same config as i do claims that it stops
> responding after multiple netscape's have been initiated.
>
> to reiterate, the problem with the iconman seems only to be in responding
> to mouse clicks. it does respond to mouseover (highlighting), and also
> gets the bevel look when i press it. i've tried mouse clicks with all
> three buttons, still no response.

It should be the same problem that is asked in the FAQ question #0.0.
It stops to work when, say, NumLock is pressed.

Either do what the FAQ suggests or replace the line similar to:

  *FvwmIconMan: *action Mouse 2 N sendcommand "Iconify"

with this one:

  *FvwmIconMan: *action Mouse 2 A sendcommand "Iconify"

"N" means no modifiers, "A" - any modifier.
