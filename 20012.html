Broc Stirton <bstirton_at_onr.com> writes:

> In fvwm v1.2.x, there was an option Opaque Move xx, where xx was a
> percentage of the total screen size. Any windows smaller than this
> would be moved w/out rubberbands. There must be something similar for
> fvwm2.0.x (2.0.45 in my case), but I can find no mention of it. I'd
> like to not use the rubberbands. help?
> TIA,
> Broc

OpaqueMoveSize should do what you want, just give it the value of 100,
ie. stick the following in your .fvwm2rc.

OpaqueMoveSize 100
