The problem discussed here (originally found in 1.24r) is also in recent
versions of fvwm2 in the modules that do CPP or M4 processing of .fvwm2rc
files.

Since this is a generic problem, I'm notifying you guys of it in hopes that
you'll come up with a fix. I don't like the fix proposed by the author,
however it certainly works. I think a better fix would be to fork and
create pipes by hand and use mkstemp to hold temporary information and
avoid the race condition.
