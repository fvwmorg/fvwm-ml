Try:
Key F1 A M Next [* CurrentDesk]Focus

Or even better, if you use a keyboard shortcut for changing desks
(we're talking desktops here, not pages, right?) you could make a
complex function like

AddToFunc "DesknFocus" "I" GotoDesk $0 $1
+ "I" Prev [CurrentDesk] Raise
+ "I" Prev [CurrentDesk] Focus

And bind it appropriately, feeding it two args, the desk x,y...
