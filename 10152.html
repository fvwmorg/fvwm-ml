>
> Well, if you don't want titlebar buttons to enherit all title settings,
> then don't use UseTitleStyle. Separately apply semi-transparency to
> title and to buttons, like:
>
> TitleStyle AllInactive Colorset 1 -- flat
> AddTitleStyle AllInactive Colorset 2 60
>
> ButtonStyle All AllInactive Colorset 1 -- flat
> AddButtonStyle All AllInactive Colorset 2 60
>
> ButtonStyle All AllInactive -- !UseTitleStyle flat
> ButtonStyle All AllActive -- UseTitleStyle flat
>
> If this does not help, then provide all relevant TitleStyle/ButtonStyle/
> Colorset lines. Or better yet, convert your full configuration (or just
> decorations) to fvwm-themes format, so I and hunderds of others may
> trivially load your theme.
