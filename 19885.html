I'm pretty sure I've heard references to there being an archive of list
messages. I am about to mail out my patch to flesh out the M_MINI_ICON packet
(which includes patching modules/FvwmIconMan/fvwm.c, the only other user in
the distributed source base). I am also about to release the first beta of my
Perl 5 module for Fvwm. In it, I now mention that the config item ColorLimit
is contingent upon application of the color-limiting patch, and that the
M_WINDOWSHADE and M_DEWINDOWSHADE packets only return 3 ints if you've applied
my patch for those packets. My next X11::Fvwm release will expect M_MINI_ICON
packets to be patched, as well. I think that it is in my best interest to
include copies of the relevant patches until the Fvwm author has the time to
make the next release (because I *don't* want to be responsible for him feeling
rushed to do so). So I need copies of my windowshade patch, and the color-limit
patch. I would prefer copies of the original messages, so that headers are
intact, given the author of the color-limit patch due credit.

Randy
