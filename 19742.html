Hi,

I'm using Pure Atria's "Purify" software on a Solaris 2.5.1 box. My
main workstation, though, is a DEC Alpha running Digital Unix 4.0b
where I use FVWM 2.0.44 as the window manager.

While this works quite nice, I stumbled accross a weird problem
yesterday: When running the Purify viewer on Solaris and displaying
it on the DEC Alpha running FVWM, the viewer window does *not* get
its window decorations (border, menu button in the upper left corner,
min,max buttons in the upper right corner) drawn so I cannot resize
or move the window.

This does *not* happen with mwm and dtwm on the DEC alpha.

Can anyone give me a clut about how to fix this? *All* of my windows
get their decorations properly. The Purify viewer is the only exception.
