Is this the right place to report a bug?

I'm running fvwm 2.4.6 on Debian (testing/Woody).

Given the following entries:

*FvwmButtons: Geometry +0-0
*FvwmButtons: ButtonGeometry 32x32

I would expect to get buttons sized 32x32 and the button bar to be in the
lower left corner. This does not happen. The button bar is in the upper
left corner. I have to change ButtonGeometry to


*FvwmButtons: ButtonGeometry 32x32+0-0

to get the buttons in the lower left corner. From the documentation, I would
have expected this.
