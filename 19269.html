stephenf_at_sybase.com said:
> I can't even start one xterm which uses colors (e.g. blue on black).
> Get the old "Warning: Cannot allocate colormap entry for "#22eeff"".

Netscape sucks up vast amounts of colors. It WILL fill up a color map
on an 8 bit deep display. And most sun framebuffers are only 8bits deep.
You have X on your Number9 configured to 16bits? Use xdpyinfo to see what
your X display can do.
