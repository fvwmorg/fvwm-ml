 Hi,
         I am not going to provide any solution to your problem. I am
 only posting this mail to report similar problems.
 
 Firstly, I am running fvwm2.x on a Sun Ultra, SunOS 5.5.1 - which is
 your machine ?
         Secondly, in my case too, fvwm does not recognize the commands
 BorderStyle and TitleStyle. But this only happens when i attempt a
 restart. If I quit from fvwm and start it again, it is able to recognize
 BorderStyle and TitleStyle.

 goofed up. It never shows a button bar. So I am forced to run FvwmButtons
 2.0.40 with fvwm2.0.45. Unfortunately, FvwmButtons 2.0.40 does not
 recognize the new syntax (with the parentheses around it) and the
 load of options that comes with it. Hence I am using an old fashioned
 button bar with very few customizable featuers.
