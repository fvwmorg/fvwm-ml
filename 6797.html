On 04 Nov 2002 17:50:55 -0500, Dan Espen wrote:
>
> The same key is also known as Alt_L, but I can't bind an action
> directly to Alt_L, but I think I could if I removed the modifier
> from the key.

You can bind a single modifier press with FVWM. Try this:

  Key Shift_R A A Exec xterm

  #Key Shift_R A A -
