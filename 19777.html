On Tue, 8 Apr 1997 bultman_at_rikz.rws.minvenw.nl wrote:

> Are there at the moment any plans to re-unite the diverging lines
> between fvwm2 and fvwm95.
> As far as I (as a layperson) can tell, the differences are
> fairly superficial, such as the ability to have a 'side-bar'
> in your menus and the fact that the menu is places next to the
> previous menu while cascading, instead of half of top of it.
> Is there a way to simulate this behaviour in fvwm2, are or the
> differences more substantial?
>
> Gert Bultman
> bultman_at_rikz.rws.minvenw.nl
> --

In .fvwm2rc,

"MenuStyle big.long.hardtotype.font.name fvwm" gives the half-
overlapping menu style. Replace 'fvwm' with 'mwm' and you'll
get the next-to/immediate popup behavior of fvwm95.

I found this one by accident, after borrowing a few pieces from
the DecorCD setup mentioned here earlier.
