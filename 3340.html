Cool. FVWM is definitely the best window manager. Some may work better out of
the box - the Windows style of window manager where it does all the thinking for
you and all you do is click and point. But FVWM lets us do it our way.

A friend of mine emailed me a couple of weeks back. He's using Gnome. He was
really irritated because every time he accidentally clicked on the upper left
hand corner of the window it closed it. Not sure why he's clicking up there,
move or resize maybe, but I told him that he won't have that problem with
FVWM. You can configure the corners, buttons, etc., any way you want. FVWM is
the best!
