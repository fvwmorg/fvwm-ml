Hi folks,

in the last few days I received an increasing number of
mails addressed to me and only me. *Please* everyone
don't reply to threads on the list to me directly. I am
*NOT* 'the maintainer' of fvwm2 but merely one of them
and the others are interested in the information too.
Furthermore if you send me mails directly I don't get
them at home where I do the work. And since my allowed
mail volume there is very limited, please don't reply
to my private address. All I will do with such mail is
forwarding it to the list (= more work for me).
If you are unsure if you should post large patches, ask
on the list. In general for everything up to 50k you can
post to the list without thinking twice about it.

Jason, Dan, does anything on the web page suggest that
folks should send mail to me? If not, could we mention
that explicitly (at least for a while)?

Thanks

Dominik
