Near 2000, Os mumbled something about:
>
> I'd like to be able to use the SGI desktop icons aswell. However there's
> a problem in that the icons get the left and middle button clicks, but
> the right button is only handled by the window manager, whereas I want
> the icons to get the right clicks if the mouse is over one of them.

Are you sure you haven't got a function defined for the right mouse button
when the mouse pointer is in the root window ?
That's the only reason I can think of, why the left and middle mouse
buttons work OK, but the right one doesn't.

> Or, failing that, is there a way in fvwm2 to use icons as shortcuts to
> files (as you can under the SGI desktop/Win95 etc.) rather than just as
> iconized windows?

As far as I know, nope. You can't.
But a while ago, somebody posted a path or module that enabled file managing
right under our very fvwm2.
