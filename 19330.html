>>>>> Jason Harrell writes:

J> Hello all!
J> To start, I would like to say that fvwm IS the best window manager in my
J> humble opinion. It's very customizable and looks alot like mwm. Now to
J> the point. I just want to know two things:

J> 1. How can I set/change the background color/picture from that dull gray
J> and black checker crap?

J> 2. Where can I find cool pixmaps for fvwm?

J> Thanks for the help in advance!

xsetroot -solid 'color'

but how about a monet in the root window?

I use

DestroyFunc NewBackground
AddToFunc NewBackground "I" exec nice "xv +noresetroot -root -random
 -noqcheck -ncols 100 -max -smooth -quit ~/public_html/Pictures/*
~/public_html/Pictures/hubble/* ~/public_html/Pictures/*/*/*"

all in one line.

this unpacks any jpeg, gif or other picture, expands it to screen size
installs it to the root window and quits.

this is REAL WORK for my workstation.
The picture appears after some time, but 'nice' takes care I can fully use
my WS inbetween.
