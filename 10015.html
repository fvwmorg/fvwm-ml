On Mon, Aug 11, 2003 at 10:38:20PM +0200, Roschi Thomas wrote:
> Hello
>
> i've a problem with my Iconify-Functions:
> I open a window (Mozilla Mail exactily). The following Line in my
> .fvwm2rc file should move the icon on the right site of the screen:
>
> Style Mozilla IconBox -1 415 -1 880
> Style *Mozilla* IconBox -1 415 -1 880
>
> This works properly for every Brows-window. But for the mailer it
> doesn't work.
> After a restart of the fvwm2 the mailer also works fine.
>
> Anyone have help for me?

I think mozilla sets the title of the mail window only after it
has bee mapped. Initially, it comes up with

  Name: "Mail & Newsgroups"
  Resource: "Mozilla"
  Class: "mail:3pane"

Try

  Style Mail* ...

or something similar.
