Amos Tibaldi <tibaldi_at_CE.UniPR.IT> writes:
> Hello, there.
>
> I am a newbie in configuring X.
> I would like to change the image of the pointer when it is off of windows.
> How can I obtain this result?

Look up "CursorStyle" in the fvwm2 man page.

You want something like:

CursorStyle Root Gumby

> Am I obliged to give a mask to the system in order to specify the position
> of the hot spot or not? What are the conventions regarding the position of
> it in terms of image coordinates?

The cursors that come with X already have hot spots.
Otherwise hot spots are optional. (Check the man page.)
