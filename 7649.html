On Fri, Jan 17, 2003 at 08:54:07AM -0000, terry_z wrote:
> hello.
>
> I have add the config in /etc/X11/fvwm2/system.fvwm2rc
> It make the different color in main menu. thank you very much.
>
> But if I open the program in fvwm2. example kmail or kword.
> The menu of the program (kmail or kword) has nothing change.
> Then how can I change the config of /etc/X11/fvwm2/system.fvwm2rc, change the color of the button when the mouse active of it in (kmail or kword)?
> I know it can in KDE or GNOME. but can't in fvwm.
>

If your app was written with the qt toolkit, you need to change its
widgets with "kcontrol". If it was made with gtk+ you need to use a gtk
theme switcher. Window managers don't have any control over your
toolkits theme.

hopefully this makes sense.
