>>>>> "Jason" == Jason L Tibbitts <tibbs_at_sina.hpc.uh.edu> writes:

    Jason> I have decided not to continue the digest. I do this for several
    Jason> reasons. Firstly, with all of the setup, maintenance, and the slew
    Jason> of bounces I'm likely to get from the initial announcements I have
    Jason> enough to do. Secondly, I think this list has a low enough traffic
    Jason> that a digest version really isn't warranted. Thirdly, I disagree
    Jason> in principle to digests of discussion lists, since the digest format
    Jason> makes it difficult to reply to individual messages and you
    Jason> invariably get problems with someone quoting the entire digest back
    Jason> to the list and such. It's too much headache. Perhaps in the
    Jason> future another person will offer to digest the list for those of you
    Jason> who can't live without it. Hoping to err on the side of
    Jason> completeness, I have added all of those who subscribed to the digest
    Jason> to the normal list.

FYI, Let met tell you that I couldn't agree less with your third point. I'm on
several mailing lists which I (and numerous others) receive in digest form, and
neither are they difficult to reply to, nor have I ever (in the last couple of
years) seen anyone quote an entire digest to a list. (Maybe I didn't look too
well ;-)

It's just a matter of using the right mail readers ...

Thanks for all the effort though.
