On Wed, 24 Apr 1996, Richard A. Guay wrote:
>
> I know what I am asking is not a topic for this list, but I do not know
> where else to go. I need to get a precompiled version of GNU gcc and
> binutils for the Sparc Solaris 2.5 Operating System (Solaris does not come
> with it's own compiler - you have to get it extra!). Can anyone out there
> give me a hand? Thanks in advance.
>

GNU/FSF sites have a precompiled binary of the gcc compiler for Solaris.
I've used it to rebuild the compiler under Solaris 2.3....I would suppose
it will also work under 2.5. The binaries can be found at the following
sites:

ftp://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/GNU/sparc-sun-solaris2/gcc-binaries-2.5.6.tar.gz
ftp://prep.ai.mit.edu/pub/gnu/......

You also might need the gzip binaries found there. I believe the
instructions that come with this package will tell you what you need to
do to re-compile the latest compiler (gcc-2.7.2.tar.gz). I did not
require anything additional out of binutils....everything else I needed
was installed with Solaris.
