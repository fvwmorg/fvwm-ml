On Wed, 8 May 1996, Greg Caddell wrote:

> I currently have a 2 button mouse which works adequately when outside any
> window, but I really need to be able to cut text from one window and be
> able to paste that text somewhere else. Currently the left mouse button
> will hi-lite areas, but of course no pasting. Does anyone have ideas on
> changes I can make to my .fvwmrc file, so that I can either use a right
> mouse button press or hold both buttons to paste while inside a window.

This is not a property of fvwm, but one to do with your X server. Fvwm
can't help.

If you're running XFree86 on Linux or similar, you can specify
"Emulate3Buttons" in your XF86Config file. This makes left + right
clicked together pass a middle click to the application.

Hmm, come to think of it, there's no reason why a similar feature
might not live in fvwm. But then, I've not seen the input parsing
code, so I can;t comment on the easy of integration. :)
