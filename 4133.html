There are a slew of converters available. E.g man2html
allows you to put your systems man pages all in a
browser format with cgi-bin. rman does it individually
but from man to anything. I just tried fvwm -> latex
and html. Does a decent job given the weird characters
that are used. But I still like HyperLatex because it
doesnt just do an automated conversion.
