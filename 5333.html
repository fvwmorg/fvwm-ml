On 17 Jun 2002 01:24:04 -0700, Malte Schuenemann wrote:
>
> Searching for the gdk-imlib I cannot find anything
> that seems to fit here to get GDK Imlib support.
>
> What is that GDK Imlib support for ? I don't know
> whether I could benefit from it or not ...
>
> Where can I find that GDK Imlib component ?

It is a part of Imlib:

  http://rpmfind.net/linux/rpm2html/search.php?query=imlib
  http://rpmfind.net/linux/rpm2html/search.php?query=imlib-devel
  http://www.alltheweb.com/search?cat=ftp&q=imlib-1.9.10.tar.gz

But if you don't use FvwmGtk module with images (say, fvwm-menu-directory
may create such FvwmGtk menus), you don't need to worry about GDK Imlib.
Additionally, Imlib version 1 is replaced with Imlib version 2, and only
the first provides gdk-imlib, I think. Don't use version 2.
