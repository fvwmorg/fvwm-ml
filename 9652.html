> Is anyone else having a problem with Mozilla Firebird 0.6.1 where the icon

(e.g., screen with two depths can cause problem here).

> I figure this is a glitch
> with Firebird, so I added the path to where the Firebird icon was on my
> system (it ships with Firebird) to my ImagePath, and I added the following
> style line:
>
> Style "Mozilla Firebird" Icon mozicon50.xpm
>
> But that didn't work, so I tried:
>
> Style "Mozilla Firebird" IconOverride, Icon mozicon50.xpm
>
> And that didn't work either.
>

I do not see why this does not work. Do you have a msg of the form
"Cannot load mozicon50.xpm" in your X error output? Are you sure that
that "Mozilla Firebird" match your Firebird window (FvwmIdent)?

Can you sent the output of "xprop" on Firebird (open a terminal, type
"xprop > xprop_Firebird", a cross should appear and click on the
Firebird window). If the output is to big send it to me only.
