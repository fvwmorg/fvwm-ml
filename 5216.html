On 26 May 2002 14:30:25 -0000, Paul Redman wrote:
>
> My $MANPATH was set to /usr/share/man:/usr/X11R6/man. So from the
> INSTALL.fvwm file I gathered the pages had been put in
> /usr/local/man/man1... I moved them to usr/share/man/man1, and the
> problem is solved ('man fvwm' works). Cheers for the pointer. Next
> time I'll be sure to read more before diving in, I am kind of used to
> the emerge/ports method of install from gentoo :)

Moving man pages every time you install fvwm is not nice and not needed.
Either update system $MANPATH to include /usr/local/man (a good idea).
Or when installing FVWM do: ./configure --mandir=/usr/share/man
