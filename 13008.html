> It does not seem like there is a function to have a mouse tooltip popup
> over buttons in FvwmButtons. I believe this would be a nice addition to

"ActiveTitle" specifies what text to display on a button when the mouse
hovers over it. Is this what you want?

This option is a recent addition to FvwmButtons so you will require
a CVS snapshot. (or wait until 2.5.11 comes out)
