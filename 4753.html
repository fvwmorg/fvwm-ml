On 29 Mar 2002 17:07:28 -0800, John Smith wrote:
>
> How can you have a different color for a label show up when you've got focus
> over an icon?

I am not sure what do you mean.

If you mean different color when the pointer enters the icon and the label
expands, it is impossible.

If you mean the color of the focused icon, it is the same as the color of
focused window decorations:

  Style * HilightFore black, HilightBack darkcyan # focused
  Style * ForeColor white, BackColor navy # unfocussed
