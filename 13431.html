On 29 Oct 2004 23:42:43 +0200, Nicola Girardi wrote:
>
> I'd like to know how to suppress application-provided icons
> replacing them with my own (simply Style "mywindow" Icon myicon.png
> doesn't seem to work), as I don't seem to be able to grep it out of
> the documentation.

It is in the long list of all Style options:

  Style "mywindow" Icon myicon.png, IconOverride

Specify IconOverride for every window name you need, "*" does not work.
