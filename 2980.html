On 22 Aug 2001 10:33:28 -0700, Ariel Cas wrote:
>
> I'm using a few gnome icons in xpm format for my
> FvwmButton bar. Under gnome the icons look fine but
> when in fvwm the icons are displayed on my button bar
> with a black background. My button bar is grey, so
> the only thing I should see is the icon with a grey
> background. I have a graphics file viewer called
> Xnview, and I can view all my fvwm and gnome icons.
> When I view them via Xnview the fvwm and gnome icons
> are indeed displayed with a black background but when
> placed on the button bar only the fvwm icons show up
> as they are supposed to.
>
> Why does this only happen with gnome icons? They're
> both in xpm format.
>
> Is there something I should add to my .fvwm2rc file?
>
> I'm using fvwm 2.2.4

Original GNOME icons are png, not xpm. I think your converting program
replaced the transparent color with the black one.

You may open your xpm files in an editor and replace "black" or "#000000"
with "None" to restore transparency. Or use a more correct convertor like
GIMP to transform png to xpm. You should get color "None" in xpm.
