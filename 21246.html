On Tue, 4 Nov 1997, Sullivan N. Beck wrote:

: I run the FvwmWinList module and the window list is not touching any of the
: desktop borders (i.e. it's right in the middle of the screen). Every time
: an item is added or removed from the list (i.e. every time a new window is
: created or a window is destroyed), the WinList shifts left one or two pixels.
: After a while, the WinList is overlapping other windows. Kind of annoying.

I had this problem too, and IIRC I fixed it by setting

*FvwmWinListGeometry +0+0

instead of something with a 1 in it. All my FvwmWinList settings are
at http://www./vex.net/~buff/linux.html, and you can compare yours to
mine.
