>>>>> On 27 Feb 1998, Pete Forman <gsez020_at_compo.bedford.waii.com> wrote:

  gsez020> Use WindowList (bound to MS3 on my root window) or FvwmWinList or
  gsez020> FvwmIconMan to jump to the window. Make it sticky and then return
  gsez020> to a desk.

Although I didn't have a default binding in FvwmWinList that would jump to
the window as you suggested above, you nevertheless gave me a idea for how to
solve my problem.

I added the following entries for FvwmWinList in my fvwm configuration:

    *FvwmWinListAction Click1 Iconify,Raise
    *FvwmWinListAction Click2 Move 100p 100p
    *FvwmWinListAction Click3 Module "FvwmIdent" FvwmIdent

The important binding is the one for Click2. This now allows me to bring up
a list of windows through FvwmWinList, then click on mb2 in the list to
immediately move a window to the current viewport screen.

This allowed me to regain my missing window.
