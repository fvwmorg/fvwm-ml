On Tue, 15 Jun 1999, Dominik Vogt wrote:

> On Tue, Jun 15, 1999 at 10:53:50AM -0400, pavese_at_chem.columbia.edu wrote:
> > Hi, All.
> >
> > Quick question: Is there any way to tie an action to clicking on a
> > swallowed app in FvwmButtons?
>
> No. Older versions of the man page simply lie about it.
>
> > I tried tying an action to a swallowed
> > xload, but clicking on it seems to do nothing. I guess what is happening
> > is that the click is getting passed on to the running app & not the button
> > bar itself.

All I managed to do is launch an application from an "xload button" by
selecting fwvm buttons and pressing the Enter key. I don't know it I'm
corect, but xload handles mouse click's itself while it does not handle
keypresses, so thay can be handled by fvwm.

Here is the code from my .fvwm2rc:

*FvwmButtons(3x2, Swallow XLoad 'Exec xload -bg \\\#667788 -nolabel &', \
             Action "Exec xosview &")

Have fun,
alex.
