%% Hyper Faze <fr0sbyte_at_yahoo.com> writes:

  hf> This might be a dumb question but here it goes.
  hf> I'm trying to get a bigger button bar on my desktop
  hf> but I can't seem to find what settings I need to
  hf> change in my .fvwm2rc file to get a bigger button bar.

The FVWM button bar is created by the FvwmButtons module, so you want to
look at the FvwmButtons man page for information on configuring it.

However, FvwmButtons sizes the button bar to be as large as is needed by
the buttons it contains, including their pixmaps and labels (plus some
border stuff, etc.)

What do you mean by "bigger button bar"? You mean, you want the buttons
to be bigger? Or to have space between them? Or the pictures to be
larger? Or what?
