> Btw, I keep getting an error in my fvwm2 log saying "Bad style
> option: NoBorder", when I look this up in the fvwm2 man page, it
> says it was part of 1.xx.

  Style * HandleWidth 0, BorderWidth 0

> I'm also a bit confused by the
> difference between handles and borders. I suppose handles are
> just the parts around the window that let you do some
> move/resize/user config thing? And borders are around the
> handles? Please explain.

The handles are indeed the invisible windows that can be grabbed
with the pointer to resize the window. The terminology is a bit
confusing. The BorderWidth and HandleWidth styles both control
the size of the relief around the window. HandleWidth is used if
the window has handles and the BorderWidth otherwise.
