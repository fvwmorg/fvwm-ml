> I got a piece of code some time ago to toggle the title bar on and
> off. It worked with 2.5.11 but now is not working with the snapshot

I'm not sure whether this information is correct. As far as I
know, combining styles and states broke on 2nd of April (after the
2.5.10 release) and was fixed on 3rd of October (after the 2.5.11
release):

 2.5.10 : ok
 April 1 and earlier : ok
 April 2 : depends on time of snapshot
 April 3 : broken
 August 3 : broken
 2.5.11 : broken
 October 2 : broken
 October 3 : depends on time of snapshot
 October 4 and later : ok
 2.5.12 : ok

> bug/change in fvwm.
>
> The code:
>
> DestroyFunc title-bar-toggle
> AddToFunc title-bar-toggle
