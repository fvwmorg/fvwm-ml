On Wed, Feb 09, 2005 at 04:35:27PM +0000, Sebastian Rodriguez wrote:
> I rewritte to the mailing list cause now i've a little problem :p
> How can i do to dont put borders or anyting else to the xine bar? the
> one you can make pause/play etc, cause now it has a border, and its
> quite annoying :'(

Style "*xine*" !Title, !Handles. !Borders

Note that you may well have to use FvwmIdent on the xine bar to
determine its name/class/resource, or what have you, if the above
matches too many instances of windows named 'xine'.

HTH,
