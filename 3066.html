On Fri, 31 Aug 2001, Subba Rao wrote:

> I am ruuning FVWM2 version 2.4.0 on Slackware 8. I have started
> the GNOME panel in FVWM. One of the features of the panel is that
> it has a menu style tool bar along with the large panel bar at the
> bottom of the screen. The menu style bar (I don't know what it is
> called) at the top is positioned a little low on the physical
> screen. How do I move this mene style bar to the top of the
> screen?

This is a gnome issue, not an fvwm one. Right-click on the panel and
go to panel, properties, type. If it's not an "edge panel", make it
one or alternatively if it's a "floating panel" go to "All properties"
and adjust its geometry.

Allin Cottrell.
