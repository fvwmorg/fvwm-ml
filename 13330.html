On 04 Oct 2004 10:42:24 +0300, Mikko Heiskanen wrote:
>
> Is there a way to see if fvwm is built with gnome support? I tried
> "fvwm-config -i" but I don't know if it should show in there.
> It does say on gnome-libs: no, but is it the same as
> --enable-gnome-hints and --with-gnome?

--enable-gnome-hints is obsolete; gnome hints are compiled in
unconditionally and may be turned off in the config if needed.

--with-gnome is related to the line you see in 'fvwm-config -i':
        gnome-libs (in FvwmGtk): yes

As you see, gnome-libs only affects FvwmGtk module and nothing else.

> I am running 2.5.10 from FreeBSD ports, and fvwm/gnome
> are behaving weird together (new programs start on page 1,
> iconified windows show on nautilus desktop, etc.), even though
> I have all ewmh* style options turned on.

I think there were discussions on this list about Nautilus recently.
You may try turning hints off rather than on (that is the default).
