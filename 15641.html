esn> I'm trying to do this now.

esn> xmodmap -ev "keycode 115 = 0xFFEA 0xFFBE"

esn> Anyone have any ideas?

Yes, use xkeycaps(1) by Jamie Zawinski. It cures hair loss caused by
xmodmap.

Xkeycaps(1) gives you a simple GUI to xmodmap that lets you fiddle
with your keyboard to your hearts content.

If you don't have it on your system, I heartily recommend getting it
from

        ftp://ftp.x.org/contrib/applications/xkeycaps-2.29.tar.Z

or one of its mirrors, or from the wizard himself:

        http://home.netscape.com/people/jwz/xkeycaps/index.html
