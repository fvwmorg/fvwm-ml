"Viren R. Shah" <viren_at_rstcorp.com> writes:

> I've a Solaris 2.5.1 box with CDE installed, I want to get fvwm as the
> default window manager on it, when I login form the CDE login
> screen. Does anyone have any pointers as to how this can be achieved?

This works really slick:

in your ~/.Xdefaults file put:

*wmStartupCommand: /path/to/fvwm/executable/fvwm2
*waitWmTimeout: 1

Then when you log in, this will automatically fvwm2 as your window
manager.
