I realize it's not what you want, but for the specific case of
Netscape, I just make it ClickFocus (one of the best features of fvwm
is per application focus policy). ClickFocus does the right thing
with dialog windows. Since Netscape is pretty useless without the
mouse anyway, having to click to get it focused initially isn't too
much trouble (and if you keep it on its own desk with no other (mouse
or sloppyfocus) windows it works quite nicely). At the same time, if
a solution that got dialog boxes to focus properly with mouse or
sloppyfocus came along, I'd be an avid user of it. Unfortunately X
programming isn't my forte.
