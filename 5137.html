On 19 May 2002 20:16:21 +0200, Marcus Lundblad wrote:
>
> I run fvwm 2.5.1
> My pager always has the window title "Desk 0", regardless of desk.
> (the pager is stucky).
> But the icon title changes depending on the desk.
>
> Before the title was always "FvwmPager", but I like the idea to have the
> title reflect the current desk.
> Is this a behaviour that is changing in the beta version?

This bahaviour was not changed in 2.5.x.

But you may have any title you want, even change it dynamically:

  DesktopName 0 "<0>"

> The relevant part of the config looks like this (generated by a script):
>
> *FvwmPager: Font none
> *FvwmPager: Geometry +0+0
> *FvwmPager: DeskTopScale 24
> *FvwmPager: DeskPixmap 0 /home/marcus/.fvwm/backdrops/0
> *FvwmPager: DeskPixmap 1 /home/marcus/.fvwm/backdrops/1
> *FvwmPager: DeskPixmap 2 /home/marcus/.fvwm/backdrops/2
> *FvwmPager: DeskPixmap 3 /home/marcus/.fvwm/backdrops/3
> *FvwmPager: NoDeskHilight
> *FvwmPager: SmallFont 5x8
> *FvwmPager: Balloons
> *FvwmPager: BalloonFore black
> *FvwmPager: BalloonBack rgb:ff/ff/9c

This configuration does not affect FvwmPager labels.

I think you launch FvwmPager without arguments and it defaults to "0 0",
i.e. shows one Desk 0. Run it as "FvwmPager * *" instead if you want it
to show the current desk.

I once wanted to change the default to "* *", but Dominik argued some
folks may depend on the current default. Hmm, just tested, FvwmPager in
2.2.x does not have any default, it refuses to start without 2 arguments.
I think I will change the default to the everyone's benefit.
