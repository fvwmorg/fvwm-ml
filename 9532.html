On Thu, Jul 24, 2003 at 11:31:17AM +0200, f.kater_at_gmx.net wrote:
> Hi,
> I recognized that under special circumstances the menu items are not
> highlighted:
>
> To produce this case:
> - click on default button / "start" button (in TaskBar)
> - select a menu item in the menu via one click...
> - ... to open a submenu (must be present)
> - then move the mouse within the sub menu items (highlighting is ok)
> - then move the mouse to a sub sub menu (must have one): here
> highlighting is not done.
>
> I just wanted to report that.
> I am using the snapshot from 2003-07-23.

That's a feature called "posting" menus. By clicking on a submenu
item, fvwm is instructed to ignore mouse motion outside this sub
menu.
