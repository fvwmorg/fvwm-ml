For what it's worth, here's what I did 2 weeks ago...

AddToFunc MyWarp I Raise
                + I WarpToWindow 50 50

Key Tab A M Next (AcceptsFocus !Iconic) MyWarp

Explaination: instead of the windowlist, I can now navigate through all
non iconified apps on the current desk with Alt-Tab (I'm in follow focus).
The neat part is the warptowindow to be at the center of the window.

Now the cool part: when I'm prompted for a passwd in mozilla for example,
a simple Alt-Tab immediately warps to the latest created window. No mouse
needed ;)
