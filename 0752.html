On Fri, May 05, 2000 at 01:54:55PM -0700, Geoff Ludwiczak wrote:
> Hey,
>
> Is it possible to have sub-menus popup adjacent to the parent menu?
> instead of overlaping...

Yes. The MenuStyle command allows complete control
over look and feel of menus. Try out

  MenuStyle * mwm

to switch to mwm like menus, or

  MenuStyle * PopupOffset -3 100

To change only the placement of popup menus. Both work
with any release from the 2.2.x series or later. If you
still use 2.0.x or earlier, you have to use the old syntax
of the MenuStyle command (see man page) and use 'mwm'
instead of 'fvwm'.
