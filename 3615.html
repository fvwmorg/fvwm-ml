Mikhael Goikhman wrote:
>
> On 11 Nov 2001 04:14:48 -0800, Erik Steffl wrote:
> >
> > is there any way to find out which styles are applied to a window?
> > e.g. I am trying lenience style with vncviewer but how do I know it was
> > actually applied (what if I have the name wrong or something...)?
>
> You can't get information whether a specific Style line was applied for a
> specific window or not. But you can get information about the result.
>
> Run FvwmCommandS module (from FvwmConsole or in your StartFunction).
> Then to get an internal fvwm info about your window, start it using:
>
> FvwmCommand -i2 'Exec exec xcalc'
>
> Note, that window flags is not the same as window styles; flags are
> constructed from all matched Style lines (order matters) and flag names
> often differ from the corresponding Style options.
