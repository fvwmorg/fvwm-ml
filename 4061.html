>
> On Wed, 23 Jan 2002 12:42:33 +0100
> Dominik Vogt <fvwm_at_fvwm.org> wrote:
>
> DV> On Wed, Jan 23, 2002 at 02:38:00PM +0300, Anton Kazennikov wrote:
> >> I've got some troubles with menu style. Can I make that menus are
> >> poped down only on a keypress, not on mouse motion?
>
> DV> Can you please describe exactly what your problem is?
>
> When I move mouse cursor through the menu and the cursor is on triangle,
> a submenu pops up. I want to popup submenus only on a mouse click. But
> they are popped up also on mouse move to the triangle. Can I do that?

I don't think you may currently change this behaviour, 25% of a menu item
at the right near the triangle is always "popup immediately or after
popdown delay if another submenu is up".

If you absolutely want it to be activated only on mouse click or key, you
may use Menu keyword instead of Popup. But there are "side" effects, no
triangle, your parent menu is closed and (by default) position is under
cursor. Maybe this is what you prefer anyway?
