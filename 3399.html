> Thanks for any help you can provide including pointing me to a sample
> .fvwm2rc file from which I could lift the required commands to lift a
> window up to its titlebar.

Here's what I use so that double-clicking the titlebar shades the window:

Mouse 1 T N Function TitlebarLeftClick

AddToFunc TitlebarLeftClick
   + I Raise
   + M Move
   + D WindowShade
