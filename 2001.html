> Glen Lee Edwards <glenlee_at_perluser.net> writes:
> > Is there a way to set a short delay on SloppyFocus before it brings a different

>
> There is no way to do this that I'm aware of.
>
> Either bind the menu to a key, or bind the menu to mouse button over

> mouse bindings, you can use the motion or double click modifiers.
