I have been reading through the more obscure modules now that I am
familiar with Fvwm2 and was impressed with FvwmForm.
Its a pity that I can't think of anything more useful than a logout
button! Does anyone have any particularly useful, "Didn't know I
needed one until I had it" applications for this?

After reading about FvwmPrompt however I feel that it is just a subset
of FvwmForm and could probably be dropped from the release without too
much bother, its very easy to duplicate that functionality in FvwmForm
and there is no real need for a whole separate module. Or am I missing
something?

Its useful enough but there is a richer module doing the same thing
released with it. YMMV.

And a stupid question : when does FVWM become non-beta? Is it 'real
soon now' or actually not far off?
