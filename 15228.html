>
> I just switched to fvwm 2.0.38 and I've been trying to get the
> FvwmIconBox to work. Everytime I start it up I all I get is the icon box
> but no icons in it. Is there something I'm doing wrong??
> this is my module definition for the iconbox.
>

You need to set, in the main part of your .fvwm2rc, all apps to have
no icon. ie:

Style "*" NoIcon

FvwmIconBox will only have icons for apps that have no desktop icon.
