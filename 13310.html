On Wed, Sep 29, 2004 at 05:22:23PM +0530, Subbiah wrote:
> I am having fvwm 2.4.3 and enabled autohide for my Taskbar on win95 style.

Please consider upgrading to 2.4.18. It has an awful lot of
bugfixes.

> If my mouse is placed on the last three pixel bottom of the screen, then my
> taskbar is coming up and going down continuously. How to solve this?

The Auto... features of FvwmTaskBar are broken beyond repair. I
suggest you disable both, the AutoHide and AutoStick options, make
the taskbar sticky:

  Style FvwmTaskBar Sticky

FAQ entry 7.17 describes how to get autohiding through FvwmEvent
for any window, not just FvwmTaskBar. However, you need 2.5.8 or
later for that.
