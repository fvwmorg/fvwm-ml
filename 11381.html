On Sat, 21 Feb 2004 22:22:33 +0000 Mikhael Goikhman wrote:
> PipeRead 'echo SetEnv SOME_PATH $SOME_PATH:/usr/lib'

Thanks for this nice and simple solution. I wrapped it into a function
(took some time, though, until I had the quoting correct):

    DestroyFunc AppendToEnv
    AddToFunc AppendToEnv
    + I PipeRead 'echo SetEnv $0 $$''$0:$1'
