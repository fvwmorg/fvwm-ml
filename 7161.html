On 04 Dec 2002 19:41:50 +0100, Jason Friedrich wrote:
>
> i am a little bit puzzled what *bar i should take. I search a bar
> which contains a TaskList (Like the FvwmTaskBar) and display my
> desktops and pages.
> At the moment i use FvwmPager and FvwmTaskBar seperately but in
> my opinion it takes too much space on my desktop :).
>
> Any ideas and/or configuration hints?

Several ideas. You may use transient pager to save your desktop space.
Here are bindings from fvwm-themes (press Ctrl-Mouse2 on the root):

  Mouse 2 R C Module FvwmPager -transient FvwmPager-Single * *
  Mouse 2 R SC Module FvwmPager -transient FvwmPager-Desker 0 3

You may also have FvwmPager to be an expanding/collapsing subpanel of
some FvwmButtons panel.

Another way is to swallow both FvwmPager and FvwmIconMan in FvwmButtons.

There are examples of all of these in fvwm-themes.

In my opinion FvwmPager is too valuable on a busy desktop to be hidden.
Try also something like this for a windows sorting in 2.5.4+:

  Mouse 2 A SM WindowList SelectOnRelease "" SortByResource, NoDeskSort, NoCurrentDeskTitle, NoGeometry, NoHotkeys
