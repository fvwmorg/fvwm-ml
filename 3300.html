> > Is it possible with fvwm2 to give icons normal window
> > decorations (a border) and a fixed size (where the
> > icon image is then placed in the center of the icon
> > window), like in Motif mwm?

The icon in FVWM is drawn with a 3d border a-la motif if the pixmap is
not transparent (i.e. has no color "None"). You can't specify a spacing.

> I hate those floating
> icons of different sizes and shape swimming on my desktop.

Then you may use wm-icons - themable distribution that currently includes
17 one-sized icon sets, each set implements the list of 84 names.
