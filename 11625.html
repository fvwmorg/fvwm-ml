On 05 Mar 2004 12:23:25 +0800, Song Zhiwei wrote:
>
> How to set the Height of the FvwmTaskBar ?

Currently you can't directly control the height of FvwmTaskBar, it is
calculated automatically to fit the content. However you may indirectly
control it by changing the font size, for example, try:

  *FvwmTaskBar: Font 12x24
