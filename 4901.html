Francis Litterio <franl_at_world.std.com> writes:
> First, kudos to the FVWM developers! This is the coolest and most
> powerful WM I've ever used.

No argument from this quarter.

> I'm trying to configure FvwmButtons to have a title bar without the
> title string in it. I'm wondering if there's anything analagous to
> NoButton but which makes FVWM omit the text from a window's title bar?

No, I don't see any obvious way to create a blank title.

I'd guess most people run FvwmButtons with no title at all:

Style FvwmButtons NoTitle

I suppose you could try using a very short name, maybe an underscore
or period, or setting the foreground and the background to the same
color, but I think you'll be happiest with NoTitle.
