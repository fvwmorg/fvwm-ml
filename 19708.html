On Sat, 29 Mar 1997, Rasmus Tamstorf wrote:

> Hi,
>
> I have just compiled my version of fvwm 2.0.45c using the CDE look-a-like
> announced earlier this week. However, when I resize my windows I do
> not get any rubberband to indicate how large or small they will be.
> Likewise if I have opaque move turned off, no outline of the window is
> shown when I move it, so it is pretty much guess-work to place it
> correctly. How can that be, and what can I do about it ?
>
> Rasmus
>

It's not the fault of my extra BTW :)
You have to set the XORValue option in your fvwm2rc. I noticed that
the disappearing rubberband happens when you use a 16-bit or 24-bit
display. Set XORValue 255 and you should get your rubber bands back.
