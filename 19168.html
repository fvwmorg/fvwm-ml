On Wed, 5 Feb 1997, Kendrick Vargas wrote:

> I guess for ActiveDown, if you want Sunk, you have to put Raised, and vice
> versa...

This is not a bug and is desired behaviour. It is described here in a man
page excerpt:

 There is an important note for the ActiveDown state. When a button
 is pressed, the relief is inverted. Because of this, to obtain a
 sunken ActiveDown state you must specify the opposite of the
 desired relief (i.e. to obtain a pressed-in look which is raised,
 specify Sunk for ActiveDown). This behavior is consistent, but may
 seem confusing at first.
