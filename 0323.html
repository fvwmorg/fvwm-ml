>
> Hello,
>
> how can I remove the `Minimize' `Maximize' `Destroy' buttons
> from the window's decorations ?

You can either remove all lines that bind keys or mouse buttons
to these title buttons ('Key' and 'Mouse' command), or you can
disable them explicitly thie the NoButton style, e.g.

  Style * NoButton 1, NoButton 2, NoButton 4
