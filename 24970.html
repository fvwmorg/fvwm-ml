I am running FVWM 2.2 on RedHat 6.0. Because of RedHat's changes to the fvwmrc
scripts, my user file is actually called .fvwm2rc.init and also most of the man
stuff on fvwm doesn't apply.
I have to say:
exec command
for each thing I want to do in .fvwm2rc.init. Maybe it helps, if you try this,
or try putting &s after each command like:
command1 &
command2 &
command3
Hopefully, one of them might do the trick...
