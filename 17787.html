kendall shaw wrote:
> But the Pager is then giant. So, I made this change:
>
> *FvwmPagerGeometry 120x58-1-1

You must not specify the offsets. Try

*FvwmPagerGeometry 120x58

X geometry works as follows:
(size in x direction)x(size in y direction)+/-(offset from right or left
border)+/-(offset from top or bottom)

The pager just happens to be in the correct place, because your buttons
also specify the same x/y offsets. Leaving the offsets out will
(hopefully :-) do the trick.
