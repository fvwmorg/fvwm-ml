> On Wed, Aug 04, 1999 at 09:57:40PM -0500, nathan.froyd_at_rose-hulman.edu wrote:
> > Dominik Vogt writes:
> Since fvwm-2.3.1 there is the IgnoreModifiers command. Perhaps playing
> with it may help you (e.g. ignoring no modifiers or only the num-lock
> key). If you don't use the num-lock modifier you could switch it off
> completely (in X). I have written a description of what to do in the
> FAQ.

  I have:

xmodmap: up to 3 keys per modifier, (keycodes in parentheses):

shift Shift_L (0x6a), Shift_R (0x75)
lock Caps_Lock (0x16)
control Control_L (0x53), Control_R (0x7e)
mod1 Meta_L (0x7f), Meta_R (0x81)
mod2 Mode_switch (0x14)
mod3 Num_Lock (0x69)
mod4 Alt_L (0x1a)
mod5 F13 (0x20), F18 (0x50), F20 (0x68)


  Note NumLock on mod3. My fvwm2rc contains:

IgnoreModifiers L3

  with:

Key L5 WTSFI N RaiseLower # <Front>

  But under the 2.3.6 snap I'm using, <Font> only works with NumLock *
CapsLock off ...
