Hi all,

sorry if this is a known and simple problem, but I couldn't solve it
after reading man, faq, etc.

The problem is that my pager always have the same geometry no matter
how many desktops I have. I have to mention that I have not specified
a fixed geometry for it :-). (Hold on! The relevant settings are
included at the end of this message.)

What I expect is that a pager with 3x2 desktops is 50% wider than a
pager with 2x2 desktops. Seems that FvwmPagerDeskTopScale is not
respected.

Here go the relevant parts of my "~/.fvwm2rc":

######################################################################
### The virtual desktop
## DeskTopSize 2x2
DeskTopSize 3x2
EdgeResistance 750 50
EdgeScroll 100 100
######################################################################

######################################################################
### The pager
*FvwmPagerBack #908090
*FvwmPagerFore #484048
*FvwmPagerFont -adobe-helvetica-bold-r-*-*-10-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
*FvwmPagerHilight #cab3ca
*FvwmPagerGeometry +0-0
*FvwmPagerLabel 0 Fvwm
*FvwmPagerDeskTopScale 50
*FvwmPagerSmallFont 5x8
######################################################################


Thanks for your help.
