>>>>> laurent_at_Grafnetix.COM (Laurent Duperval) writes:

> One of the neat things I could do with olvwm was to move windows from on
> section to the "pager" (in fvwm-linguo) to another by selecting that
> window in the pager and moving it elsewhere. Is there something similar
> in fvwm?

>From the FvwmPager man page:

     Dragging mouse button 2 on a miniature view of a window will
     cause that window to be move to the location where you
     release the mouse button, but your viewport will not change.
     If you drag the window out of the pager and onto your desk-
     top, a full size image of the window will appear for you to
     place. There is no way to pick up a full size image of the
     window and move it into the pager, however. Since some mice
     do not have button 2, I have made provisions to drag windows
     in the pager by using pressing modifier-1 (usually Alt) and
     dragging with button 3.

Hope this helps,
