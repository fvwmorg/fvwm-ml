Staffan Hämälä <sh_at_dc.luth.se> writes:

> On my fvwm2 setup (2.2.2), I'm using an FvwmButtons bar at the
> bottom of the screen and would like to get my maximizefunctions
> not to place windows over the bar.
> It works if I do "Maximize 100 842p" on this particular machine,
> but I would like to do something that works on all machines
> I work on.
> For example, "Maximize 100 -80p" could maximize windows depending
> on the current screen height, minus 80 pixels from the bottom.
> Sadly however, that doesn't work at all.
> Is there a way to do a work-around for this, or something?

Have you tried something like

Maximize 100 90

Which will maximize to 90% of the height of the screen. Admittedly it
won't give perfect results all the time but it should work nicely in
most cases.
