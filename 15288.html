I recently upgraded from pl3x to pl40. Everything appears to work
fine except for the following minor display bug. I saw this bug
before in 1.2x or an early 2.0, but it was fixed. Now it's back.

Open any Openwindows app. Move the mouse pointer slowly from top to
bottom across the title bar of the app. As the pointer crosses the
boundary between the top frame and the title bar, the whole frame
flickers very briefly from the active to inactive back to the active
color. In fact moving the mouse across any part of the inner part
frame border causes this very brief flashing.

Unless you're paying attention, you probably won't notice it because
it happens very quickly.
