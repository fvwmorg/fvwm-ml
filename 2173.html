Andre Bonhote <andre.bonhote_at_linux.ch> writes:
> hi list
>
> i would like to have my icons lined up in a box right next to my
> FvwmButtons-column at the right window border. i opened an xterm,
> moved and resized it and used "FvwmIdent" to show its coordinates.
> now i put all this in my fvwm2rc, using these coordinates:
>
> Style * IconBox 20x71+1007+0, IconFill left top

Xterm gives you window sizes in terms of character lines and columns.
You've got a 20x71 pixel icon box.
Use xv to get the geometry instead.
