On Sun, Aug 11, 2002 at 11:14:50PM +0200, Johan Svedberg wrote:
> Hi!
>
> I'm using FVWM on alot of diffrent clients and some of them have
> diffrent screenresulutions.
>
> My question is this; is it possible to get a FvwmButton-bar to automaticly
> adjust to the screens vertical size (100%)?

With 2.4.0 or later:

  *FvwmButtonsGeometry $[vp.width]x57+-2-0

Replace the -2 with the width of the decorations.
