Subba Rao <subb3_at_attglobal.net> writes:
>
> Ok. This may be too basic. I have the following messages in my log file.
>
> [FVWM][executeModule]: <<ERROR>> No such module 'FontPath' in ModulePath '/us
> r/libexec/fvw
> m/2.2.4'
> [FVWM][executeModule]: <<ERROR>> No such module 'FontPath' in ModulePath '/us
> r/libexec/fvw
> m/2.2.4'
>
> Does this mean the "fonts" directory has to have a symbolic link in this dire
> ctory?
> The "FontPath" value is different from the one in the above message. In the "
> configure" script,
> I have not seen the fontpath option. What is the significance of this message
> and how can I
> fix this?

The simple answer is, find the line starting with the word "FontPath"
in your .fvwm2rc and remove it.

Here's a more complete answer:

When fvwm encounters a command that it doesn't recogize, it trys to
find a "module" to execute with that name. When it doesn't find a
module with that name, it issues the above message.

Its really trying to say that "FontPath" is not a valid command,
AND there is no module with that name.
