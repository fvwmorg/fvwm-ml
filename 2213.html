On Tue, 6 Mar 2001, Dan Espen wrote:

> Ulrich Fahrenberg <uli_at_math.auc.dk> writes:
> > Hello,
> >
> > I need some kind of semi-sticky behaviour: I want a window to follow
> > me when I'm on Desk1, but when I switch to Desk0 I want it to stay on
> > Desk1, until I switch back to Desk1 -- then it should follow me again.
> >
> > I'm running 2.2.4 on SunOS5.7, and I guess the above behaviour can be
> > achieved with FvwmEvent, I'm gonna work on this. But maybe this is an
> > enhancement request:
>
> I think you will admit that this is a very strange requirement.

Well, yes. But the desktop-dependency of stickyness is something that
makes sense to me: I use different desktops for different projects, so
what I want sticky on Desk0 is different from what I want sticky on
Desk1. So I thought a style

StickyOnDesk <num>

would make sense, and then why not extend this to pages, too -- after
all it is the _flexibility_ of Fvwm we enjoy...

So yes, I guess the StickyOnDesk/Page thingy is pretty strange, but
StickyOnDesk is something I like.
