Cameron Simpson wrote:
> On 12:19 17 Dec 2002, Ben Sferrazza <bsferraz_at_lsil.com> wrote:
> | I've previously written about this problem, but I have yet to solve it.
> | I cannot get FVWM to properly display XPM pixmaps at their original
> | color depth (they appear to be at most 8-bit). [...snip...]
>
> There are XPMs with more than 8-bit colour?
> I'm not trying to discard your actual problem, but the above is news to me.

Almost all XPMs specify 24 bit color (#rrggbb), and it seems that the
version of libxpm that I have installed (XFree 4.2) has no problem
parsing 48 bit color (#rrrrggggbbbb) either. If you're talking about
the number of distinct colors in an xpm file, xpm allows for more than
one character as a color specifier (e.g.: "m+ c #327E58".)

I threw a small example at http://themuffin.net/wastesedge_32x32.xpm
(since I don't want to send a 6kb attachment to everyone on the list ;)
