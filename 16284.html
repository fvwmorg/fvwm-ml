So, have you a patch for the other buglet :-) ? Trim it so:

AddToMenu "Window" "Move%mini.move.xpm%" Move
+ "Resize%jresize.xpm%" Resize
+ "Close%mini.excl.xpm%" Close

Mouse 0 1 A PopUp Window

start xcalc (for exemple) and choose close from the titlebutton menu.
The application (xcalc) correctly close, but you have got a dot cursor
and you have to click (and ear the beep) before anything else.
