>
> All that information is available in the fvwm2 man page.
>
> Title bars have 3 colors, depending on the state of the window.
>
> AddToDecor MyDecor
> + TitleStyle Centered ActiveUp (Solid cornflowerblue
> -- Raised) \
> Inactive (Solid Navy -- Flat) \
> ActiveDown (Solid dimgrey -- Sunk)
> Style "*" UseDecor MyDecor
>
> The little icon is a miniicon:
>
> Style * MiniIcon iconname
>
> The taskbar will use the same miniicons.
