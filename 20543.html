->Hi,
->
->i have a extra .Fvwm2Buttonsrc configfile.
->how can i change this without restarting fvwm2 ?
->
->--

It depends on how your menus are configured. Most FVWM configs have a
"modules" menu or something similar to start up things like the pager,
FvwmIdent or a buttonbar. If you have one of thesethen simply close
your current button bar, and start a new one. You could also do this
through FvwmTalk.
