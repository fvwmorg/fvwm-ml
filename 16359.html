In fvwm-2.0.41, this style line:

  Style "xbuffy" NoTitle,NoHandles,Sticky,WindowListSkip,RandomPlacement

and this resource:

  *FvwmButtons(2x2) - NULL Swallow "xbuffy" Exec xbuffy -priority 16

would make the transient pop-up windows (i.e., those created by xbuffy
when a mailbox button is pushed) come up undecorated. This fails in
fvwm-2.0.42, i.e., they come up with a titlebar.

Anyone know what could have changed? How can I get the old
(undecorated transient pop-ups) behavior back?
