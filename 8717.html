On 24 Apr 2003 12:39:46 +0200, Klaus Zeitler wrote:
>
> this is fvwm 2.5.5 with fvwm-themes 0.6.2 on SunOS 5.8.
>
> I'm having a heard time getting fvwm-themes to run properly.
> Here are 2 problems that are reported in session error log file:
>
> 1. during startup lots of message like:
> [FVWM][ReadDecorFace]: <<ERROR>> couldn't load pixmap 16x16/empty.xpm
>
> fvwm-config --info says "xpm: no" so maybe this is to be expected

Is this correct for you installation, no xpm

It is not very useful to use fvwm-themes without XPM support.

Can you compile fvwm with XPM support? FAQ or INSTALL.fvwm may help.
Moreover, in the future PNG support will be required too for some themes.

> 2. and (constantly growing) the follwing 2 error messages:
>
> [FVWM][FvwmErrorHandler]: <<ERROR>> *** internal error ***
> [FVWM][FvwmErrorHandler]: <<ERROR>> Request 66, Error 8, EventType: 19
>
> [FVWM][FvwmErrorHandler]: <<ERROR>> *** internal error ***
> [FVWM][FvwmErrorHandler]: <<ERROR>> Request 66, Error 8, EventType: 0
>
>
> about 3500 errors in 20 minutes. Unfortunately I have no idea how to trace
> it down to the culprit.
>
>
> Also when I try to select a menu entry from the root menue, sometimes
> my whole session stalls (3 times today while I was playing with all
> these themes) and I have to kill the session.

If I am correct, the error means "BadDrawable, PolyRectangle".
It may or may not be related to the fact you didn't compile with XPM.
You should not normally get "internal error", but it happens.

You may try running fvwm-2.4.15 and fvwm-themes-0.6.2, but probably you
will get problems without XPM support too.
