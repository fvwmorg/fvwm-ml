I work primarily in fvwm, it's my WM of choice. But, occasionally I have
to switch to olwm for GUI testing. Unfortunately, I have not discovered a
way to gracefully switch back to fvwm. As it stands now, when I exit
olwm, I get logged out, and thereby loose all of the work I had running in
other windows. I've tried killing olwmslave -- this has no apparent
affect. Neither did I see anything in the pulldown menu that looked
useful.
 
Is it possible to switch back to fvwm from olwm? If so, how?
 
Environment: SPARC 20, Solaris 2.4, fvwm 1.24r
 
I have fvwm 2.0.41 available, but haven't switched over to it yet.
