On 29-Nov-96 10:02:04 Lionel Cons wrote:
> > We have a lot of users to convert from fvwm1 to fvwm2. We checked your
> script that is part of fvwm 2.0.43 and we found some "lacks of
> feature".
>
> Do you have a more recent version?

I apologize for not responding sooner; I have been rather busy here.

I do have a more recent version; however, the only change is the addition
of handling for ClickToFocus. Other than that, it is [functionally] identical
to the current version.

Adding the line
 /^ClickToFocus$/ { print "Style \"*\" ClickToFocus"; next; }
to the script just after /^StickyIcons$/ will implement this change.

Other than that, I can't suggest anything. If you're familiar with awk,
you can try to extend the script. What features do you think are needed?
