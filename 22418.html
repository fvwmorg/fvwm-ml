Neil Zanella wrote:

> I have a feeling that fvwm cannot help me select the font for my xterms.
> Am I correct?
> That is, the only way to specify what fonts an xterm will use is to give the xterm/remoteXterm command the right argument.

You can get XTerm menus by holding down the Control Key and pressing buttons 1, 2 or 3.

Button 3 will give you a font selection menu, which you can customize in /usr/X11/lib/X11/app-defaults/XTerm

You can customize 6 fonts I think, plus the default you specified on the command line.

FVWM wouldn't have anything to do with xterm, as far as the system is concerned FVWM is just another program like xterm. It
only handles decorations for the window borders, and modules for other features. It can't normally change features of other
programs I don't think.
