On 18 Oct 2003 12:37:53 +0800, Brian W.Kernighan wrote:
>
> How can I add a Windows like "Star Here" button to my Fvwm2?
>
> Now I have a root menu, and I want to bind it to a button("Start Button"),
> so When I click on it , the Root Menu will display on the screen ,
> just as all
> Windows "Start Here" button do.(It may use fvwmbutton, I guess.)
>
> Is there anybody can help? Thanks in advance!

You may use either FvwmButtons or FvwmTaskBar modules, the second has the
Start button support built in, see the man pages.

Here is the line that gives you the good menu position in 2.5.x
for the task bar configured at the bottom:

  *FvwmTaskBar: StartCommand Popup FvwmMenuRoot rectangle $widthx$height+$left+$top -5p -100m
