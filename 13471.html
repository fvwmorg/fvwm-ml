 --- Ingo Wardinski <ingo_at_gfz-potsdam.de> wrote:
> Hi all,
> I try to configure my fvwm2 in the way, that if I want to get away from
> a specific desk background picture to a colored desk, lets say solid
> gray, then how can I get this done?

I'm not sure what you're asking, but let's assume that you have a 3x3
desktop (nine pages). If you want to make all of them use darkgrey, then
you'd use:

*DestroyModuleConfig FvwmBacker: *
*FvwmBacker: Command(Desk *, Page * *) - solid darkgrey

Ensuring that in your "StartFunction" you have the line:

+ I Module FvwmBacker

Does that answer your question? You can have a combination of pixmaps and
colours for different pages on desks -- see "man FvwmBacker".
