>>>>> On Tue, 9 Dec 1997, Choi, Hyoung-Kee said:

  Choi> The fvwm2 is running on Ultra Sparc 1. I am suffering lack of color.
  Choi> Is it possible to increase the max number of color in SUN machine.
  Choi> How may I increase the color to 2^^16 instead of default 256?

You need to buy hardware for this, I believe. I'm not sure about the
Ultra Light, but for the SPARCstation 4 it was possible to buy
additional video memory for about $300. Assuming that an Ultra is not
worse than an SS4, this should be possible for the Ultra, as well.
You might want to contact your local Sun dealer.

NB: The Ultra 1/170E has got a Creator graphics card which groks true
color. Maybe you can buy such a card for your Ultra?
