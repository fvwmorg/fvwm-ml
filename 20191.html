I built fvwm 2.0.45 for IRIX 6.3. I didn't use gcc. It works fine most
of the time, but often freezes for a few seconds.

Usually, keyboard and mouse freeze up for 5-6 seconds, then for another
5-6 seconds an instant later, and then a third time...then things will
be fine for an hour.

Sometimes, only the mouse freezes up and I can use the keyboard if I
have focus.

Other times, everything freezes up tight and hangs there until I log in
from a remote terminal and restart the X server.

I've applied the X server patches. I also installed the replacement
motherboard SGI shipped me.

Does anyone else have experiences, good or bad, with fvwm under IRIX
6.3? Many thanks...
