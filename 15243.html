On Jan 22, David Kennedy writes:
> Subject: FVWM: Compilation help.
>
> gcc -g -Wall -O -xF -DSYSV -DSVR4 -xF -Wa,-cg92 -I. -I/home7/dcjk/lib -I/usr/openwin/include -DSVR4 -DSYSV -c builtins.c
> gcc: language F not recognized

When you do a xmkmf, the definitions taken for your compiler are for Sun's
compiler (-xF -Wa,-cg92). the -x option does not have the same definition in
gcc. Either modify your installa tion or modify the Imakefile/Makefile to
override thes option flags.
