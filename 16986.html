>>>>> "DN" == Dan Norris <d-norri_at_forrest.cso.uiuc.edu> wrote:

>> You can use
>>
>> ExecUseShell /usr/local/bin/tcsh
>>
>> to make fvwm2 use tcsh instead of the default sh, if that's your
>> problem.

DN> Any way to do that in the ol' fvwm-1.24r?

Well, if your problem is really the shell, you can always do

Exec "xterm" /usr/local/bin/tcsh xterm &

This will call "sh" to execute "tcsh" to execute xterm. I have not
tested but it may work.
