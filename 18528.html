    Jamie> Hey, How do you get fvwm to activate a window just by
    Jamie> clicking the mouse inside the boundaries of the window?
    Jamie> (as opposed to activating by clicking on the top, or by
    Jamie> just entering the window)

Jamie,

You can do this by setting Style "*" ClickToFocus and then using the
FvwmAuto module to autoraise windows. In addition, Chuck is planning
to add a flag if you want to pass the first click to the app or not (I
think now it does go to the app).

Later,
