Hi there!

I've found a way to simulate the Alt-Tab behavior similar to another GUI (which does not deserve that name):

  Key Tab A M altTabSwitch

  addtofunc altTabSwitch
  + "I" Prev Raise
  + "I" Prev FlipFocus

seems to work fine (using Fvwm Version 2.2.4).
What do you think about it?
