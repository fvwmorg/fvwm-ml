I would like to have a small screen next to my main screen, and put my button
bar for both screens on the small screen. This way, I will have more real
estate on my large screen, and still have plenty of space for a large number of
buttons and swallowed apps.

I would like the small screen to have no virtual desktop, but I would like the
pager for the large screen to be swallowed by the button bar on the small
screen. Also, when the button bar is used (pushing buttons), I want the
resulting actions to affect the large screen.

That is, I click on a page in the pager on the small screen, and the BIG screen
flips to the page I selected.

Is this possible with FVWM?
