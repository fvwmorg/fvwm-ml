"Jerome GOUT <"<gout_at_laas.fr> writes:


> I am using FVWM 2.0.42 on a sparc running on Solaris 5.4.
> Can someone tell me how to configure the left paddle-key of my sun
> keyboard.
> The main keys are "front" "open" to raise-lower and iconize-open.


Here is what I have in my .fvwmrc (or .fvwm2rc):

Key L3 A A PopUp "Window-Ops" Window-Ops #"props" key
Key L5 A A RaiseLower #"front" key
Key L7 A A Iconify #"open" key
Key L9 A A WindowList #"find" key


Hope this helps.
