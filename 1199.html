On 03 Aug 2000 15:36:59 -0500, Jack Christenson wrote:
>
> I have installed fvwm 2.2.4, and everything is working well except
> FvwmButtons. It is also working except that it doesn't show icons on the
> buttons. I have set IconPath and PixmapPath in my .fvwm2rc file. Is there
> anything else I have to do?

No, this is enough. You can also use ImagePath instead of obsolete
commands IconPath and PixmapPath starting from fvwm 2.2.4.

Can you see icons at all? Supposing you have xterm.xpm in your ImagePath
(or PixmapPath), do you see this icon when iconifying xterm after:

  Style "xterm" Icon xterm.xpm

If no, check again your *Path commands; did you compile with xpm support?
If yes, there is a problem with your FvwmButtons config, I guess.
