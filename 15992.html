On Sun, 24 Mar 1996, David A. Berson wrote:

> An interesting use of such a feature is to use it to support modules that
> can reconfigure themselves on the fly. Say, have FvwmTalk support
> "send moduleName configCommand"
> Sure would beat edit .fvwmrc/restart cycles.

Sorry to jump in, but I second this wholeheartedly. While I love FVWM
more than any other WM, something that I really hate is the fact that when
I make a change to my .fvwm2rc file, I have to restart. While I'm at my
console, this isn't a problem ... but when I'm sitting up in the computer
lab it's a pain (it takes FOREVER to restart). Jarl's Buttons have the
option of an external rc file which I really, *REALLY* like. I can make
changes w/o starting FVWM. Not to compare apples to oranges, but OLWM
lets me change menu items w/o restarting.

I'd love to see an option that I could add to a menu:

+ "Reread Config" Reconfig
