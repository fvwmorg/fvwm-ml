Hello.

Currently I have in my .fvwm2rc file

=====================
AddToFunc SelectWindow
+ I Focus
+ I Iconify false
+ I Raise
+ I WarpToWindow 50 8p

Key Tab A M Next [*] SelectWindow
=====================

and I can cycle through windows using M-Tab and choose
the one I want raised and focused. But I am not happy
with the order of the windows when cycling through the
available ones. I want them ordered with the most
recently focused on top.

Also I would like to not have the windows raised
while cycling through them, until I stop cycling,
that is, until I have chosen the window I want.
Is it possible to just present the name/icon of the
window while cycling, and only raise and focus the
chosen one?

Any clues on how to achieve these?
