On Sun, Nov 22, 1998 at 07:09:24PM +0000, kayak_at_sisna.com wrote:
> How do I change the default sounds on my desktop to ones the I would
> prefer (That bounceing sound is irratating)

That depends on how the sounds are generated. If you are using
FvwmAudio or FvwmEvents the configuration should be in your
.fvwm2rc file (maybe the system-wide file). See the manpages for
this modules for details.

> and I also what to change
> the Background picture to one I used in M$ Windows?? If I remeber right
> there is a file somewhere that has all this set, but I cannot remeber
> where it is or what name it is or even if there is another way to change
> all this

You can do that quite a few ways. You may find that in .fvwm2rc or
perhaps xinitrc (/usr/X11/lib/X11/xinit on my system) or in ~/.xinitrc.
The background image may be set by FvwmBanner (see manpage) or perhaps
by xsetroot, xpmroot or xv.
