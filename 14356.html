> Currently I am running FVWM on a Sparc10 (Sun OS4_1_3) and can't seem to
> get it to exit gracefully. I either need to kill the console (started in
> .xsession with an xterm -C) or do a kill -9 X(pid). Does anybody have an
> idea why I'm having this problem? If so, what can I do to fix it?

Is fvwm the last thing started in your .xsession? From the sounds of it,
you have the 'xterm -C' as the last item.

Try changing it so that fvwm is the last thing run (but dont run it in
the background!) Once fvwm exits, the .xsession script ends, and xdm will
end the session.
