So I've now got it working pretty much as I want. I've figured out
flipfocus by searching for it in the man page beyond its own
definition. The example of it being used with windowlist led me to
redefine togglewindow as

AddToFunc togglewindow "I" Prev (!iconic CurrentPage) FlipFocus
+ "I" Current (!iconic CurrentPage) Raise

and I now have the behavior I want.

That leaves the problem with empty arguments being passed (maybe
that's a bug?) and one other minor nit I noticed when I did a restart
via FvwmCommand:

[FVWM][Done]: <<ERROR>> Call of 'fvwm2
' failed!!!! (restarting 'fvwm2' instead)

Somewhere along the line I thought I'd seen that one fixed, but I
guess not.

The only thing still bugging me is why warp-or-run can't seem to
properly run FvwmForm.
