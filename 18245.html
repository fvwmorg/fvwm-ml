Here's a little oddity I just noticed.
(I believe these are the pertinent lines from the config file:)
EdgeScroll 100 100
EdgeResistance 350 10

If you move a window all the way to the edge of the screen, so that it is
flush against the border without popping across, and then put your mouse
up against the edge of the screen and click as though you were trying to
grab the resize handle, you will instead get a root menu.
Of course this only works when you are on a border that leads to another
virtual screen. At the Virtual edge, you get a mouse pointer that DOESN'T
pop up a root menu, but instead seems to select the window for moving.
