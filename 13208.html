> Klaus Umbach <Klaus.Umbach_at_fvwmwiki.org> writes:
> > Hello support-center,
> >
> > I am the proud owner of a Logitech Mx310, a Mouse with 8 buttons
> > (including the wheel). Button 8 is not detected by X, but the buttons 6
> > and 7.
> > Now I want to use the buttons 6 and 7 with fvwm, which does not work.
> >
> > Mouse 6 R A DynMenu
> >
> > Is this an my fault, or a missing feature (fvwm 2.5.10 with
> > gentoo-patches)?
>
> See question 6.8 in the FAQ.

I think the FAQ is obsolete. The Fvwm core *should* support
buttons 6 to 9 out of the box. I've taken the opporunity to
revisit the whole issue and made numerous patches to the core and
the modules. Now, up to 9 buttons are supported out of the box,
and up to 31 can be enabled by modifying the
NUMBER_OF_EXTENDED_MOUSE_BUTTONS macro in libs/defaults.h.
