Right Shift + NumLock allows the keypad to control the cursor.
1,2,3,4,6,7,8,9 move the cursor (hold down * while moving to speed it
up), and 5 is the mouse click (it toggles btwn left, right, or center by
hitting /,*, or - b/f hand)
