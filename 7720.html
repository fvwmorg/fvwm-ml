Fvwm's home page at http://www.fvwm.org/ says:

  NEWS FLASH! FVWM 2.4.15, a stable release, is now available for download

which is terrific, however a NEWS FLASH without a date looks rather
odd. The infrequent visitor is given no clue whether the news
flash was posted two days ago or a year ago.

The "Last modified" date at the bottom of the page indicates
that this is not an abandoned site, however it is not certain
that the "Last modified" date pertains the news flash or some
other item on the page.

Similarly, the "Latest News" link takes us to:

  http://www.fvwm.org/generated/NEWS.html

where again we are given no dates, thus we are left in the dark as
to how "Late" the "Latest News" may be.

Several other related pages suffer from the absence of dates too.
See, for instance,

    http://fvwm-themes.sourceforge.net/

where it says: "Stable fvwm-themes-0.6.2 is released", but doesn't
say when.

Please consider including dates corresponding to announcements and
news releases.
