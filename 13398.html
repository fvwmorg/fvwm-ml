On 18 Oct 2004 16:24:37 -0700, Elliot Sowadsky wrote:
>
> I dont suppose theres a way of fvwm reaching into a window's environment
> variables, is there?
>
> I want to make windows started from a different "environment" look
> different.
>
> Running 2.5.12

If your xterm sets WINDOWID (mine does) and if you run FvwmCommandS,
then in your shell's initialization files (or from anywhere else) run:

  FvwmCommand "WindowId $WINDOWID WindowStyle HandleWidth 20"

You get the idea.
