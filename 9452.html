gabe f <gabe_1_at_mac.com> writes:
> Hi, I was wondering how to change backgrounds of the pages in fvwm,
> because
>
> FvwmBackerDesk 0 -solid black
> FvwmBackerDesk 1 -solid blue
> FvwmBackerDesk 2 -solid red
> FvwmBackerDesk 3 -solid yellow
>
> sets all 4 of my fvwm pages to black. Please direct me to a faq if
> there is one, I
> know this is a basic question.

Please mention the release you are using.
You are probably mixing up pages and desks.
Here is the syntax you want to use:

 *FvwmBacker: Command (Desk 0, Page 0 1) -solid midnightblue
