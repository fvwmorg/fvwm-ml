On Wed, Feb 28, 2001 at 10:31:50AM +0100, Andre Bonhote wrote:
> hi list!
>
> when i have several icons on my desk, new windows will not cover them
> when i am using TileManualPlacement. i'd like to place all my windows
> automatically *over* the icons.
>
> at the right side of my screen i have FvwmButtons running. now, when
> no icon is around, Maximize grow 100 does a fine job, expanding
> exactly to the border of FvwmButtons. But when there are icons around,
> Maximize will only maximize to the first icon appearing.
>
> how can i change all the icons to be ignored, that means, not to be
> treated as windows? is there a way to do this?
>
> i am currently using
>
> FVWM version 2.3.29 compiled on Feb 26 2001 at 10:01:28
> with support for: ReadLine, XPM, GNOME WM hints, SM

Currently you don't have much control over this. You could try
MinOverlapPlacement. Another possibility would be to use
MinOverlapPercentPlacement and edit libs/defaults.h manually and
replace

  #define PLACEMENT_AVOID_ICON 10

with a small value

  #define PLACEMENT_AVOID_ICON -0.05

I can't guarantee that this will help, though.
