>MB > I've seen this request several times, and I must be missing
MB > > something.
MB > >
MB > > You can move the windows around *within* the pager to different
MB > > desks, thus moving it to another desktop. You can drag windows out of
MB > > the pager onto the current desktop (i.e., the inverse of what you
MB > > describe). What extra functionality does the olvwm behavior get you?
MB > > Can you give an example?
>
>
No, you won't get *extra* functionality. It's just easier. What do you think:
If you want to move a window, is it easier to grab the real window and drop it
over the Pager onto another desktop, or is more convenient to pick up the
*small* representation (7x7 pixel) in the GoodStuff and drag this. If you say,
the second option is better, then you won't get extra functionality from this
olvwm-like behaviour.
It's the same with the zooming effect. It's just a little feature that gives
you a hint, where your icon went.
