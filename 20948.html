> I went to sunsite to mirror FVWM and they don't have the new one.
> But what I did see was fvwmCDEb or something like that. What is it,
> anyone know?

FvwmCDE is a fvwm2 module which mimics the appearance and some of the
functions of the standard CDE taskbar. It's based on the XForms
library. It's home page should be

http://gibson.eee.upd.edu.ph/~orly/unixcentral/FvwmCDE.html

but I'm not able, as of now, to reach that host.

I rather like FvwmCDE, I'm using it all the time... Latest version I
know of is something like 1.5.
