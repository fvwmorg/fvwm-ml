de5_at_sws5.ctd.ornl.gov writes:

} I miss SloppyFocus--it lets me move the pointer out of the way without
} losing focus. Is it coming back?

it never left. :) you just have to specify it differently - in a style
line, instead of by itself. here's the line from my .fvwm2rc for it:

Style "*" BorderWidth 4, HandleWidth 2, SloppyFocus

that's the one of the first style lines in my .fvwm2rc, so everything
that isn't defined specifically later, recieves those styles...
