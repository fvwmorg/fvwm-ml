On Tue, 18 Nov 1997, Jerry Ingerman wrote:

->
-> I have one monitor which I understand to be 'Desk:0'.
->My .fvwm2rc states 'DeskTopSize 4x4'. So there 16 desktops are
->allocated. Now I want to have a button in the button bar that
->will cause a jump to a specific one of those 16 desktops.
-> I believe that the "GotoPage" call is for transferring
->to Desk:1 or 2 or 3, but *not* within Desk:0. Either that or I
->just dont know how to use it correctly.
-> Thanks in advance for any help!

DeskTopSize 4x4 sets the size of each desk to 4 by 4 "pages" not
desks. The GotoPage call does exactly that, it tranfers your viewport
to a certain page, to change desks you would use Desk 0 1 or
something.

Your desktop looks like this (roughly):
        _________________________
        | 0 0 | 1 0 | 2 0 | 3 0 |
        _________________________
        | 0 1 | 1 1 | 2 1 | 3 1 |
        _________________________
        | 0 2 | 1 2 | 2 2 | 3 2 |
        _________________________
        | 0 3 | 1 3 | 2 3 | 3 3 |
        _________________________


so to go to the second page down on the right hand side, you would use

GotoPage 3 1

Hope this makes sense, I'm hungover as hell and not particularly well.
