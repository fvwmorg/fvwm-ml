Last week, I posted a question asking for a mouseless way to switch
among screens. From the responses I got back, i was clear to me that I

desktop. What I'm looking for, is a way to jump between screens in a
multi-screen environment.

when fvwm comes up, it shows me 2 desktop 0s, one in each screen. I
was hoping there was either a direct way to jump between screens, or a
way to assign desktop 0 to display :0.0 and desktop 1 to screen :0.1,
or a way to assign pages within a desktop to each screen, or...

Anyway, anybody got any ideas?
