Kai Grossjohann:
> Fvwm supports 2^15 (or 2^16?) desks out of the box

2^32-1, I'd guess:

        The total number of distinct desktops need not be specified,
        but is limited to approximately 4 billion total.

> Should be enough for most purposes.

Well, yes, most of the time. :-)

FWIW, I've been happy with five or six desks (I don't use pages).
