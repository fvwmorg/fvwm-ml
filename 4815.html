I have downloaded fvwmconf-0.19 to assist me with configuring the
successfully-installed fvwm2-2.4.6.

fvwmconf makes some suggestions about where to put its FvwmConf file and
its fvwm modules - namely /usr/lib/.fvwmconf and /usr/lib/X11/fvwm2.
None of which sound right for my box.

The Modules installed by fvwm2 are located at
/usr/local/libexec/fvwm/2.4.6/, the Executables at /usr/local/bin and
the Data files at /usr/local/share/fvwm.

Since fvwm2-2.4.6 and fvwmconf-0.19 appear to have somewhat different
ideas on appropriate location for their <files|directories>, I would
welcome some additional guidance before I damage something beyond
repair.
