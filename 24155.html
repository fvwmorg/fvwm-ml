At first, I'm sorry for my bad English.

At second, I understand, that version 2.3.1 is beta. ;)

I have serveral problems.

1. When I press "Start" button in FvwmTaskBar, location of start menu
is wrong. This location must will be above TaskBar window. Instead
this, lower left corner of menu is position mouse cursor.

In fvwm 2.1.7, in my config file, construction

*FvwmTaskBarStartMenu StartMenu 0p -25p

worked fine. Lower left corner menu was been placed in +0-25 at
root window. In fvwm 2.2 and above, this construction not worked.

Follow construction

*FvwmTaskBarStartMenu StartMenu root 0p -25p

is not works.

Help me, please.


2. I defined StartMenu as

AddToMenu "StartMenu_at_fvwm-menu.xpm@^#808080^"
+ ...

With these settings, background color of pixmap field must will be
#808080. However, I don't see it. Instead this, I see gray color of
background menu.

In fvwm 2.2 it worked fine. Where is problem? Help me, please.
