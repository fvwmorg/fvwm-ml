On Tue, 15 Dec 1998, Chad Hall wrote:

> I need a way to restart fvwm2 via command line or something
> similar.

> I apparently have a buggy rc that will lose all changes to buttons
> after a while, so I'm left with the default middle button and nothing
> else.
> This means I can't restart via mouse1 on root. I have tried to bind
> keys as well, and all keymods get lost when this happens. This has
> been happening for about a year now, and it's stuck with me thru all
> the changes I've made to my .fvwm2rc...any advice would be appreciated.

I'm not sure I understand are saying that you are making changes during a
fvwm session, through FvwmTalk or one of the other modules, and those are
not being saved to your rc. If that is the case that is the correct
behavior, currentlly there is no way to get external changes to the
config put back into your rc file.

However if you mean your running and suddenlly everything changes in your
environment then I think we have a problem, if it is this second case I
think we would need to take a look at the rc file, and a better
description of what is going on when the problem occurs.
