I have recently switched to fvwm2.0.43 (on SunOS4.1 and Linux) after
many years of using twm/ctwm and am very happy with the move, thanks
to all fvwm2 developers.

Now for the problem; I have a WinList permanently displayed

*FvwmWinListUseSkipList
*FvwmWinListGeometry -15+90
*FvwmWinListUseIconNames
*FvwmWinListMaxWidth 110

but as items are added and deleted to it it wanders off in a South
Easterly direction. Is there a known fix for this problem?
