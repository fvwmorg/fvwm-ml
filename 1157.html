Here's the patch which I forgot to attach at the fvwm list.

I hacked a variable StrokeWidth. The patch is attached
and contains also an update of the docs. Try:

    Mouse 3 A M StrokeFunc StrokeWidth 10 DrawMotion

I've also changed the allocation of the coordinate
array (for undrawing) to be dynamical, so that strokes
of arbitrary length (depends on your memory) can be drawn.

Well, I've been told it's a feature freeze ...
At least I consider the dynamical allocation for the
strokes a bug fix :-)
