In order to see mini-icons in the pager, you need to have
a mini icon associated with the window that the pager shows.
For example, if you are looking at an xterm in the pager,
you need:

Style xterm MiniIcon mini.xterm.xpm

Its easiest to start with:

Style * MiniIcon mini.xterm.xpm

of course you need a mini icon with that name around too.
