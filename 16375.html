Hi; Brad Smith <brads_at_bitinc.com> said:

|I looked in the man page, which told me to look at
|the/usr/include/X11/xkeysymdef.h file.

Try "/usr/openwin/include/X11/keysymdef.h" (yippee for SunOS 4).

|Key open A N Iconify
|Key SunXK_Open A N Iconify
|Key SunOpen A N Iconify
|Key XK_Open A N Iconify
|Key OSF_open A N Iconify
|Key Open A N Iconify

A good way to find out which keysyms you're generating is to run "xev" (not
shipped with our Openwindows - you may have to build it). Press the key's
you're interested in, and "xev" will tell you what their keysyms are.
