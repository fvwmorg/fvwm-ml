On Fri, 21 Jun 1996, Jeff Magill wrote:

-*> Sometimes one of my windows just doesn't seem to be visible anymore.
-*> This usually happens after I've dragged a window across pages using the
-*> little pager module.
-*>
-*> It still shows on my winlist, but I select it off the win list and my
-*> mouse just warps to the top of the page where there's nothing.

I've had this happen before. After your mouse warps to the top of the
screen, hit alt-F5, or whatever keystroke is bound to "Move". The window
will now be in 'move' mode, and you can drag it back down from never-never
land with the mouse.

Hope this helps :)
