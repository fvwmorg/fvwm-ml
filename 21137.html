rjray_at_uswest.com (Randy J. Ray) writes:
> OK, finally quit putting it off and went to 2.0.46. It seems that my
> FvwmButtons cannot get colors for the pixmap background like it did
> under *.45. Mind you, this is the same pixmap I use for my
> active-state title and handles. Right this minute, this composition
> window has a sane pixmap, while the buttons box looks, well,
> bleached. Despite being the same pixmap. Was there a patch I may
> have had (but forgotten) in 2.0.45, that I need for 46?

If you had applied the "colorlimiting patch" in 2.0.45, it wouldn't
have any affect on your colors unless you added a "ColorLimit" line to
your .fvwm2rc. So if you see a line like that in your .fvwm2rc, odds
are you applied that patch to 2.0.45.

Why you are getting a different set of colors for the same pixmap in
2 different places isn't clear. Yet.
