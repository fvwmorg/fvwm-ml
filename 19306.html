>>>>> "AF" == Adam Fitzpatrick <afitzpa1_at_technetium.helios.nd.edu> writes:

AF> How can I configure FVWM so that the mouse buttons are reversed for a
AF> left hander?

This is not generally a window manager function; X handles it at a lower
level. Look at xmodmap. In fact, the xmodmap even tells lefties what to
do:

xmodmap -e "pointer = 3 2 1"
