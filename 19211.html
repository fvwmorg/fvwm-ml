Brian Schroeder writes:
> I have assigned a few buttons in my button bar to activate fvwm menus. I
> am thinking of replacing FvwmButtons with TkGoodstuff (any comments on that
> idea?), and I am wondering if it is possible to do the same thing there.

I have a button in tkGoodStuff that is bound to

   fvwm send 0 {Menu Utilities Nop ""}

This brings up whatever the "Utilities" menu in fvwm is defined as,
assuming that tkGoodStuff is running as a module.
