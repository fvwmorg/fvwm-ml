On Wed, Sep 10, 2003 at 06:19:50PM +0200, KONTRA Gergely wrote:
> Hi!
>
> I have a specific idea, but I don't know how to do in in fvwm.
> I'd like to have a taskbar at the bottom, but in that area, I also want
> to have a process monitor and a pager.
> So basically I want this.
>
> +-------++----------------------------------------+-----+--------+-----+
> | Start || xmms | gimp | xawtv | |pager|cpu:50% |15:02|
> +-------++----------------------------------------+-----+--------+-----+
>
> AFAIK FvwmTaskbar cannot swallow anything, and I don't know how to tell to
> it not to fill to the right. So I'd like to avoid Taskbar's windowlist
> and pager/process monitor overlapping.
>
> Any ideas?

The usual way to emulate this is to use FvwmButtons as the base
container, define a button for the menu and swallow FvwmIconMan
for the application button. Any number of additional buttons and
swallowed apps can be defined easily.

I'm not sure whether a config for this fairly complex setup is
floating around here.
