>You can move the windows around *within* the pager to different
>desks, thus moving it to another desktop. You can drag windows out of
>the pager onto the current desktop (i.e., the inverse of what you
>describe). What extra functionality does the olvwm behavior get you?
>Can you give an example?

Umm, I used to use it to clean up my current screen. For example,
take *this* window (*this* being the one under the pointer) and drag it
to desk 3.

The extra functionality is that when I am moving my windows from the
current screen to another, I have a *big* target to click the mouse
on, instead of little teeny weeny windows in the pager.
