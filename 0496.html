On 14 Mar 2000 15:30:23 +0100, Christian R Molls wrote:
>
> how can I make the WindowList appear in the center of the screen and
> not where the mouse pointer is?

In the developement version 2.3 a menu position (WindowList is a menu) is
fully configurable. For example, Ctrl-Mouse2 does what you want:

Mouse 2 R C WindowList Root c c NoDeskSort, SelectOnRelease
