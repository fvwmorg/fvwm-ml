On Fri, Jan 08, 1999 at 09:16:46AM -0500, tchappell_at_gsu.edu wrote:
>
> (fvwm 2.1.5)
>
> I'm using SetMenuStyle and ChangeMenuStyle to customize the appearance
> of my menus. Is there any way to make these customizations apply to
> the builtin WindowList as well ? The MenuStyle command appears to
> affect the coloring, font, etc. of the WindowList, but it does not
> have the option that allows to do things make the background be a
> pixmap like SetMenuStyle does. I can't get ChangeMenuStyle to affect
> the WindowList. Is there any way to make the builtin WindowList be
> backed by a pixmap ?

The WindowList always uses the default MenuStyle. If you give
that one the background pixmap WindowList will get it too.
Unfortunately you can't use ChangeMenuStyle on the WindowList
