On 22 Nov 2002 12:36:04 -0800, Ben Sferrazza wrote:
>
> Is there a way to display the smaller 16x16 icons next to the text
> window title for the buttons in FvwmTaskBar?

Yes, it is possible. Try something like this:

  ImagePath /dir/with/your/icons:+
  Style XTerm MiniIcon mini/terminal.xpm
  Style XCalc MiniIcon mini/calculator.xpm
