>I am new to this mailing list (and to this WM) so I may be asking a basic
>question. I couldn't find the answer in the FAQ and on any other online docs.
>
>I am looking for a way to set Icons one after the other (say from Top left
>to the Top right). For now, every time a window is being Iconify the icon
>appear on a random position on the desktop (near the mouse).

>From my .fvwm2rc:

Style "*" IconBox -200 500 -5 -5
Style "xterm" IconBox -200 150 -5 -5
Style "emacs" IconBox -200 300 -5 -5

This places all xterms in one area of the desktop, all emacsen in
another, and everything else above that. (The numbers are the pixel
corners of the icon box)
