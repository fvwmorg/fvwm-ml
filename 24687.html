> antiquewhite -fg black -bd magenta4 -e rlogin -8 ssbun &

> ######### End of snippet #########

> You can see that the xterm arguments "-title" and "-name" are followed by just
> one word, but the "Wait" is looking for what I have my Title bar labeled with.
>
> How can I get the "Wait" to only have the same word as the "-title" and "-name"
> arguments?

Edit the file? :)

> Also I would like to use FvwmM4 to process this file but when I use the
> following:
> fvwm2 -cmd "FvwmM4 -m4-prefix -m4opt -I$HOME $HOME/.fvwm2rc"
> and have this
> "m4_include(`.fvwm2desk') .
> in my .fvwm2rc file, I get the following error:
> m4: bad option: --prefix-buttons

Try taking the `-m4opt' out. The manpage for FvwmM4 says it's not needed.

> So I ended up having to use FvwmCpp to process my file, but now I get a ton of
> errors because I have a ton of comments in my .fvwm2rc file.
>
> I wish it was as simple as putting in "read .fvwm2desk" in my .fvwm2rc file.
>
> Well, right now my biggest problem is the "wait" portion of the .fvwm2desk
> file. Can anyone help me with at least this?

Is this mysterious .fvwm2desk file auto-generated from someplace? If not,
why not simply edit the file to say `Wait XTerm' or what have you?

Also, if you don't want (or don't care) about m4 pre-processing, I believe
there is a command called `Read' in fvwm2. Check the manpage.
