Hi!

Here is my working configuration example:

*FvwmButtons( Size 60 56, Swallow(UseOld) "asclock" "Exec asclock -led
PaleGreen -exe 'bin/FvwmCommand \"Next [plan] Iconify -1 WarpToWindow 10 10\"'
-12 -shape")

On click it's deiconize plan.
