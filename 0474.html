Jack,

        Are you using CDE on Solaris?

        If so, the easiest way (relative to doing ANYTHING
        under CDE!) is to modify the file:

                /etc/dt/config/XServers

        by changing the last line, which should look something like
        this:

:0 Local local_uid_at_console root /usr/openwin/bin/Xsun :0 -nobanner

        Add the option "-ar1 NNN" like so:

:0 Local local_uid_at_console root /usr/openwin/bin/Xsun :0 \
                -nobanner -ar1 249

        Adjust "249" to whatever you feel comfortable with.
        AFAIK, trial-and-error is the only way to experiment.

        This, of course, has nothing to do with fvwm. :-)

Bill
