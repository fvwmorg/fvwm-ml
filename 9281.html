| Others have already suggested xv and xloadimage (also known with
| its aliases xli and xsetbg).

And of course fvwm-root, from the fvwm package itself. I tend to size
backgrounds with a separate tool (if they're not the right size already)
and then call fvwm-root with the resized image. This lets one separate
the "set the wallpaper" from the image composition.
