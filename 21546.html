On Mon, 22 Dec 1997, Brian Snyder wrote:

> Hello. I remember reading that FVWM might be ported for use w/ gnu
> autoconfig. I have successfully compiled many apps with this utility,
> and I have been unable to get IMAKE to work with fvwm. So my question
> is this, 'is it being ported to autoconfig, and if so, is there an ETA

Yes, I'm working on autoconf for fvwm. There are patches, as previously
posted, but they should be considered ALPHA quality. I'm working on them
right now, so they will be volatile for the next day or so. After that,
I'm on holiday for a bit.

If you want to download them and test, I'd greatly appreciate all bug
reports as well as success stories. But, please hold off until after
Christmas day before downloading the patches.

I hope to finish the work in early January.
