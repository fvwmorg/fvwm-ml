On 17 Aug 2002 18:54:15 -0500, Wilbert van Bakel wrote:
>
> I installed fvwm-2.5.2 and I want to use xft2 from the
> fcpackage-02.07.28-something.tar.gz.
> Maybe I didn't install the fcpackage right, but the header file which
> declares FcBool is found in /usr/X11R6/include/fontconfig/fontconfig.h.
>
> Which steps do I need to take to compile fvwm-2.5.2 with xft2 support?

config.log contains a reason why xft2 was not detected.

Try to specify:

  ./configure --with-freetype-prefix=/usr/X11R6

(or --with-freetype-exec-prefix=/usr/X11R6), this sets a prefix of your
fontconfig-config script, i.e. /usr/X11R6/bin/fontconfig-config .
