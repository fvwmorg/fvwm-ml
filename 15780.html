Doing something besides complaining for a change. Here's a list of
button styles I've collected from various sources, some come with the
fvwm source distribution, some I lifted from the dotfile generator, and
some I created myself by modifying existing ones.

### buttons styles ###

#ButtonStyle * 4 50x25_at_1 75x75_at_0 25x75_at_0 50x25_at_1

#ButtonStyle * 4 50x65_at_1 35x35_at_1 65x35_at_1 50x65_at_0

# tall box, looks like ||
#ButtonStyle * 5 40x25_at_1 60x25_at_1 60x75_at_0 40x75_at_0 40x25_at_1
# the normal little box .
