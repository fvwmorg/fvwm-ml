Being one of the people who voted for the logo that won - what a great
design - I got to wondering what Aquatint would do with it. Aquatint is
an elegant little program for MacOS X that does only one thing - it
"liquefies" a shape. I'm _not_ promoting my experimental variations in
any way, but I posted them just for fun. I couldn't turn it into a tabby
cat though :-)
