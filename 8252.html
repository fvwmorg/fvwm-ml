I have something like this in my menu, it works fine here.

AddtoMenu RootMenu
+ "Play DVD" exec /home/sa/bin/xine-play.sh

and /home/sa/bin/xine-play.sh is:
#!/bin/bash
aumix -v 99 -w 79 -i 79 -o 79&
xine -a 2 --auto-play -s dvd &
