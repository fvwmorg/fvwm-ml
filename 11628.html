Colorset 9 NoShape, VGradient 13 1 #f6f6f6 50 #000000, Alpha 50

DestroyDecor DefaultDecor

+ TitleStyle LeftJustified Height 13

+ AddButtonStyle 1 Inactive Vector 4 44x44_at_3 44x45_at_3 45x45_at_3 44x44_at_3 -- Flat
