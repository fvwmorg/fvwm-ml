On 30 Dec 2002 19:15:04 +1100, Ross Hamilton wrote:
>
> Am I correct in assuming that the shadowed font feature doesn't
> work with anti-aliased text rendered through xft?
> If so, is there any likelyhood of getting the two to work together
> in the future?
> I'm using fvwm 2.5.4 on a Debian system with xfree 4.2.3 and a
> fairly recent freetype2.

Both features worked nicely together from the start. There were problems
with MultiPixmap and shadowed text, but even this works well in cvs now.

I suppose you use colorsets for defining fg, bg and fgsh of your text.
Otherwise text shadow is not drawn.

Try this:

  Colorset 1 bg gray30, fg gray80
  Style * Colorset 1
  Style * Font "Shadow=2:xft:Verdana:Medium:size=12:minspace=true"

If you have any problems please post relevant configuration that uses
colorsets and does not work for you (after running the daily snapshot).
