Whenever an icon is autoplaced, its placed in the first open slot,
regardless of its prior position. (Unless its been moved.)

In my opinion, the functionality requested is reasonable. I might
apply a patch if it was submitted. (Hacks won't do.)

I think this is better described as "icons automatically remember their
place".
