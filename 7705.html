On Mon, Jan 20, 2003 at 10:02:57AM +0100, Dominik Vogt wrote:
> On Sun, Jan 19, 2003 at 09:31:19PM +0100, Klaus Umbach wrote:
> > Hello list,
> > when I minimize my xmms (with equalizer and playlist) I have one icon in
> > my fvwmiconman. But after restarting fvwm while having xmms minimized I
> > have three icons. How can I change that?
>
> Which xmms version do you have? Fvwm condenses all the icons of
> transient windows into the main window's icon. Optionally, it can
> be told to treat window groups likewise.
>
> Losing the icon grouping over a restart hints either at a changed
> fvwm configuration. Either that or the application has deleted
> the window group/transient status of the windows. What happens if
> you issue a "Recapture" instead of a restart? Do you see the same
> effect?

No.

>
> In any case, try
>
> Style * IconifyWindowGroups

OK, now I have one icon, but when I doubleclick after restarting fvwm I
only get the mainwindow back, neither the playlist nor the equalizer
