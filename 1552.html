Still I think some info may be helpful.

On 29 Oct 2000 09:07:02 +0100, Nicola Girardi wrote:
>
> Hello, I have a problem in customizing my titlebar. I'm trying to
> get fvwm2 (2.3.21) look like twm, and I can't find in the fvwm2
> manual page the solutions to the following problems:
>
> 1. Erase relief around buttons, making them part of the titlebar.
> ButtonStyle All -- Flat doesn't seem to do the trick.

You should make both title and buttons flat.

> 2. A border (say, 5 pixels) around buttons and title of the window,
> title background included.

This is configured by TitleStyle Height.

> 3. The TiledPixmap background on the title should start after the
> window title.

This is not possible as of now.
You can play with "TitleStyle Pixmap -- right" though.

> To make things simpler to understand I've just put online a couple
> of images of the two title bars.
>
> http://www.swlibero.org/~nikke/junk/twm-titlebar.xpm (This is how it
> should look like.)
>
> http://www.swlibero.org/~nikke/junk/fvwm-titlebar.xpm (My current
> configuration.)

Here is a better emulation (there is rc file in the same directory):
