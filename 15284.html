>>>>> Jeremy A Bussard writes:

J> On Thu, 25 Jan 1996, John Junior Dawkins wrote:
> i am assuming that
>
> AddToFunc "InitFunction" "I" Module FvwmAuto 250
>
> is equivalent to the AutoRaise function in fvwm1.2. but my windows don't
> automatically come to the top when i enter them. i have to click on the borders
> to get them to come to the top. what am i doing wrong?

J> I use the same thing in this manner:

J> AddToFunc InitFunction "I" Module FvwmButtons
J> Module FvwmAuto 50

J> Note that there's no "I" before Module for FvwmAuto...

well this is not a part of InitFunction then:
it's simply a command standing in .fvwm2rc on its own.
So it's executed as .fvwm2rc is read.

it'd have to be a
        + "I" Module FvwmAuto 50
in order to be added to the InitFunction definition, and be executed as
soon as InitFunction was called.
