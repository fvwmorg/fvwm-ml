On 15 Feb 2003 18:47:35 +0100, Wojtek Aleksander wrote:
>
> What should I add to my Styles definitions to turn the possibility
> of resiszing maximised windows off? Oh, and what to do to block moving
> maximised windows at the same time?
>
> This is how I maxisie my windows:
>
> AddToFunc Max
> + I Maximize layer grow grow

You can't currently disable resizing if you use Resize, but you can
disable resizing if you use MyResize (or MyMove):

  AddToFunc MyResize
  + I ThisWindow (!Maximized) Resize
