Does anyone here know of repositories for mini-icons (other than those
that come with FVWM)?

I'm not even necessarily looking for mini-icons, as much as small
icons... esp. height-wise. E.g., I have a little xterm pixmap that's
29x23 and that's very close to perfect size... could be a little taller
but not much.

This is more for button bars than for menu or frame buttons: I don't
want my button bar to be too tall (wide is OK, since I use a horizontal
bar :) and I want to stack two buttons on top of each other.

I have the AIcons package, but don't see much smaller than 32x32 in that.

Alternatively (or in addition :), does anyone have a reasonably reliable
way of shrinking xpms, without too much distortion? Seems unlikely, but
you never know :)
