Mm, the newer pager is much better; but could it not
have a x/y-offset option of 'auto' where it'll
put it in the default place unless it's going to be off
the screen? My pager is on the bottom of my screen, most
windows are fine, their balloons are visible. A window
near the bottom of the pager however will have a balloon off-
screen. But it I set it so that this is avoided then my
balloons for windows higher in the pager appear outside the
pager and hence I can't see them (autoraise interferes
a bit).

If this is way too tricky then fair enough, but it'd be
a better behaviour. More automatic = good.
