> While on this topic, I would like to ask about the double quotes
> around the "C", "D", "M", "I", "H".
>
> Although I see them in many of the examples posted here,
> they are not present in the examples on the fvwm2 man page.
> I happily omit the quotes in my .fvwm2rc, and things work just fine.
>
> If they are not needed, why does anyone use them? Backward compatibility?

I think they were necessary in some obscure, very outdated
version. Many people simply have not yet updated their config
files and removed them because they are still allowed. You won't
find the double quotes in the current man page anymore.
