> FVWM version 2.4.0 compiled on Aug 13 2001 at 13:54:45
> with support for: ReadLine, XPM, SM
>
> and uname -mprs:
> FreeBSD 4.4-RELEASE i386
>
> These are my keybindings:
> Key 1 A CM MoveToPage 0 0
...
> Key F3 A M WindowList
>
> Till some weeks ago, I used Xaccel and all the Keybindings worked

Check the FVWM FAQ, start with question 0.1:

0.1 A few minutes after fvwm is started my keyboard and mouse
     bindings stop working. What can I do?

A: Probably your NumLock, CapsLock or ScrollLock key is pressed. See
   Q 5.5 for more details.
