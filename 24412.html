>What I did find works is if I hit Num Lock on, all my bindings seem to
>get passed through to the vnc window, instead of the external fvwm. I
>never figured out why it worked, but then again I didn't want to start
>messing with it and have it break. So try num lock and let me know if
>it works for you as well.

Cool. Seems to work for me. I bet what's happening is that
numlock-on/numlock-off counts as a 'hyper key' for X purposes.
Consequently, when the numlock is on, your normal control/meta/shift
combinations don't work because they also have the "numlock-on" bit
set. Since the combination doesn't trigger an action, it's passed on
to the underlying window. This all makes sense except it doesn't
account for why the internal fvwm ignores the numlock. hmmm..

Anyway, thanks for the tip.
