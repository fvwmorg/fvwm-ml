I used to work with olvwm but have decided to change for
performance/memory issues. One of the neat things I could do with
olvwm was to move windows from on section to the "pager" (in
fvwm-linguo) to another by selecting that window in the pager and moving
it elsewhere. Is there something similar in fvwm? If so, how does it
work? So far, the only way I've been able to do so is by making the
window sticky, then moving to where I want it to appear and unstick it.
I find it somewhat cumbersome. Of course, I could be patient and open
windows only where I want them to appear in the pager...

Thank you for any/all answers.
