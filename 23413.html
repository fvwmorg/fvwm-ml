Side pixmaps are in the current 2.1.x series. Download 2.1.4

> Also, how 'bout sound binding for menus in FvwmAudio??

AddToFunction DriveOfficeMateCrazy "I" Exec exec audioplay MenuNoise.au
AddToFunction DriveOfficeMateCrazy "I" Popup MyMenu

"MyMenu" could be $0, MenuNoise.au could be $1.
Totally untested, but should give you the idea...
