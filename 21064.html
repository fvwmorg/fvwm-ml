On 5 Oct 1997, Manfred Bartz wrote:

> I have seen a fvwm2 titlebar design somewhere that uses a vertical
> gradient and in addition 3 or four evenly spaced lines overlayed on
> that. It gives a sort of ``knurled'' look.
>
> I think I understand the vector drawing facility, but titlebars can
> vary dynamically in length, so fixed coordinates won't work.
>
> Does anyone know how to produce those lines?

I think I can guess. What you may have seen is the combination of a
sticky window and a vertical gradient. Maybe. Anyway, vertical gradients
you do by using something like

TitleStyle VGradient 16 3 Red 20 Blue 30 Black 50 Grey

This is pulled directly from the result of "man fvwm2". I don't really
get this gradient business 'cause I'm not sure how it works along with the
HilightColor setting and the ForeColor and BackColor style settings. No
experience.

Which leads me to a similar question. How do you use "TitleStyle
VGradient" so that you get one gradient for unfocused windows and another
for focused windows?

G.F.
