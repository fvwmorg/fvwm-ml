I wanted to thank everyone who responded to my cry for help with
autorepeating keys on my Ultrasparc and fvwm. I finally got the keys
repeating by deleting the .xserverrc file from my home directory.

It contained the following line:

/usr/bin/X11/X -ar1 350 -ar2 30

After I removed that file and ran starx, my keys repeated. I don't
really understand why, but I'm happy.

Thanks again
Steve
