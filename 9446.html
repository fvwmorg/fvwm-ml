I've recently been writing up a new .fvwm2rc for a new machine and when
I got FvwmPager loaded, I came across a problem I've had in the past
where I didn't want my desktop to be 3x3. I knew there was a way to set
the size of the desktop, so I looked in the FvwmPager man page to no
avail. I also searched the fvwm2 man page; also to no avail.

So, I checked one of the sample .fvwm2rc's and found what I was looking
for: "DeskTopSize XxY". However, I still cannot find this in the
provided documentation. Shouldn't this option at least be in the fvwm2
man page? (Unwieldly as it is already.)

I personally think that if it is included in that man page, it should
also be referenced in the FvwmPager man page, since that's what alerted
me to wanting to change the desktop size.

Thanks!

-Andy
