In Fvwm version 2.0.39, in fvwm/builtins.c, line 998 reads

    char *ModulePath = FVWMDIR;

I believe it should read

    char *ModulePath = FVWM_MODULEDIR;

instead. I had to change it to get fvwm to find modules.
