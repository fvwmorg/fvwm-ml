Quote: "Make sure the images from the example are in your ImagePath".

You may create self containing config that may be Read, using $. variable
(that is the config's dir). For example:

  Read $./another-config-in-the-same-dir
  ImagePath +:$.

or maybe even:

  Test (!i smiley12.png) ImagePath $.:+
