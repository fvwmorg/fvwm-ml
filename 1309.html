> 2. Are the configurations for the nice screen shots on

Most of these screenshots are done for 2.3 configurations.
If you use the latest betas, just (re)move your .fvwm2rc and choose from
the root menu "Setup 95 Script" to configure Windows95-like setup.
Read the top and bottom of system.fvwm2rc-sample-95 if you need more info.
