I just did this yesterday... took me 20 minutes to find the part in the
man page about it. If you ask me, the section in the man page about the
Style option should have specific info on what each style does. As it is
now, they're all scattered throughout the manpage(s).

anyway...

SloppyFocus automatically does a GrabFocusOff, so you HAVE to put
GrabFocus AFTER SloppyFocus.

like:
Style * SloppyFocus
Style * GrabFocus


hope this helps,
