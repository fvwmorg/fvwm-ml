On Tue, 27 May 1997, Alfred von Campe wrote:

->Maybe I'm missing something obvious, but I've tried to RTFM and
->the FAQ and can't find the answer. I'd like to get my icons to
->behave the way they used to with mwm. Basically, I want this:
->
-> 1. No icon box
-> 2. Specify where icons should go and in which direction they
-> should "grow".
->
->Specifically, I like the first icon to go in the top right corner
->and have subsequent icons go to the left of that (i.e., I like the
->icons to be at the top of the screen from right to left). Is this
->possible with fvwm? If so, how?
->

This is in the manual but under the misleading heading of IconBox in
the Style commands. In my .fvwmrc I have the following as part of my
global style definition..

Style "*" IconBox +0 +150 +200 +750

This specifies that all icons go in a region of the screen from 0,150
at top-left to 200,750 at bottom-right. However, despite being called
IconBox there is no box, this just defines an invisible boundary. The
icons fill up from left to right by default and as far as I know
there's no way to change this.
