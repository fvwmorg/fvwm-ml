I've been having some trouble getting sound to work consistently well
with fvwm --- not that it's necessarily fvwm's fault.

When using rplay with FvwmEvent (with rplay (v. 3.3.1) support compiled in and
``builtin-rplay'' as FvwmEvent's Cmd), all the sounds are garbled while
typing ``rplay foo.wav'' from a terminal sounds fine.

With esd and artsd I've noticed that FvwmEvent will sometimes play the
same sound twice for one event and (as far as I can tell) apparently at
random.

What has worked best for other fvwm users? I know this isn't the most
important function of a window manager, but I figured it might something
simple I'm missing. Thanks for any input.
