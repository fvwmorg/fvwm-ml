>>>>> Kent Vander Velden writes:

  Kent> When I click on the title bar of a window the pointer changes
  Kent> to a circle and there seems to be a strange delay of perhaps
  Kent> 1.5 seconds before I can really do anything.

This is the time that Fvwm waits to see if you're doing a double
click. Just move the mouse a couple of pixels and you'll see that
Fvwm doesn't wait any longer.

You can set this time from your .fvwm(2)rc file, I think it's the
ClickTime parameter.
