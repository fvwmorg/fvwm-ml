On 2000-Sep-13, Sven Esbjerg wrote:

>> I'm quite satisfied with my settings except for two things. When I open a new
>> window (Xterm, emacs whatever) it doesn't automaticly recieve focus. This is
>> especially annoying when programs use pop-up boxes for verification of
>> questions. Is there any way to enable this?

Sven,

   Add GrabFocus and GrabFocusTransient to your default 'Style'.

   See the man page.

   You may need to upgrade your fvwm to a more recent version than 2.0.46,
   I think GrabFocus and GrabFocusTransient are fairly recent features.
   You should consider upgrading anyway, the more recent releases
   are really a lot nicer than 2.0.46.

Cheers, Joseph
