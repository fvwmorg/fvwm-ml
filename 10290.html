"Brian W.Kernighan" <is00gzw_at_taurus.zsu.edu.cn> wrote:

> How can I add a Windows like "Star Here" button to my Fvwm2?
>
> Now I have a root menu, and I want to bind it to a button("Start Button"),
> so When I click on it , the Root Menu will display on the screen ,
> just as all
> Windows "Start Here" button do.(It may use fvwmbutton, I guess.)
>
> Is there anybody can help? Thanks in advance!
>
> AddToMenu "Utilities"
> + "Root" Title
> + "&1 Rxvt" Exec rxvt -bg White -fg Black
> -ls -name "Term" -fn
> -Misc-Fixed-Medium-R-Normal--20-200-75-75-C-100-ISO8859-1
> + "&2 Gterm" exec gnome-terminal
> + "&3 Mozilla" exec mozilla
> + "&4 Gimp " exec gimp
> + "&7 Xffm " exec xffm
> + "&8 Screenshot" exec gnome-panel-screenshot
> + "&9 Xterm" exec xterm -fn 9x15
> + "&0 Xeyes" exec xeyes
> + "Programme &p" Popup Applications
> + "" Nop
> + "&r Restart" Restart fvwm2
> + "&k Kill fvwm2" Quit
>
