wang yin <wang-y01_at_mails.tsinghua.edu.cn> writes:
> Hi,
>
> I've been using FVWM for several month and get quite involved with it.
> But now I have a strange problem.
> Some of my windows will disappear unexpectly sometimes.
> I don't know when and which will disappear. It seems the problem is not eas
> y to reproduce.
>
> I only know that rxvt is the most often to disappear of all and the most li
> kely occasion is when I restart FVWM.
> Once the windows vanished, I can't get it back! It's not in the WindowList
> or else where. Nor can I get it back with a "All () MoveToPage".
> It just closed! Why?

Use this command to see if the window is really gone:

xlsclients | grep rxvt

If the window is really gone, perhaps rxvt printed an error message
and exited. Find stderr for fvwm and look there. Stderr might
be on your console, or you may have redirected fvwm's stderr when
you started it:

fvwm2 >/tmp/fvwm.out 2>&1

If that still doesn't help, perhaps rxvt core dumped.

Make sure core dumps are enabled (man ulimit), then look for
$HOME/core.
