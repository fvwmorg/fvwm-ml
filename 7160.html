On 04 Dec 2002 21:42:37 +0000, taviso wrote:
>
> hi guys, ive made my pager transparent apart from the background of a
> selected page, which ive made transparent with a tint of black, and the
> title of the selected desk.
>
> i know its a nitpick, but is there anyway i can get the title
> transparent with black tint? i can set the background tinted easily, but
> not the title :(

Seems like the pager titles are not completely converted to colorsets.
Only fg and bg from HilightColorset are currently used for titles.

There were also several requests to separate title colors from page
hilight colors. Maybe someone will have a time to add a new TitleColorset
option to FvwmPager to apply all (non just fg/bg) colorset properties
to titles, it may default like now to fg and bg from HilightColorset.

> heres a screenshot to show what i mean:
>
> http://taviso.decsystem.org/images/linked/pager.png
>
> i can tint the part i've labeled [2] (although its not tinted here), but
> not the bit labeled [1], i dont mind the opaque icons, but is there a
> way to get a border on them ?

Do you mean a 16x16 (well, 18x18) border around transparent mini icons or
something else?

You may tint non-transparent parts of mini icons using IconTint or
IconAlpha in FvwmPager's WindowColorsets.
