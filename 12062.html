Hi,

I am totally new at FVWM. In fact I have discovered it 2 or 3 days ago
but I am yet, having fun with it.

It seems so powerful that I plan to use it instead of my other WMs.

For instance I used to use Ion which is another approach to windowing
management.

I already have had a few discussions about FVWM with a couple of people
on DLFP (Da Linux French Page) site [1] and few have shown me some
amaizing screenshots.

I also played with configurations from Taviso, Ikaro... and wow ! This
is amazing stuff !

So I registered yesterday the domain name FVWMFR.ORG and plan to
install tikiwiki on it so that french people can share their hints,
tips, excerpt of configuration files, screenshots, announces...

Feel free to visit it. I plan to finish to install it on Sunday evening.

Aim at this is for me to learn more on this fabulous Window Manager,
share my knowledge (yeah I know it is poor at the moment), discuss
about FVWM2 features,...

I hope I would be able to federate many french (or not) people around
this topic.

Thank you
