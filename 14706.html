What I want is for GoodStuff/FvwmButtons to swallow one app, but launch
another on click.

For example, I can have GoodStuff swallow "coolmail", an xbiff. When I
click on the swallowed "coolmail", it (coolmail) launches my mail reader.
Unfortunately, not every swallowable program has this kind of behavior.
I'd like to have GoodStuff _not_ pass the click events to the swallowed
program, but rather interpret them itself.

What I want is to take an application like "xdaliclock" that's been
swallowed, and have GoodStuff launch ANOTHER app (maybe plan or
xcalendar) when I click the button.
