On 12 Mar 2005 21:06:56 +0800, kaleasy_at_21cn.com wrote:
>
> I have define this key,
> Mouse 2 1 A Stick
> But,when i press the button 1 with middle mouse click,it take no effect,
> so it is,the sticky doesnot work at all.Other window can easily cover
> the Sticky window.

The sticky window is the one that appears on all desks, it does not
necessarily stays on top of windows. What you want is Style StaysOnTop
(change window layer statically) or Layer (change layer dynamically).

> if i change the key to
> Mouse 2 1 A Exec exec xterm
> it does work,and pop up a xterm window.
>
> how could i do then?

Do the following:

  Mouse 2 1 A Layer 0 6

You may even bind a function that toggles between Layer 4 and Layer 6
(left as an exercise).
