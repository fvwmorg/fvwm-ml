> Has anyone an idea how to start Xdm with bpp 16?

You could get rid of your 8bit modes in the Xfree86 config file.

or have ":0 Local local /usr/bin/X11/X -bpp 16" in the xdm/Xservers file.

To do 16bpp with startx do:

startx -- -bpp 16
