> (And (just as a side note): is there any way to start a programm at a

> XEmacs always live at +0+0?)

Yes, add them to your ~/.Xdefaults file:

XEmacs*Geometry: 0+0

and then run:

xrdb -merge ~/.Xdefaults

(I have that line in my ~/.xsession)
