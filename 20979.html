Can you send messages as plain text instead of HTML?

Anyway, your message requires some mind-reading. Apparently you are
starting fvwm and some parts of your set-up work while others do not.
What kind of .fvwmrc file are you using? Your problem could just be
caused by an incorrectly-arranged FvwmButtons (GoodStuff?) or something.
You have checked execute permissions on what? Please clarify. Your
problem could be caused by the wrong READ permissions on something as well
as it could be caused by the wrong EXECUTE permissions on a module. What
version of fvwm are you using?
