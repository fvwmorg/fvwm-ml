Jim Knoble wrote:
>
> do you have NumLock or CapsLock pressed? X11R6 and later have made the
> *Lock keys into modifiers; fvwm has strange behavior when they're
> activated (e.g., ClickToFocus works oddly if NumLock is pressed).
>
> --jim

No, definately not.

Though I do invoke my menu through a little function/hack so it always
pops up in the lower left hand corner.

The MS keyboard window key is assigned to Kanji by xmodmap:

        keycode 115 = Kanji

and Kanji is bound to a function in my .fvwmrc.keys file:

        Key Kanji A A PopupUtils Utilities

and PopupUtils is defined as follows:

AddToFunc PopupUtils "I" CursorMove -1000 +1000
+ "I" Exec vplay
/usr/local/sound/win95/Robotz.Menu.Popup.wav
+ "I" Popup Utilities


But I never had any problems with this before upgrading to 2.0.45.
