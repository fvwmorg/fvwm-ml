Jim Knoble sez...
>
> Yes. You probably have a `*geometry' resource set for xterm like:
>
> xterm*geometry: 80x40
>
> This will affect the geometry of the vt100 window (the one most folks
> use), the menus, and the tektronix window (the one most folks don't
> care about), as you have discovered. Instead, you should use:
>
> xterm*vt100.geometry: 80x40
>
> That will only affect the vt100 window, and won't affect the menus or
> the tektronix window.
>
> --jim

perfect. Thanks a lot.
