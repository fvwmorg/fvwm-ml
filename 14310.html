While, for the time being, fvwm_at_wonderland.org is simply an alias for
fvwm_at_hpc.uh.edu it is strongly preferred that you not send mail meant for
the FVWM mailing list there. Wonderland is behind some convoluted and
flaky mail plumbing and there are significant delays in crossing the ocean
to get from there to hpc.uh.edu (in Houston, Texas, USA). Yes,
wonderland.org is in Britain. More annoying is the fact that I get all of
the bounces sent to the wonderland list owners which usually includes some
delay messages in sending the messages back across the pond.

 - J<
