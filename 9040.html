On 27 May 2003 13:10:14 +0200, Klaus Zeitler wrote:
>
> I'm trying to compose my personal theme with bits and pieces from some
> of the themes (mainly default + olicha).
>
> Versions info: fvwm 2.5.6 (with support for: XPM, PNG, Shape, XShm, SM, NLS)
> fvwm-themes 0.6.2 on Solaris 5.8.
>
>
> Hope somebody can help me with a few minor details:
>
> 1. Can I remove the left button and the right window that shows the time on
> the TaskBar?

Kind of. Go to "Themes -> Current -> modules" and choose one of the
supplied WinList or IconMan options instead of TaskBar. A side effect of
this will be that the window list will grow up. If you want it to behave
exactly like FvwmTaskBar, but without its left and right parts, ask the
theme author (or me) to add this options to modules_at_olicha.

> 2. I'd like to have a border around FvwmButtons-FvwmApplets>. I thought
> I can change this with the following line in styles-extra:
> Style "FvwmButtons-FvwmApplets" BorderWidth 2
> but this doesn't help

You should do this in modules-extra, not styles-extra, because
styles-extra is read before modules.

> 3. is there a tool/module that I can use to see all the Colorsets of a theme

Type in FvwmConsole (Alt-F12) the following:

  FvwmScript FvwmScript-Colorset

But this tool is not perfect. It seems to be buggy, the colorset
background image flashes for me and then is replaced with a solid bg.

> 4. when I open the Pager-Desker panel (theme olicha), hide the panel
> behind another window, and then bring it back to the foreground,
> the border corners, that were hidden, look gaudy (sometimes a
> different color, sometimes some weird pixels)

Can you suply a screenshot showing the problem?

BTW, there is a fvwm-themes-users mailing list for the questions.
