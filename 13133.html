On Thu, Aug 19, 2004 at 07:57:52PM +0000, lokischmidt_at_lycos.de wrote:
> I'd like to have the main menu pop-up as a fixed and never disappearing
> menu on my side panel. How can I specify a static position in a layer for
> the menu?

Assuming your menu has the name MenuPanel:

  Style MenuPanel UsePPosition, layer <target layer>
  Menu MenuPanel root 0p 0p

Then move the mouse over the menu and press backspace.

> And how to make it show up at start and keep on appearing, no
> matter what and where I click on?

At the moment, menus can be torn off only interactively. In
2.5.11 there will be the option TearOffImmediately for the menu
and popup commands:

  Menu MenuPanel root 0p 0p tearoffimmediately
