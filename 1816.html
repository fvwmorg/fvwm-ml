On Fri, Dec 22, 2000 at 02:30:21AM +1100, Shao Zhang wrote:
> Hi,
>
> In fvwm, what is the right way to make the window maximized by default?
> I looked at the options for Style and there seems no geometry options.
>
> In apps like xterm, I can pass the geometry parameters. But not all the apps
> have this feature so I would like to do it in fvwm.

To specify a certain size for your apps you can edit your
$HOME/.Xdefaults or $HOME/.Xresources file. POut something like
this in it:

 <appname>.geometry: 990x740+24+0
