Now why does that sound familiar? :>
>
> 1. In my fvwm2 setup, I'm missing the information that allows the frame
> to be shown when I drag a window to a larger/smaller size. How do I get
> the frames to show?

Somewhere in .fvwm2rc, set XORValue=255 . You might have to play with
the value a bit.
>
> 2. How do I get minimized program icon's to "bunch up" in the bottom
> left corner of the screen?

Something like this: (but change the numbers)

Style "*" IconBox +875+8+927+800
# Auto Place Icons is a nice feature....
# specify left top right bottom of box

>
> 3. In fvwm, there was an option for "AUTORAISE" which brought the
> active window to the top after a set time, how do I get that to happen
> in fvwm2?

It's now a module. In your init and restart functions, add

+ "I" Module FvwmAuto 500 (I think that's in milliseconds)
