If you really feel compelled to suppress the message,
download a recent snapshot
-OR-
find
<install-prefix>/share/fvwm/<version>/fvwm/ConfigFvwmDefaults
and remove the help key binding
-OR-
Use xmodmap to assign the keysym "Help" to a key you don't currently
use.
