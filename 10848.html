I normally run with the mozilla mailer and opera both maximized
on the same desktop. Since upgrading fvwm to v2.5.8, I have

to the top. An example of this is when I'm moderating on

my moderation catagory, mozilla mailer pops to the top and
I have to alt-tab back to opera to continue.

is my configuration flawed somehow?
