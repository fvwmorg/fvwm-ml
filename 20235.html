> Hi,

> quick question: how to make Netscape Mail sticky, without
> making any other Netscape windows follow me around. And no,
> the obvious "Style Netscape*Mail Sticky" doesn't seem to
> work, even though that approach does work in every other
> context I try.


> Any help much appreciated,
>
> Simon

For me, this Style entries are working:

Style "Netscape" MiniIcon mini-nscape.xpm, NoPPosition
Style "Navigator" Icon netscape3.xpm
#Style "Netscape News:*"
Style "Netscape Mail" Icon MailBox.xpm
#Style "Netscape: Bookmarks"
#Style "Netscape: Address Book"
