On 13 Feb 2002 13:18:46 -0800, beej_at_piratehaven.org wrote:
>
> When my screen is full of windows, fvwm is kind enough to bring up the
> outline of a new window and prompt me to place it. When this happens,
> the mouse is at the upper left corner of the new window outline.
>
> The question: is there a way to reposition the mouse in this case so it
> falls in the center of the new window outline?

You may do this using FvwmEvent, add_window and WarpToWindow 50 50,
see FAQ #3.18.

But just purely by chance, you may do it now without changing configs.
If you press Shift while placing a window manually, you may also resize a
window by dragging, not just move it (this is a mwm compatibility mode).
Resize starts from the window center.

So if you press shift together with mouse button 1 and without dragging
(i.e. you immediately release the mouse button), you get what you want.
