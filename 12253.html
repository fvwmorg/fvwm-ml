> When you grep for something in ps-output, you usually also pick up the
> process of the grep itself (since it's argument is exactly what it's
> looking for). In this case, it picks up the sh-process created by
> fvwm.
>
> I know two ways to get around that:
> - grep for a regexp like '[F]vwm' (because it doesn't match itself)
> - or add a 'grep -v grep' to the command line
>
