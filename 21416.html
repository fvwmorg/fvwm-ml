On Sat, 29 Nov 1997, Adam Kopacz wrote:

> hi,
>
> i need some spezial buttons like an extra maximize button for some apps.
> i think i can only do this by decors or ??
> how can i start with this ???

You can have up to ten title bar buttons. Read the fvwm2 man page, and
reply back if you are still stuck. I'll give you a hint. Bind a button
to something, and it will appear. Here, for example, is what you'd add
to your .fvwm2rc to get a second button on the left of the titlebar:

    Mouse 1 3 A Maximiz 100 100

G.F.
