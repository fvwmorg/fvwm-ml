 I'd like to have a couple options for sorting, controlled by module
options or something like that. For example, since I mostly use
WindowList to find lost windows, I suspect I'd like a 'sort alphabetical
by name' option. If one wants to go whole hog, one could introduce label
formatting ala FvwmIconMan and an option to sort based on the results of
that.
