On Thu, Mar 24, 2005 at 11:42:42PM +0800, Eric Sun wrote:
> reading the man page, it seems this space is left for a mail icon. How
> can I get this icon appearing? Or, if I just don't want it, how to
> remove this space.

You can remove it by using:

*FvwmTaskBar: Mailbox none

You can make it active, by doing:

*FvwmTaskBar: Mailbox /path/to/mbox
