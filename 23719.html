Dan Espen wrote:
> > BTW, are there any plans to make the thin, invisible screen-edge borders
> > optional? Clicking at the edge of the screen invariably pops up my root
> > menu, which is undesirable most of the time.
> >
> > And, is there a way to disable page scrolling with the mouse, except
> > when it's dragging a window?
>
> Sounds like you are looking for the new "EdgeThickness" command.
> It deals with both issues.

Yep. I tried it, and it does the opposite of what I want.
"EdgeThickness 0" prevents me from dragging windows across pages (bad),
but keeps the root menus at screen edges (also bad). :(

Previously, I have just disabled mouse-page-scrolling and
edge-root-menus in the sources, leaving on only the bits which handle
window drags.


Otherwise, this release is great! I've been scaring my co-workers all
day, since I "Woo-hooey!" every time I find a new feature.. :)
