Hi,
        I just found a little bug in TitleStyle... I've been playing with
my setup (a variation based on dummies-fvwm), and I ran accross the
following problem. If you wanna achieve something like:

TitleStyle ActiveUp Solid grey55 -- Sunk
TitleStyle ActiveDown Solid grey60 -- Sunk

The ActiveDown Sunk/Raised option has to be switched like so:

TitleStyle ActiveUp Solid grey55 -- Sunk
TitleStyle ActiveDown Solid grey60 -- Raised

I guess for ActiveDown, if you want Sunk, you have to put Raised, and vice
versa...
