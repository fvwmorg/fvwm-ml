> I have two monitors on my computer running XFree in dualhead mode (not

> cat I change the focus between them without my mouse? I don't want to
> touch my mouse for that. Is there a way?

No, I think X requires that the pointer travels to the second
screen to activate it. You would have to have a second mouse and
keyboard on the other screen to activate it.
