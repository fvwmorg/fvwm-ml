> Anyway, I want to change the style of the mouse in the root menu
> (instead of this "horizontal double left arrow" - what kind of styles
> are there? I tried to use some of "the names" from one of the demo
> programs, but it does'nt work. I use SuSE 6.3 with fvwm2 2.2.0 and I
> tried this
>
> CursorStyle ROOT rigth_ptr

You mean right_ptr, not rig*th*_ptr, right?
This cursor context (ROOT) is only available in the betas 2.3.10+.
For 2.2 versions you can run this command in InitFunction or .xinitrc:

  xsetroot -cursor_name right_ptr
