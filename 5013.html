On 01 May 2002 16:04:52 -0500, David A. Rogers wrote:
>
> Is this the right place to report a bug?
>
> I'm running fvwm 2.4.6 on Debian (testing/Woody).
>
> Given the following entries:
>
> *FvwmButtons: Geometry +0-0
> *FvwmButtons: ButtonGeometry 32x32
>
> I would expect to get buttons sized 32x32 and the button bar to be in the
> lower left corner. This does not happen. The button bar is in the upper
> left corner. I have to change ButtonGeometry to
>
>
> *FvwmButtons: ButtonGeometry 32x32+0-0
>
> to get the buttons in the lower left corner. From the documentation, I
> would have expected this.

I am not sure this is bug. ButtonGeometry, if specified, fully overwrites
Geometry, and 32x32 is considered to be 32x32+0+0.

The documentation says a standard X11 window geometry should be specified
for both options, it does not say that part of the geometry is taken from
Geometry option and part from ButtonGeometry option.
