On Tue, 6 Jan 1998, Adrian Burd wrote:

: I've just installed fvwm2.0.46 on a DEC Alpha running DU4.0. So far
: everything works fine except that I seem unable to get any styles
: for the buttons in any titlebars for any windows.

I'm no kind of expert on fvwm or buttons, but here's a fragment from
my rc file. I took this from a sample fvwm2rc somewhere, I think, but
I had to change one or two things. It may help - at least you can
plug it in and then tweak it.

# ----- Buttony stuff.

# All my buttons should be red.
ButtonStyle 1 (Solid Red -- Flat)
ButtonStyle 2 (Solid Red -- Flat)
ButtonStyle 4 (Solid Red -- Flat)
ButtonStyle 6 (Solid Red -- Flat)


# Down Triangle for menu or close (top left corner)
AddButtonStyle 1 ActiveUp (4 50x75_at_1 25x25_at_1 75x25_at_1 50x75_at_0 )
AddButtonStyle 1 ActiveDown (4 50x75_at_0 25x25_at_0 75x25_at_0 50x75_at_1 )
AddButtonStyle 1 Inactive (4 50x75_at_1 25x25_at_1 75x25_at_1 50x75_at_0 )

# Left-most top right.
# 3 stacked rectangular panels like stills of a window being minimized
AddButtonStyle 6 17 95x20_at_0 95x60_at_0 75x60_at_0 75x40_at_0 75x70_at_0 55x70_at_0 55x60_at_0 55x85_at_0 15x85_at_0 15x60_at_1 55x60_at_1 30x60_at_1 30x40_at_1 75x40_at_1 45x40_at_1 45x20_at_1 95x20_at_1

# Centre top right.
# 2 stacked rectangular panels like stills of a window being maximized
AddButtonStyle 4 11 95x10_at_0 95x75_at_0 25x75_at_0 65x75_at_0 65x90_at_0 10x90_at_0 10x55_at_1 25x55_at_1 25x75_at_1 25x10_at_1 95x10_at_1

# Right-most top right.
# An X
AddButtonStyle 2 16 20x20_at_1 30x20_at_1 50x40_at_1 70x20_at_1 80x20_at_1 80x30_at_0 60x50_at_0 80x80_at_0 70x80_at_0 50x60_at_0 30x80_at_0 20x80_at_0 20x70_at_0 40x50_at_1 20x30_at_0 20x20_at_1
