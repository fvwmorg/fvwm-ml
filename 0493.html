On Tue, 14 Mar 2000, Carlton Davis wrote:

> I work in a french environment and at time I need to send correspondence
> in french. Is there a way to switch from engish keys to french keys and
> visa versa with fvwm2. I know that it can be done with KDE window manager
> but I don't particularly like KDE?

You could probably use xkeycaps [http://www.jwz.org/xkeycaps/] to generate
your two different keymaps, and then have a little script to flip between
them.
