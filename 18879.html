> David> problems with the new UseDecor commands. Is anyone else
> David> getting inconistent results? Eg I set a decor for my
> David> 'xpostit' notes, then added 'NoButton 1' etc to the style,
> David> only to always get buttons. Argh!
>
> I don't see how this affects UseDecor (I may be seeing wrong). I have
> XTerms with smaller titlebars and just did Style "XTerm" NoButton 1
> then Recapture in FvwmConsole and it seemed to take effect.

Fair enough. Its probably not the UseDecor per se.
Anyone care to show me the error of my ways in this simple snippet:
(mainly for me to learn how to use all 'this new fangled' stuff)

AddToDecor notes
+ BorderStyle Simple -- HiddenHandles NoInset
+ TitleStyle -- flat
+ HilightColor white black
+ WindowFont -b&h-lucida-bold-r-normal-*-*-180-*-*-p-*-iso8859-*

Style "notes" UseDecor notes, HandleWidth 1, Sticky, Color black/white
Style "notes" Icon Notes.xpm, NoButton 1, NoButton 3, NoButton 5, NoButton 7

Style "XPostit" UseStyle notes

This is pretty much per the man page example (under AddDecor).
Now, the problem is that this (and many variations of it) leaves me with a
custom decor window, but with all the buttons on it. Er..why?
And no icon either come to think of it.
