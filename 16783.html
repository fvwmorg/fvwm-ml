I don't have an alpha, but I do have a DECstation running Ultrix, and I
just wanted to let you know that my icons are working just fine, in case
that gives you some encouragement. I say this because Ultrix (even 4.3,
which is where we stopped upgrading), is pretty hokey. Lots of stuff
doesn't work right. The Alpha boxes run OSF or something, don't they?

Ultrix was spent getting OSF right, so I doubt that it's your
workstation.

Why did you compile it without m4? I compiled everything in, and I have
found that m4 is semi-handy. Maybe if you re-build it, you will find
some detail that you inadvertently skipped the first time. That's all I
can say.
