On 10 Jul 2000 15:33:09 +0100, Martin Eyles wrote:
>
> I'm just starting in the world of .fvwm2rc configuration files, and am
> trying to get buttons on my button bar to open up remote logins with the
> xrsh protocol, and to run netscape and nedit. I am also trying to put
> these in my menu (left click on the desktop) too.

I suppose running xrsh via a corresponding submenu of your root menu works
for you. To run netscape, add it to one of your menus (say, Utilities).

> However the buttons don't work. Can you help?
> The .fvwm2rc file is attached

You use an incorrect syntax. The correct syntax for a single button is:

*FvwmButtons(1x1, Icon mini-term.xpm, Action `Exec exec xrsh mimoza`)

You can use many other options, like Title together or instead of Icon,
see 'man FvwmButtons' for details.
