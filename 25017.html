    Dominik, I tested your configuration and it worked marvelously.
    My FvwmButton has width of 48 pixels and is vertical with whole height of screen and I changed your function a little to my
1152x864 screen and new location (at right hand side border) to

AddToFunc showhidebuttons
  + "I" next (FvwmButtons) move 1149p 0p
  + "I" current (FvwmButtons) move 1104p 0p

    So my worthy space grew up 46 pixels :))))) . I see 2 pixels to
get buttons back and I don't need to limit my
maximize to get a "visible" maximum :)). Now I have my whole screen
back to me :)
    I have swallowed apps and I think they had no problems (astime/push buttons/xpostit/iconman/pager) after these changes.
