> Thanks very much, this works great. One minor improvement I could make
> is if I could specify a region of the screen that FVWM should not place
> any windows in.. is that possible?
>
> The reason is that I use SLS mode on 2 monitors that have different
> resolutions, ...

There is now a better way. I just wrote a new command. With
XineramaSlsScreens you can define the geometry of your Sls
screens, e.g.

  XineramaSlsScreens 2 1024x768+0+0 1280x1024+1024+768

Just use this instead of XineramaSlsSize
