On Fri, 11 Apr 1997, Mark Krischer wrote:

> is there a way to get the side bars to allow resizing in the
> horizontal/vertical direction only, similar to windows and os/2?
>
> thanks in advance
>

I have the following lines in my .fvwmrc:

Mouse 1 FS A Resize-or-Raise

AddToFunc Resize-or-Raise "I" Raise
+ "M" Resize
+ "D" Lower

Which describe the behaviour you want.
