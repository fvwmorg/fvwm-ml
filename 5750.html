On Wed, Aug 07, 2002 at 09:21:55PM +0500, Johann Oskarsson wrote:
>
> Hello, I'm sort of new to fvwm and don't know anything about anything.
> Thanks, guys (if you who make it are reading this).
>
> What I want to know is how I make the AutoRaise module start automatically
> when I log in to X.

Place the lines

  AddToFunc StartFunction
  + I FvwmAuto 500

in you $HOME/.fvwm2rc file. Although its very big, the man page
is a good source of fvwm related information, but don't hesitate
to ask questions on the mailing list - that's its main purpose.
