On Thu, Nov 13, 2003 at 10:34:57AM -0700, Toshi Isogai wrote:
> When I invoke open office 1.1 swriter, it opens as shaded. I don't
> specify shaded window anywhere in config files. I don't even have shaded
> command in my menus. I have to issue "windowshade false" from
> FvwmConsole to open it up. It only happens on swriter but not other
> programs in open office or anything else.
>
> Does anybody have any idea?

Try

  Style OpenOffice* GNOMEIgnoreHints
  Style OpenOffice* EWMHIgnoreStackingOrderHints
  Style OpenOffice* EWMHIgnoreStateHints
  Style OpenOffice* EWMHIgnoreStrutHints
  Style OpenOffice* NoPPosition
  Style OpenOffice* NoUsposition
  Style OpenOffice* FixedPPosition

(probably a subset will do).
