On Sat, 17 Nov 2001, Riswick, J.G.A. van wrote:

> When I use the 'desk guide' from the gnome panel,
> the contents of fvwm2 desktops is not displayed
> in the desk guide (mini windows)
> except for the contents of the current desk.
> Also, a desk is only displayed when there is actually
> a window on the desk, otherwise not.
>
> Is there some way to set up fvwm (or the desk guide)
> so that this works better?

Have you considered simply using FVWM's Pager instead of the GNOME one?
You could have your .fvwm2rc launch an FvwmPager, and then have it
swallowed into a GNOME Panel. I do this myself. Frankly, I don't
understand why people use the Desk Guide... it has virtually 0 features.
Namely, it doesn't have the one FVWM Pager feature I can't live without:
dragging windows off a pager area and into the current page.
