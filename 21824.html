I think I may have failed to mention an important point in my original
message: I was moving the window in the little desktop view provided by
FvwmPager. I clicked and held the window's representation in the desktop
view, then moved it from one portion of the virtual desktop to another,
except that I moved quickly and FvwmPager exhibited a bit of sluggishness,
and when I let go of the mouse, whoop -- the window was nowhere to be seen.

I've had this kind of thing happen more than once. Usually I kill the
process involved. (But this time it was a window on another machine in which
a process' output was going, and I was hoping to see the output :-).)

It may be related to load; it only happens to me when FvwmPager is responding
very sluggishly (window movements inside the pager window have hysteresis),
which happens when the workstation is loaded in general.
