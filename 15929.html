<this is a bit of a nit but it has happened enough to warrant a post>

Would it be a problem if the titlebar button action took place on a
button release (ButtonUp) event rather than a button press (ButtonDown)
event?

The problem is that if I click on a titlebar button (Iconify is the
typical culprit) and the button happens to be sitting over the pager,
the window gets iconified as it should and the pager switches to a new
desktop/viewport! The pager reacts to ButtonUp events not ButtonDown.

I have to be real quick on the mouse to have the ButtonUp event occur in
the window being iconified and not in the Pager.
