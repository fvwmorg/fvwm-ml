Andrew Morelock <amorelock_at_onblur.org> writes:
> I am funning FVWM on an embedded install of RedHat that utilizes a touch
> screen and is using Mozilla as it's primary UI. I am looking for a way to
> disable the drag feature that most if not all window managers allow.
>
> In mozilla a user has the ability to drag images out of pages and cause
> major problems to the user-experience.
>
> Thoughts anyone?

Yes.

I believe your problem has nothing to do with the window manager.
The only part of Fvwm that has anything to do with dragging that
I know about is FvwmDragWell which is an optional module which
I don't believe you are running.

I just dragged a Mozilla image, and it just flew back to where
it started. I'm curious as to why thats a problem.

I think you might be able to disable drags by asking questions
on the Mozilla lists.
