Walter Dnes <waltdnes_at_waltdnes.org> writes:
> I've started off with the barest ~/.fvwm/.fvwm2rc file, and am
> building up from scratch. I notice that grabbing the sides or corners
> changes the cursor, but does not enable moving or re-sizing.
> I have to
> click on the leftmost button, and use the menu. This is probably due to
> my barebones .fvwm2rc file. What style commands should I use to enable
> moving/resizing by clicking with the cursor ? Even better, can somebody
> please point me to the appropriate manpage, so I can read up in depth ?

Did you remove the ConfigFvwmDefaults file?
The ability to resize a window by dragging on the corner
is in that file. The builtin commands are described in
the fvwm2 man page under "BUILT IN KEY AND MOUSE BINDINGS".
