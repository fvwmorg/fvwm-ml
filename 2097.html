> Anyway, I noticed that when I switch the console, then switch back, the
> color scheme is gone. All windows, menus and other backgrounds are black.
> Only some borders and shadings can be seen - none of them are the correct
> color. Can this be fixed with a config? I seem to have to reset the
> Xserver to get it working again. This does not happen with KDE. Is there
> another way to fix it without logging out?

Sounds like some kind of colormap issue -- the "Device" section of your
/etc/X11/XF86Config might be relevant.

I do not see that problem on Red Hat 6.2 with "Fvwm Version 2.2.4
compiled on Feb 4 2000 at 03:41:44" and this /etc/X11/XF86Config entry

# Device configured by Xconfigurator:

Section "Device"
    Identifier "Matrox|MGA G400 AGP"
    VendorName "Unknown"
    BoardName "Unknown"
    VideoRam 32768
    # Insert Clocks lines here if appropriate
EndSection
