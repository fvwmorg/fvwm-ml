On Thu, 24 Mar 2005 17:12:10 +0000, Thomas Adam
<thomas_at_edulinux.homeunix.org> wrote:
> On Thu, Mar 24, 2005 at 11:50:46PM +0800, Eric Sun wrote:
>
> > Some programs have more than two windows, like Gimp and MPlayer (one
> > for GUI, another the movie window). Take MPlayer as an example, I want
> > to decorate only the movie window and not the main GUI window since it
> > already has a titlebar. How to do this? Thanks!
>
> If you mean the main movie window of mplayer, then it should already be
> decorated by default. You can use FvwmIdent on the movie window to
> ascertain the class/resource name, although you want something along the
> lines of:
>
> Style *Mplayer* Title, Handles
>
> If you find *Mplayer* is too greedy a match, the use the classname or
> resourename returned from FvwmIdent.
