Prabhu Valiveti <Prabhu.Valiveti_at_dalsemi.com> writes:
> Hi,
> I am using fvwm 2.2.5 and seem to have successfully installed the
> 3.4k XPM lib (configure picks it up). I have also pointed ImagePath to the
> location of these .xpm files. However, when fvwm2 starts up, I do not see my
> specified icons for the buttons and the xterm styles.
>
> I had a very similar setup in 2.2.2 and my .fvwm2rc used to work (i used
> iconpath and pixmappath then)
>
> Can somebody help me out.
> Here are portions of my .fvwm2rc ---
>
> ModulePath /proj/i2common/prabhu/fvwm-2.2.5/libexec/fvwm/2.2.5
> #PixmapPath /proj/i2common/prabhu/fvwm-2.2.5/icons
> #IconPath /proj/i2common/prabhu/fvwm-2.2.5/icons
> ImagePath /proj/i2common/prabhu/fvwm-2.2.5/icons
>
>
> # button definitions
> ############################################################################
> #
>
> #ButtonStyle 1 MiniIcon
> ButtonStyle 1 Pixmap mini.xterm.xpm
> ButtonStyle 3 Pixmap mini.bomb.xpm
> ButtonStyle 2 Pixmap mini.resize2.xpm
> #ButtonStyle 4 Pixmap mini.winXX-iconify.xpm
> ButtonStyle 4 Pixmap jresize.xpm
>
> Style "xterm" Icon xterm-sol.xpm, Color white/steelblue4
> Style "emacs" Icon xemacs.xpm, Color white/steelblue4
>
> I do not see even the buttons with the right icons.

You need to have actions bound to the buttons, like:

Mouse 0 3 N Close
