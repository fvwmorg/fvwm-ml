> Yes. Some look very good but the descriptions sound like it is much

It takes some! Have you seen this one yet?

http://fvwm.sourceforge.net/tmp/migo-desktop-20021122.png

Gorgeous! A serious incitement to envy, but also to settle down on a cold
winter evening and work out how it's done. It's not too hard, especially
with bits of the recipe built into the screenshot!


Have you discovered fvwm-themes yet? A good way to explore
possibilities, by playing with elements of the ready-made themes and
colorsets.
http://fvwm-themes.sourceforge.net/


FvwmConsole is a good way of experimenting with tweaks to your
desktop. Other ways are suggested in the FVWM FAQ


All those button bars etc can be set up to disappear at a key-stroke.



Good luck!
