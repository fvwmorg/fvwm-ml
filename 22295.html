Hi,

Currently the windows in the WindowList list are sorted by
desk number and Z order.

I think it would be very useful to offer a sort-by-Most-
Recently-Used option.

With a MRU list, one can flash back-and-forth between the current
and last window in different desks simply by selecting the top
entry of the window list. One can also go down further in the
list to select windows visited less recently.

Even better, with an option to suppress the WindowList title,
one can simply click mouse 2 on the desktop to flash back.

-kin
