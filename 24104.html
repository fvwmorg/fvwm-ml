%% Dan Espen <dane_at_mk.telcordia.com> writes:

  de> For me, "gnumake", or "gmake" installs by default as "make".
  de> (I admit, I don't like that, and I rename it "gnumake".)

As with any properly constructed autoconf'd package, you can use the
various options --program-prefix, --program-suffix, or
--program-transform-name with configure to modify the installation names
of all installed binaries.

In your case, "configure --program-prefix=g" (or "--program-prefix=gnu")
should do the trick.

If that doesn't work, it's a bug; please let me know.
