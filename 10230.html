On 13 Oct 2003 00:22:36 +0200, Marcin G?ogowski wrote:
>
> Thank You very much for the previous answers.
> I have a little problem with my middle mouse button.
> I have bind the button with WindowList, but when I release the middle
> mouse button the WindowList is turned into a window with titlebar.
> I'm using such command to bind the mouse button:
> Mouse 2 A A WindowList

First, "Mouse 2 A A" is a pretty bad binding, you will not be able to use
the middle button in any application completely. Try:

  Mouse 2 A A - # restore middle button in applications
  Mouse 2 A SM WindowList # Shift=Alt-middle button anywhere
  Mouse 2 R A WindowList # middle button in the root

Second, I am afraid you should buy a new mice, since its middle button
sends duplicate clicks.
