Hello,

I encountered an issue with GNU Krell Monitor (http://gkrellm.net), when
using multiple pages per desktop. If GKrellM is started on an other page
than the top left (or northwest), the main window disappears shortly
after startup. It seems to be related to the XShapeCombineMask function,
as after commenting gtk_widget_shape_combine_mask in the gkrellm
sources, gkrellm works even when started on the other pages.
