> See "CursorStyle" in fvwm(1).
>
> BTW, if you use a shell in genuine xterm(1), then you can change
> cursor colors/shape in Xresources...
>
> XTerm.VT100*foreground: rgb:af/af/a0
> XTerm.VT100*background: rgb:12/00/20
> XTerm.VT100*cursorColor: darkorange1
> XTerm.VT100*pointerColor: rgb:00/00/00
> XTerm.VT100*pointerColorBackground: rgb:b0/b0/00
>
