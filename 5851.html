Mikhael Goikhman <migo_at_homemail.com> writes:
> On 14 Aug 2002 19:57:37 +0800, Seng Huat Yeoh wrote:
> >
> > When I maximise a window, it gets maximised to the entire available
> > dimension of my screen. Hence covers up my taskbar as well.
> >
> > How do I prevent it from covering my taskbar?
>
> Someone should create a FAQ entry for this.
>
> Instead of Maximize use "Maximize 100 -30p" where 30 is the width of
> your FvwmTaskBar.
>
> Or use EWMHBaseStrut in 2.5.x.

Got it.
