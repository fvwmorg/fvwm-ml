I'm trying to minimize my time, while trying to maximize my I/O (:-)).
I'm so used to Wintel's Alt+Space shortcut to show
Window-Ops I;m trying to implement it in Linux.
I issued a command:

Key Space A M Menu Window-Ops

I also looked into /usr/include/X11/keysymdef.h and I think
(thought) I found a key for that -- `space` I tried -it didn't
work. I checked to see if the whole combination was
right, I substitutted F1 for space -- it worked.
And now I have a plea to anyone that uses `space` short
cut -- *** what key do you use ??? ***
