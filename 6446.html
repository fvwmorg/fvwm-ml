Greetings! I've recently returned to fvwm after a few years absence -
it's even more fun than I remembered. 2.5.3 on Debian Woody.

I'm trying to branch out a little with FvwmButtons; appended below is a
specimen which will undoubtedly make you laugh or cry! The four
identical buttons are just for experiment at this stage ;-) The specific
error I get from this is

"MyBar2: unmatched END in config file"


It looks to me like the END is matched - can anyone tell me what is
missing?

Is there anything else missing? ie what is a reasonable minimum
configuration for a small panel to fire up four programs?




8<------8<------8<------8<------8<------8<------8<------8<------8<------

FvwmButtons MyBar2

*MyBar2:Geometry +0+500
*MyBar2:BoxSize smart
*MyBar2:Back red
*MyBar2:Fore white
*MyBar2:Rows 2
*MyBar2:ButtonGeometry 64x64
*MyBar2: (Icon /usr/local/src/AquaFusion/32x32/apps/xemacs.png, Action `Exec xemacs`)
*MyBar2: (Icon /usr/local/src/AquaFusion/32x32/apps/xemacs.png, Action `Exec xemacs`)
