Dear Educators:

Introducing MySchoolYears.com, a new Internet service where students can create their own web pages to showcase their academic achievements. Using a standard web browser, students can create web pages that contain a summary of their Academic Studies, Test Scores, Report Cards, Attendance Records, and Extra Curricular Activities for each school year - from Elementary through College.

Students can even upload their favorite school pictures and share their web pages with family, friends or anyone involved in their academic career. Teachers can even log-on and add comments. Parents will gain a better understanding of their child's academic progress when they help create the web pages.

Other features include:

  • Member Referral Program (Earn money through referrals)
  • Free Software Download (KIDdisk Child ID and Safety Software)
  • On-line Photo Contest (Students can enter Best Class Photo Contest)

MySchoolYears.com is an excellent technology project for schools, PTA fund-raiser or sponsorship opportunity for the school's Business Partners. We are planning to launch the program during the 2001 back-to-school time frame. Your school can participate in this promotional campaign and make the program available to all the students in your school.

Click here to learn more about this exciting new program. To see a sample web page, Click DEMO. The user name and password have already been entered. Click SEARCH. When the student's record is displayed, click THIRD GRADE (This sample page has already been created.)

If you have any questions or need order information, please feel free to contact us.
