On 03 Mar 2003 18:50:36 +0100, christopher peirs wrote:
>
> i have a problem with my menus.
>
> as you can see in the screenshot (http://www.lazef.be/ec2003/transfer/menu.jpg) i have a white vertical border on the most leftside of my menus.
> the width of this white part seems to be function of the total width of the menu
>
> this is my menustyle-part of my .fvwm2rc:
>
> # Colors
> Colorset 0 Pixmap Green-Menu2.xpm, fg green
> Colorset 1 fg seashell2, bg #808080
> Colorset 2 fg black, bg #F0F0F0
> Colorset 3 fg green, bg "dark green"
>
> # Menu
> MenuStyle * Win
> MenuStyle * ItemFormat "%2.4s%.1|%.5i%.5l%.5l%.5r%.5i%2.3>%1|"
> MenuStyle * MenuColorset 0
> MenuSTyle * ActiveColorset 3
> Menustyle * Font "xft:Arial:size=10:encodig=iso8859-15;"
> MenuStyle * SideColor "dark green"
>
> anybody has any ideas what i did wrong? or how to fix it?

Load Green-Menu2.xpm into your text editor and remove the one pixel line
(one or more chars on every encoded line), don't forget to decrease the
pixmap width in the header. Or use a graphical editor to edit the image
if this is easier for you.
