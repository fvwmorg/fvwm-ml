>>>>> Patrick Haller writes:

  Patrick> In the FVWM faq, it says that your page size should be the
  Patrick> same size as your physical screen size. But with my Linux
  Patrick> box with cirrus 5434 and infiniti monitor, the page size is
  Patrick> 20% larger than the physical screen size. Anyone know how
  Patrick> to change your page size FVWM95-2?

I wager 5 guilders that you're using a graphics card which does a
virtual screen in hardware. If there is memory left over for
additional pictures some graphics cards provide a panning feature
(pan if mouse touches edge of screen) in order to look at the
additional pictures. Of course, Fvwm knows nothing about this
hardware feature. Turn it off in your hardware or X setup.
