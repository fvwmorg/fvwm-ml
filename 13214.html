On Thu, Sep 02, 2004 at 04:51:53PM +0200, Felix E. Klee wrote:
> Hi Chris,
>
> On Thu, 3 Jun 2004 14:24:29 +0200 Chris Rouch wrote:
> > I want the gimp to start on desk 3, so I have the following
> > configuration:
> >
> > Style "gimp-2.0" StartsOnDesk 3 SkipMapping
> >
> > The splash screen goes to desk 3 without altering my current desk.
> > However as soon as the main application appears my desk is switched, so
> > it looks like the SkipMapping is being ignored.
> >
> > Does anyone know how to fix this?
>
> Did you ever solve that problem. I find it quite annoying.

Put

  BugOpts ExplainWindowPlacement on

in your fvwm config file (2.5.10 or later). Fvwm then prints a
detailed explanation of why and how new windows are placed. The
output should enable us to find out what's going on.

By the way, I just checked the sources, and there is no way fvwm
ignores the SkipMapping style (unless it's overridden by another
style).
