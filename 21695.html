Hi guys!

I love FVMW2, and I brag about it to everyone I meet. EdgeResistance
is one of the bragging points -- how easy it is to line a window up
against the edge; but there is one feature of EdgeResistance which I
think could be improved.

I run:

  EdgeScroll 0 0
  EdgeResistance 0 100

I occasionally wish to move a window partially off the desktop, but
when I overcome the 100 pixel EdgeResistance, the window jumps those
100 pixels. I would love it if the window simply started moving again
once the EdgeResistance was overcome. That way I could drag it 125
pixels in order to place it 25 pixels off screen. Can this be done?
