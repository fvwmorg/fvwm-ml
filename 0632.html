I have xautolock working with fvwm on Solaris 2.6.
You set it up in the Xsession.fv2 file right before the
window manager something like:

        /usr/local/X11/bin/autolock -time 10 -locker '/usr/openwin/bin/xlock
-allowroot -mode life' &
        /usr/openwin/bin/xconsole -iconic -geometry +2-2 &
        /usr/local/bin/fvwm2
