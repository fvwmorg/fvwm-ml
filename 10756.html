I don't understand how apt-get may work at all, does not it require an
updated list of packages to start to work, how did you define this list?
Unless I miss some apt-get repository that features fvwm-2.4.17 rpm,
do not use apt-get, it can't help you here.

Amyway, just install one of the libstroke rpms that you may find on the

You may find one of the libstroke rpms on our rpm page, should work with
any RedHat version.
