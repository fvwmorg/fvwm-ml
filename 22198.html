The gnome people are inviting anyone interested in
window managers to subscribe to their list (gnome-list_at_gnome.org)
and give input -- currently, the icewm author has some proposals
for hints that most people seem to agree are needed. The gnome
people also say that x session management is a necessary addition
and someone has offered to help window manager authors implement
it. I think it's necessary that this gets added -- icewm is the
only wm so far that is working on x session management.
