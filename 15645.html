Eric F. Kahler (ekahler_at_mars.superlink.net) writes:

> The following binds the ms$ left window key to the Kanji keysym. Any keysym
> will do (though it should be unused by your system -- look at keysymdef.h) as
> long as the same names are used on the command line and in .fvwmrc.
>
> xmodmap -ev "keycode 115 = Kanji"
>
> Key Kanji A A Popup "Utilities"

You can bind to all three of the keys on the MSWin keyboard, I used to
have all three keys bound to menus and if I remember correctly the
keycodes were consecutive... if it is of any help you may actually
want to use a high function key, I had them bound to (from left to
right) F33, F34 and F35. You might want one of the keys to start the
WindowList... In addition to xkeycaps also man xev which also helps
debugging keycodes.

PS. Also I had Alt-Space bound to give the popup window menu.
