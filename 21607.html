                                                       Breaking News Press Release
                                                          AMERICA INVADED!
        
                                              The USA goes to WAR January 31st!

                 WORLD'S TOP COMMANDOS CHALLENGE THE US MILITARY ELITE
                       Man to Man...Woman to Woman!...Hand to Hand!
        
        January 31st will be a day to be hammered into the annals of history as a contingent of world military warriors converge on the Crown Coliseum in Fayetteville, North Carolina for the USA vs WORLD combat event.

        A team of the USA's top military formerly active in the SEALs, Marines, Army and Air Force will defend their turf, Hand to Hand, in eleven action packed bouts against a relentless assault from a crack group of world commandos from Russia, the Middle East, Asia, Canada, Europe and Cuba.

        Specially developed fight rules designed for combat hardened veterans will ensure an intensity never before seen, throughout the eleven fights.

 Each bout challenges fighters to three 1 1/2 minute rounds in a pentagon shaped ring.

        The world team is fully loaded with some of the most aggressive fighters on the globe and America has jumped at the challenge.

         Hundreds of applications have flooded the promoter's office. "Americans are patriotic, disciplined and determined to defeat the world team... this is going to be unbelievable!" says promoter Don Arnott. "I never expected this kind of response, everyone's in an uproar except the fighters, they just want to get down to it!"

        The event is available worldwide via satellite, providers expect unprecedented audience numbers due to the uniqueness of this event. Says one excited fight fan, "I seriously hope Americans know what their in for, this is a dream come true for some of the world team guys, it will be a night to remember!"

       New scoring format where only knockdowns count (just the way commandos like it) guarantee action, intensity and courage rarely seen in the ring. This will be one of the most exciting fighting events you will ever see!

               USA vs World is sanctioned by the North Carolina State Boxing Commission.
