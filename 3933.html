On 04 Jan 2002 10:28:42 +0100, Pier Paolo Glave wrote:
>
> I have a little problem with FvwmScript's "MiniScroll" objects (for
> example, the ones used to change Hours and Minutes in FvwmScript-Date
> example.)
>
> The problem is that it is quite difficult to make a mouse click short
> enough to change the object's value by 1 unit only.
> With some kinds of mouses this is even impossible, and the shortest
> click makes the value increase/decrease at least by 2 units.
>
> Is there a way to configure the repetition rate or mouse sensitivity
> for this purpose?

No, this is not configurable, indeed there is a duplication when you use a
mouse. I will commit a fix in a few minutes.

But in the latest versions (check out 2.4.4) you may also use a keyboard
arrows to change the value in +1/-1. Pressing Tab or Shift-Tab circulates
through widgets.
