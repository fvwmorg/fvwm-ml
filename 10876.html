This works, but menus flicker pretty noticeable using this method.
They flicker much less with "RootTransparent buffer". Also "bg average"
does not work, so you can't automatically get good colors for 3d effect.
Also I feel that if I suggest to use "Tint" together with "Transparent"
(something that I don't really suggest) then we will get a lot of
questions, why this does not work at all with decorations and modules.
The wording in FAQ seems to be ok (but I will replace "possible to tint"
with "possible to efficiently tint"), FAQ should not replace the man
page, but provide useful hints and pointers.
