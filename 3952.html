  Let me start off by saying that yes, I do have "IgnoreModifiers L25"
in my .fvwm2rc file. I'm running fvwm 2.4.4. I can {ALT-TAB} between
programs with any combo of Caps/Num/Scroll Lock active. However, I
can't jump to a window by clicking on a title on FvwmIconMan if either
Num or Scroll Lock is active. Caps Lock does not interfere.

  I'm running a simple configuration. I want to build it up from
scratch, partly to have full control, partly to learn the guts of FVWM.
