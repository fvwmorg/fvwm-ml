> Hi!

> I wanted to display FvwmPager the desktops as a matrix, or
> at least vertically instead of horizontally. Is that at
> all possible? FvwmPagerRows/Columns doesn't seem to do
> what I expected...

Uhm, if you are talking about desktops, ...Rows and ...Columns
are the right setting. Try this:

  DestroyModuleConfig FvwmPager*
  *FvwmPagerRows 2
  *FvwmPagerColumns 3
  Module FvwmPager 0 5

If you are talking about pages, not desktops, you can set the
desktop geometry:

  DesktopSize 3x2

BTW, we have a nice mailing list also: fvwm_at_fvwm.org. Please do
not send configuration questions to the bug tracking system.
