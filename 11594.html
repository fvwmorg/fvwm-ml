2.5.8 had a problem for me of being slow.
2.5.9 shows the same problem.
2.5.7 is ok.
The menu background opens quick but then takes about a second to fill in.
Even w/ no .fvwm2rc, the default root menu takes a little while to fill in.
Switching desks is also slower than on 2.5.7

I'm running solaris 5.8 on an ultra 10, starting from xinit.

My config is...

env CXX=g++ CC=.../bin/gcc LDFLAGS=-s CFLAGS="-O3" ./configure --without-cxx \
--with-xpm-includes=.../X11/include --with-xpm-library=.../X11/lib
--disable-shape --disable-sm --disable-bidi --with-iconv-library=no --without-gnome

(... = path)
