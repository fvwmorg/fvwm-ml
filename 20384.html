Sorry, but I was not trying to respond hostilely to all critics, just
whiners that never contribute but just whine all the day long!

I love critics, but to be a true critic one has to suggest a plan of
action that will help resolve the issue. If they do not plan to
help, then they are just whiners!

If you want to help, great! I would love to, but I have to work for
a living and do not have the time (or probably the expertise). I
have contributed one small module that I do not know if anyone is
using (there seems to be no positive feedback on this list, just
whiners!).

Sorry. A of the posts caught me in a bad mood after listening to
people just whining all the time at work! It is never productive to
whine! Please, offer suggestion to get things working. Like
instead of one maintainer, split the tasks up like the Debian group
does among many maintainers. Many maintainers of different parts of
the code might make it harder to organize everything, but it might
keep one person from getting worked to death!
