OoO Lors de la soirée naissante du mercredi 29 décembre 2004, vers
18:50, Scott Rubin <slr2777_at_cs.rit.edu> disait:

> The second thing I want to do may be more difficult. I was watching
> someone use a powerbook and it has this really neat
> feature. Basically they push some key combination and the screen
> zooms way way out. Every open window is displayed with no
> overlapping. All the windows turn into seperate thumbnails and they
> all fit nicely in the screen. Then whichever thumbnail gets clicked
> becomes the top focused window. Is there a way to do this with Fvwm
> or even X11? It's really cool.

This is called Exposé. There are some attempts to mimic this with
X11. The best one is Skippy :
    <URL:http://thegraveyard.org/skippy.php>

Skippy-XD, the version for X.org, is able to do the same as Exposé in
Mac OS X. The non X.org version is unable, with fvwm, to paint
correctly windows outside the current screen and is pretty slow to
take snapshots.
