> You can even query the X server for the X_RESOLUTION (pixels
> per meter) and pick the font based on that.

Some caution is needed here. The report should be taken with a
grain of salt, since -- at least on a PC -- the X server may not
know the actual screen size. It knows the screen is, say, 1200
pixels high; but it does not know for sure whether those 1200
pixels are spread over 30 cm or only 20 cm.
