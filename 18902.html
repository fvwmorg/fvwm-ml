On Fri, 17 Jan 1997, Ludvig A. Norin wrote:

> If you move a window with WindowShade activated, and then deactivade
> WindowShade, and then try to use a menu on the menubar in the application,
> say any Tcl/Tk program or Netscape etc., the menu will pop up at the
> previous position of the window.
>
> Try it out :)

Yup, just tried it and saw it :-). I failed to heed the synthetic
ConfigureNotify, but I changed it to do so by sending a ConfigureNotify
when it is unshaded, which corrects the problem. Thanks for noting this.

The fix is in a new all changes patch:
http://www.rpi.edu/~veliaa/projects/fvwm_patches/dist/andrew2.2.0.44.diff.gz

The diff from the andrew.2.0.44.diff.gz is here (line is cut in two):
