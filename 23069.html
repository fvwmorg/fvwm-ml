I have the evrsion of Fvwm that came with Red Hat 5.1 installed, version
2.0.46 I think.

I have 2 xterms open. I did not fiddle around with my .fvwmrc file.
I left myself logged in and left the room. When I came back I realized
that xterm responded to mouse clicks (I guess that's really GPM) and
