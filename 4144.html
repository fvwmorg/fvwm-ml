If I do not use the ClickToFocus style (the Unix
approach) then RaiseLower raises the next window as it
should. If that is a small window that sits on top of
a larger one, then as soon as I move the mouse over
the larger one, the smaller window that was raised
will disappear again because of the `Unix approach`,
i.e. the lack of ClickToFocus. Is there a solution to
that without reverting to ClickToFocus?
