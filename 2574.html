On 09 May 2001 12:20:41 -0400, Amit Kumar wrote:
>
> I am a new user of FVWM and i would like to configure my desktop
> with "Click to focus" style rather than focus following the mouse
> pointer, Also Is there a way to make ALT+TAB switch focus between window
> and bring the relevent window in front (Similar to MS Windows)????

Add these lines to your .fvwm2rc file:

  Style "*" ClickToFocus
  Key Tab A M WindowList Root c c NoDeskSort

Please note, that in 2.2.x versions the line defining Alt-Tab does not
work on systems (like mine) where Tab generates ISO_Left_Tab. This is
fixed in 2.3.12.
