"Philip H. Jensen" <pjensen_at_columbus.rr.com> writes:
>
> By the way, speaking of mouse-free operation: I see how fvwm2 can be
> taught to make key combinations move the cursor - but can any keyboard
> action be made equivalent to a button click? (Just curiosity - I don't
> insist on _never_ touching the rodent - and indeed the cut and paste
> behavior is much nicer than Winblows.)

This has been brought up in the past.

Right now, fvwm2 doesn't have a way to convert keystokes into
button pushes. Even if it did, applications will be able to tell
the difference. (They are marked as "synthetic"). Some applications
may refuse to accept them. (Its a security issue.)

I would imagine that a patch would be accepted...
