Got this error:

ulmo_at_Q:~$ exmh&
[1] 23441
ulmo_at_Q:~$ -geometry 878x718+-1+-1: bad geometry specifier "878x718+-1+-1"

ulmo_at_Q:~$ egrep 878 .exmh*
.exmh-defaults:exmh.geometry: 878x718+-1+-1
.exmhaudit:Show inbox 2878
.exmhaudit:Rmm inbox Mh_Rmm inbox {887 883 882 881 880 879 878 877 876 875}
.exmhaudit:rmm +inbox 887 883 882 881 880 879 878 877 876 875
ulmo_at_Q:~$

So, Fvwm2 must have given it negative positions. Dunno where it all
came in. Note to FVWM people: Exmh saves what positions there are.
