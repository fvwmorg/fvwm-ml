I don't think that beeping has to do with fvwm at all.
In an xterm, type xset -b or xset b off or man xset.
Also, other than the bell there is the clicking too
which you may find annoying. If you are lucky then you
may disable clicking with the xset command as well.

p.s. I hate that bells as well.
