> Hello,

> I have just switched to fvwm2...
>
> I am trying to integrate the pager in the Buttonbar, the line I made is as
> follows:
>
> *FvwmButtons(4x2) - whatever Swallow "FvwmPager" Module FvwmPager 0 0
>
> As I understood this from the man page this should display my pager in a
> box 4 times standard icon length and 2 times as high as standard icon.
>
> I have two rows declared, and the pager button is the last button. In this
> configuration the pager is drawn in a single height 8xlength box, quite
> squashed. It works only if I put the pager as one of the firt items. I find
> this a bit odd, since I prefer the pager in the right lower corner (I HAVE
> enough buttons to push it there...)
>
> --
If you use a "Container" to put all your buttons is you will effectively have
a button large enough to override the placement of the Pager as the biggest
button. That is, assuming you have 2.0.42. The details of how to handle
containers are in the manual page for FvwmButtons.
