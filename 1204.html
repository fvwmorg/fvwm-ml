How dumb of me. I had my MANPATH set for the Sun network, so I wasn't
getting a fvwm2 man page. I changed my .bash_profile to now read:

case `uname` in
        Linux) ;;
        SunOS) export MANPATH=/home/dehn/richter/man:/usr/tex/man:/usr/net/man:/usr/man:/usr/local/man:/usr/openwin/share/man
esac

and now I can read the 3383 line long fvwm2 man page!
