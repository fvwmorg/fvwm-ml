On Wed, Jan 12, 2000 at 09:24:36PM -0500, tt_at_bu.edu wrote:
>
> What happened in 2.2.4 distribution to the TaskBar module that used to be in
> 2.2.2 and is used, for example, by AnotherLevel?

Nothing. The TaskBar wasn't modified between 2.2.2 and 2.2.4.
If you don't have a specific reason to upgrade from 2.2.2 to
2.2.4 you can as well stick with your old fvwm. There were
only a few minor bug fixes that don't justify upgrading if
you didn't encounter specific problems.

> Or I do I get an autohiding taskbar in 2.2.2?

If it were auto hiding you could still see it. Probably the
task bar doesn't start up properly. Can you find an error
message on the console? Can you see it with 'ps'?
