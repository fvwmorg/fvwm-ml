I recently got FVWM 2.0.45 up on my Sun Ultra2 with Solaris 5.

Since then, I have been having a problem with communication between
GNU Emacs 19.34 and Netscape 3.0.

When reading mail with VM, I used to be able to click the middle mouse
button on a URL and it would send a message to netscape to open that
URL. Since moving from olvwm (yuck!) to FVWM (yay!), this hasn't
worked. I get a message "Sending URL to Netscape... Done." but nothing
actually happens.

Again, as far as I know, the only change has been in the window manager!
