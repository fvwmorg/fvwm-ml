On 26 Aug 2001 11:08:26 -0700, Xucaen wrote:
>
> > Bindings used by FvwmPager are not configurable as of now.
> > Normally you need 2 clicks, one to change viewport and one to focus.
> >
> > But you may do this using only one click if you use:
> >
> > *FvwmPager: SloppyFocus
> >
> > SloppyFocus in FvwmPager was introduced in fvwm-2.3.7.
>
> Why was this changed in the first place? I am using FVWM 2.2.4 and the
> pager with only one click to change viewport and focus. I like it that
> way.

Mouse bindings used in FvwmPager in 2.4.0 are equivalent to ones in 2.2.x.
In both versions you need one click (button 2) to focus a window and another
click (any button, but they work differently) to change viewport.

Please reread the original message in this thread.
