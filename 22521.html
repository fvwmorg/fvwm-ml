I'm new to Linux and I'm running Redhat 5.1 (i386) with fvwm2/fvwm95.
The default fvwm setup produces xload, a pager titled "Desk", and a
pager titled "Apps" in the lower right corner (both 4X4). I'm not sure
what the "Apps" pager is for (I assume for applications), and I really
don't want it there, just xload and "Desk". I've grepped through what
seems like the appropriate directories and have found only the following
lines in /etc/X11/AnotherLevel/fvwm2rc.modules.m4:

*FvwmPagerLabel 0 Desk
*FvwmPagerLabel 1 Apps

So I commented out the second line and now I have a "Desk" pager and a
"Desk 1" pager. Can someone tell me how to remove the "Apps" pager.
