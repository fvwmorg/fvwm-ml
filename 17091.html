Now this is the 3rd dimension in the Goto hierarchy:

        Goto ({ Absolute | Relative } { Display | Desk | Page })+

and the 4th dimension in the positioning hierarchy
(if we don't count the internet address components - domain/host):

        Display Desk Page Geometry
