I've just built Fvwm. In Fvwm.tmpl, I set:

#define FvwmBinDir /use/ask/u4/havardf/bin/alpha
#define FvwmDir /use/ask/u4/havardf/lib/X11/fvwm2
#define FvwmLibDir /use/ask/u4/havardf/lib
#define FvwmManPath /use/ask/u4/havardf/man

i.e.: I replaced /local/X11R5 with /use/ask/u4/havardf.

However, I got problems building, and it turned out that all the
Makefiles had BINDIR, MANPATH and USRLIBDIR set with /local/X11R5
instead of /use/ask/u4/havardf.

Has anyone else experienced this?
