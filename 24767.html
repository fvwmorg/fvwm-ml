Alright you are just making this to hard try something like

netscape -xrm "*Navigator.maxHeight: 200" -xrm "*Navigator.maxWidth: 400" -xrm "*Navigator.minHeight: 200" -xrm "*Navigator.minWidth: 400"

You could also put thos values in your .Xdefaults file.
