        Just a minor beef, but remember; since the pager has been ripped out
of the fvwm2 executable (at least I think it was.. :) you can COUNT on a
FvwmPager process, and most likely a FvwmButtons process as well.

        Also, I found a small buglet. The fvwm2 man page says that the
UseStyle directive takes a style arguement in quotes, ala:
Style "Invis" NoTitle,Sticky,WindowListSkip
Style "Fvwm*" UseStyle "Invis"

However, that doesn't work. You must do:
Style "Fvwm*" UseStyle Invis
