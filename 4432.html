Hi,

I would suggest adding an extra bit into your
$HOME/.bash_profile file, thus:

export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/usr/lib/ && ldconfig &>
/dev/null 2>&1 &


(N.B. The above should all be on one-line)

HTH,

Thomas Adam
