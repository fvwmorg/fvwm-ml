On Thu, Jul 22, 2004 at 02:53:55AM +0200, Jan Kanty Palus wrote:
> When I use mplayer in fvwm, with x11 or xvidix video output in
> fullscreen mode, a strange stripe appears at the bottom of the
> screen. It is about 2-3 pixels and has color of background
> color/wallpaper. Is this a fvwm related problem (in other WMs it's ok)
> or should I report it to mplayer mailing list?

I think mplayer has set some hints that forbid its window having
the exact size of the screen. It may have a specific aspect ratio
hint set that does not match the width/height of your screen, or
it has a resize step size that does not fill up your screen
exactly. This should be reported to the mplayer folks.
