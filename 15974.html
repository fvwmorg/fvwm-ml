Hi all,

I've defined the following in my .fvwmrc (using 2.0.40):

        Key Muhenkan A A PopupList WindowList
        
        AddToFunc PopupList "I" CursorMove -1000 +1000
        + "I" WindowList


And it works. . . sort of.

When I press the desired key, the window list does appear in the desired
location (lower left hand corner), but I then have to make 2 key depresses to
do anything. i.e. I have to press the number corresponding to the window name
twice to focus on the chosen window, or I have to press ESC twice to get rid
of the WindowList.

Any ideas as to how I can get this down to one key depress.

Thanks,
Eric.
