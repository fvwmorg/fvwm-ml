On 17 Feb 2004 15:52:35 -0800, Robert Knighten wrote:
>
> >From time to time I loose track of the location of the mouse cursor, and may
> have trouble locating it. Windows XP has a handy feature whereby pressing the
> control key produces a momentary animation pointing to the mouse cursor. Does
> someone have a vaguely similar facility using fvwm? Anything that will draw
> attention to the cursor (e.g. flash it, put a box around it, ???) would be
> good, and any short key sequence will do.

Take a look at FAQ #7.16.

Or maybe even:

  Key Super_L A N Exec xmessage -name "SmallBlob" -bg red -fg white -nearmouse -timeout 1 -button "" -xrm '*cursorName: none' -xrm '*borderWidth: 2' -xrm '*borderColor: yellow' -xrm '*Margin: 12' 'I am here!'

Mikhael.
