"Frank Wilcox, Jr." <fewilcox_at_unity.ncsu.edu> writes:

> i've seen several posts about problems w/ edge-scrolling. What is
> edge-scrolling? My screen NEVER scrolls, so i have no clue what u guys are
> talking about. (Plus, i just got fvwm2 about a month ago and have had NO
> time to play w/ it).
>
> can it be explained in a few words? or is it something i should look up
> somewhere? (again, i'm a relative newbie).

The pre-condition is that you have configured fvwm2 to give you a virtual
desktop. E.g. ``DeskTopSize 2x3'' gives you a virtual screen 2
physical screens wide and 3 physical screens high.

The ``fvwm2'' man page covers all the details. Search for ``desktopsize'',
``edgescroll'' and ``edgeresistance''.

My personal setup includes:

## Virtual desktop size in units of the physical screens.
DeskTopSize 2x3

## Amount of Resistance for moving from desk to desk with the
## mouse ... 0 0 allows for only window moves and resizes to
## reach into the next desk. mSec, pixels
EdgeResistance 500 150

## Percentage of a page (horizontal, vertical) to scroll when the
## cursor reaches the edge of a page ... 0 0 turns off scrolling
EdgeScroll 100 100
