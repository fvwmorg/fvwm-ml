On 28 Apr 2001 11:58:56 -0500, Larry W. Irwin Sr wrote:
>
> I am trying to install FVWM 2.3 but am missing a library. I need
> librplay.so. I went to fvwm.org and downloaded the librplay rpm but
> it did not contain the library. I see the rplay daemon starting up at
> boot time. What's up?

I am not sure I understand the question. Do you speak about ./configure
script reporting that FVWM will not have rplay support? Or something else?

This rplay-3.3.1-1 rpm includes /usr/lib/librplay.a, which should be
enough to build FVWM with rplay support. You don't need librplay.so.

You may need to rerun ./configure (and probably remove config.cache file).
Anyway rplay support is not really needed, it is fully optional.
