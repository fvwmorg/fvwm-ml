Could you make two options for it so it will have a 2x2 effect?

CurrentDesk - Shows only windows from current desk, otherwise all desks.
Iconized - Shows iconized windows as well as non-iconized windows, otherwise
just non-iconized windows.

This way, someone could pick and choose whether they wanted iconized/non and
current/all windows displayed.
