John Smith <ludwicza_at_telus.net> writes:
>
>
> Hi, I have a question... I installed wm-icons, and my ImagePath is set like
> so:
>
> ImagePath \
> /usr/local/share/icons/wm-icons/menu:/usr/local/share/icons/wm-icons/norm
>
> However, a lot of the icons have the exact same names, so how am I supposed
> to use just the norm section of icons for regular icons and the menu
> section for menu icons? Is there a way to do that? Like be able to
> specify norm/terminal.xpm or menu/terminal.xpm?

Exactly.

Make you imagepath /usr/local/share/icons/wm-icons

then refer to the icons as norm/terminal.xpm and menu/terminal.xpm.
