        OK, I grabbed a quick screen capture of piewm with a pie menu up.
It's a bit large (194K, 1024x768 desktop) to post to the list, so I put it
up on my Web site temporarily. If you want to grab it, just fetch

                http://www.pcnet.com/~proteus/Fvwm/Pie-desk1.jpg

        It's not much to look at. I've never bothered doing any serious
configuration of piewm, as I don't really use it: I just bring it up
occasionally for the novelty. Anyway, there's a pie menu in the upper
right hand corner, just below the icon manager box that's still labelled
"TWM" (I *told* you I didn't do any configuration.)

        Now, lest you set yourself up for disappointment...the "pies" aren't
round. They're conventional rectangles, but they're subdivided as though
someone were cutting a pie: the menu selections fan out from the center,
each occupying a "slice" of this rectangular pie. ("Pie are square...???
Son, what the hell they been teachin' you at that fancy college?")
