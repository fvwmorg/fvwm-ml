The problem I'm experiencing is with using FvwmPager to switch desktop
views. I'll button1 click on Pager to switch, move the mouse to where
the window I want will be drawn, watch the window get drawn in, but
NOT get the focus despite having the mouse in it. I have to move the
mouse out of the window and back in _once_ to get it to focus. This
has happened with emacs, netscape, xterms, xv, and several other
clients.

This bug was definately introduced in 2.0.44. I never saw before with
2.0.43.
