On 09 Dec 2002 20:16:51 -0500, Timothy Groves wrote:
>
> Is there a way to cause a window to unshade when the mouse is hovered
> over the title bar? Also, if there is, is there a way to cause the
> window to re-shade when focus is lost?

With SloppyFocus, put this your in menus or StartFunction:

  FvwmAuto 200 "Silent WindowShade off" "Silent WindowShade on"

Although this does exactly what you want and kind of fancy,
I am not sure this is very usable.

  KillModule FvwmAuto
