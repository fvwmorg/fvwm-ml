> I use fvwm-themes w/ fvwm and am curious to know how I can switch to
> label1 1-3 in FvwmPager-Desker. Normally in the default desktop there is
> a "main" desk and you can move windows to desk 1,2,3. But how can I show
> desk 1,2 or 3 *without* moving a program to it? I can't seem to be able

The subject of this message puzzles me. If you have FvwmPager-Desker
(that is a pager with usually 4 desks, NxM pages each) you may just click
in it. I suppose you speak about FvwmPager-Single (shows one desk only).

Please see FAQ to learn about main default bindings. Ctrl-Alt-1/2/3/4 may
be used to switch between different desks, there are bindings to switch to
different pages inside a desk too. Alternativelly press Shift-Ctrl-Mouse2
on the root to get transient FvwmPager-Desker to switch to any desk/page.
Or if you want this desker on the screen persistently, enable it in the
Themes / Current / modules menu.
