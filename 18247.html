Eli -

        Whew! A different topic. "For this relief, much thanks."

> move a window...to the edge of the screen...flush against the border...click
> as though...to grab the resize handle...get a root menu.

        This works fine on my system (Linux, AcceleratedX server, fvwm 2.0.43).
I'd be very surprised if fvwm were the culprit here. I would suspect first that
your monitor might be a little out of adjustment: that there is a tiny bit
of screen real estate that doesn't show, and that the window only appears to be
flush against the edge. Could this be the case? I can readily make it happen on
my Mag monitors by playing with the resizing controls.

        When you click, do you see the "I'm-on-the-edge" or "I'm on the corner"
cursors, or the default desktop cursor (perhaps partially obscured)? Or, can
you verify that the x,y coordinates are really at the edge of the screen as
viewed by X & fvwm, not by your eyes? (God, I hope I don't sound condescending!
Been reading too much stuff with an unpleasant tone to it around here lately.)

        Good luck!
        Bob
