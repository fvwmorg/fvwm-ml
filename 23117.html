> Where should I actually put my bug reports? To the fvwm-workers mailing
> list or at the bug tracking system on the web page?

Bugs should be reported in Jitterbug. If you pick an item from
list, you should report that fact to the list and tell us what
exactly you tested where (the test setup). I want not only to know
what went wrong but what was tested successfully too. If you find
lots of bugs you may want to post a short overview to the list.
But in the end all bug reports (hopefully) make it into Jitterbug
and it's better if you hack them in directly (otherwise I'll have
a boring cut-and-paste session).
