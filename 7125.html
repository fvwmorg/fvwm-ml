> get the following error.
>
> #rpm -Uvh /usr/src/redhat/RPMS/i386/fvwm-2.5.6-1.i386.rpm

Please don't name the release 1, there is no final 2.5.6.
Just leave the default 0.20021203 when doing "make rpm-dist".

> error: Failed dependencies:
> perl(Gtk) is needed by fvwm-2.5.6-1
> perl(Tk) is needed by fvwm-2.5.6-1
> perl(Tk::Dialog) is needed by fvwm-2.5.6-1
>
> I couldn't find appropriate packages for Redhat 8.0
>
> could someone tell me where I can get these packages?
> When compiling, FvwmGtk support was disabled.

Hmm, yet another "smart" mechanism that should be turned off somehow.
These perl packages are fully optional. Anyway, a quick search shows:

  http://rpmfind.net//linux/RPM/redhat/8.0/i386/Gtk-Perl-0.7008-25.i386.html
  http://ftp.silug.org/pub/kspei/add-ons/redhat/8.0/i386/perl-Tk-804.024-0.i386.rpm

The first is needed for FvwmGtkDebug, the second is really not needed
unless you want to create your own modules in Perl/Tk.

I will upload the official rpms for the latest stable/unstable versions
today/tomorrow. They should not have these dependency problems.

Note, that 2.5.5 is less stable than 2.5.4. At least for me.
