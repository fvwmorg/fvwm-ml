On 04 Sep 2002 20:26:07 +0700, Andrey R. Urazov wrote:
>
> I'm using `de_DE.UTF-8' locale and compiled FVWM 2.4.9 with multibyte
> support. What I got is that I see nothing in WindowList, just empty
> lines, and I see garbage in FvwmPager windows thumbnails. Balloons there
> are also empty. But when a window has a title with Cyrillic letters it
> is displayed correctly, though German umlauts aren't (a header
> containing umlauted letters is not displayed at all).
>
> Without multibyte support instead of Cyrillic titles I get garbage
> there.
>
> Before I used FVWM 2.2.5 from Red Hat 7.2 distribution (with the same
> locale) and everything was okay.

If so, just don't specify --enable-multibyte and you should see the
same results in 2.4.9.

On the other hand I don't know what patches RedHat added to 2.2.5.

It is hard to say anything concrete without knowing details, like whether
an application specifies titles in utf8 or koi8-r. And which fonts
you use in modules and decorations.

Also try the 2.5.3 rpms to see whether you have different results.
