tmo_at_sun4.hep.utexas.edu said:
> Perhaps this falls under the 'Need to fix to work correctly under
> 24bit (TrueColor) displays' in TODO, but whenever I run Netscape on a
> Ultra30 + Creator 3d on fvwm2 .0.46 I see all the graphics, but no or
> little of text. Usually I just get horizontal blue lines where the
> text should be; sometimes I see something (like stuff in <pre></pre>
> or <strong></strong>).

This sounds more like the X-font bug included in Solaris2.6,
which Netscape happily triggers. Try changing your font in
Netscape into one of the linotypes (type 1 fonts are the problem).

Sooner or later Sun might post a patch for this, in the meantime
I'm quite happy using palatino as main font in Netscape.
