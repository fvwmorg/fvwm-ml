After playing around with my .fvwmrc file and configuration recently
I found that on logging in FVWM started up and then chucked me straight out
again... after a breif spell in NCDwm purgatory I'm back in FVWM heaven.
I eventually traced the fault to a missing or faulty icon in one of my
menus. And it struck me that having FVWM dump you out just because it
can't read an pixmap is silly.. It didn't even leave any errors for me to
read.. After all if it can't find a colour it just says "couldn't parse
firebick2" or whatever.

Would it not be better for it to report the lack of pixmaps and just
carry on regardless leaving a blank space or "unknown1.xpm" instead?
