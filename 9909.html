What exactly did you try that does not work, did you actually try what I
said? There were several similar questions in the past. It works.

  Exec fvwm-root -r somebg.png
  Colorset 5 RootTransparent
  #MenuStyle * MenuColorset 5 # redundant for fvwm-themes

Or better yet:

  Colorset 5 RootTransparent buffer, bg average, Tint black 30, bgTint black 30

I don't think you may currently have RootTransparent and TiledPixmap,
Olivier may correct me. But RootTransparent and Tint looks well.

problem. How about Subject: transparency in menus with tiled png image
