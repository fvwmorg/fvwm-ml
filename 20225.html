hello to all.
like a few people I like the windowshade command. thanks to whoever
wrote it. however I use it in a similar way to inconify.

what I would like is for a window that has been shaded and then inconified
to revert to full when it is deiconified.

perhaps including windowshade into the iconify function would work.

you could then have something like

iconify 2 for windowshade. (perhaps keep an alias so that
                windowshade could continue to be used as a command)

then
iconify -1 would deiconify to full yet the actions of iconify and
                windowshade you remain spearate for each other to the user.

hopefully these changes will not involve too much coding.

thanks

Graeme
