JLT> Any smooth scrolling at the edge of the screen is a function of
JLT> your X server, not of the window manager. Thus any solution will
JLT> not involve FVWM. The answer is specific to your X server and

Unless of course he means that he really doesn't want to use the
virtual desktop features of fvwm, in which case he should either make
the DesktopSize be 1x1 or use the version 1.xx command TogglePaging to
turn off paging from his .fvwmrc file.
