>>>>> Andreas Schumann writes:

  Andreas> Does anybody know how to configure the Emacs icon title so that the
  Andreas> filename of the current buffer
  Andreas> appears in the title instead of
  Andreas> emacs_at_HOSTNAME ?

Modify icon-title-format and/or frame-title-format to get what you
want.

(setq frame-title-format "%b") ought to do something useful for you,
but is untested.
