>>>>> Mark Boolootian writes:

  Mark> o When I run some applications (e.g. imagetool, audiotool)
  Mark> under fvwm2, subsidiary windows, such as those for properties,
  Mark> don't have any mechanism for removing them from your screen.
  Mark> Under openwindows, you normally can click the "pushpin" to
  Mark> dismiss the window, but under fvwm2 (as I have it configured),
  Mark> no such clickable button exists. Is there any way to
  Mark> configure for this?

Bind the "Close" function to a menu entry or to a button and use that
to dismiss the windows. I've used this a lot, and it works well. You
can even use a pushpin pixmap for the button if you like... :-)
