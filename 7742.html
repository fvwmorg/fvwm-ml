> I don't think your fvwm2rc
>is part of the problem.

The problem is that left clicks are treated as right clicks in applications
(not on window buttons) and a left click can only be simulated by clicking
left+right together. As mentioned this behavior is only *in* applications,
not in fvwm itself. Left click is left click on window title bar and left
click is right click in i.e. galeon.
