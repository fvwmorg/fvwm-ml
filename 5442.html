> mouse it won't go to the right side of the taskbar to give me a two direction

Actually, your description doesn't sound like you are using fvwm
at all. FvwmTaskBar can only be at the top or the bottom of the
screen, not on the left or right side. Other taskbars can usually
be dragged around the screen: press and hold the first mouse
button over an empty part of the taskbar, then drag it towards the
bottom center of the screen. The taskbar should jump there
directly or show its outline in the new position.
