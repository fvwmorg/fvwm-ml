RTFM:
       Key Keyname Context Modifiers Function
                           [snip] Keyname is one of the entries from the file
              /usr/include/X11/keysymdef.h, with the leading XK_ omitted, but
              only key names that are generated with no modifier keys or with
              just the Shift key held are guaranteed to work. [snip]

So, you should take a look at /usr/include/X11/keysymdef.h.
