Hey Frank,

Sometime in the late 1900s you wrote:

> It most often happens when i'm running netscape. I'll right click to back
> up, or download an image or whatever, and the mouse button "stick." The box
> that popped up when i right clicked stays there when i let go AND it won't
> let me click on anything else, w/ any of the buttons. The keboard hangs,
> too...or at lest seems to...i can hit "Stop, A" and then "boot" to reboot the
> terminal...but that's it.

   I remember a similar problem. Almost exactly as you
   described it. You even may find a post by me in the
   fvwm2 list archives regarding this very same problem.

   For me it was with netscape. This would ONLY occur
   when netscape was still "busy" retrieving the page
   when I would right click to go back.

   It would seem that the entire box was stuck. But not
   so. Netscape would have a hold on the xserver, that's
   all. On my Linux/86 box I could switch to a virtual
   terminal/console and kill netscape from there and
   return to the X session and things would be fine.

   Another way out would be to just wait a while. I
   think it took something like 3 to 5 minutes or so
   and then Netscape would snap out of it and let go.
   Then things were back to normal.

   I have not noticed this behavior w/Netscape 4.04
   yet.

   Hope this helps,
