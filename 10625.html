Dear all,

my fvwm configuration is as described above:
- 4 Desks, organized 1x4
- 2 DeskTops per Desk, organized 1x2

I managed to configure

Key Up A M Scroll +0 -100
Key Down A M Scroll +0 +100

such that I can press <alt-up> or <alt-down> to switch between the
DeskTops.

However, is there any way that I can configure a key-binding to switch
between the desks?

If possible, I would like to have <alt-Up> to switch the Desk one
Desk upwards if I am already on the upper Desktop, Otherwise, I would
be more than fine if I could bind <alt-PgDown> to switch the Desks
downwards, respectively <alt-PgUp> the other way round...
