Well I don't know what version you are running but I'm using version 8,
and I know it lets me drag buttons around. Just hold left mouse down on
the one you want to move and drag it on top of the one that you want it to
come after i.e if you have buttons 1 2 3 and want to make the order 1 3 2,
you just drag button three onto 1 and then the preferences screen will
list them as 1 3 2
