Try the HP-UX Porting and Archive Centre

The packages are available at the following WWW sites:
UK: http://hpux.csc.liv.ac.uk/ Germany: http://hpux.ask.uni-karlsruhe.de/
US (WI): http://hpux.cae.wisc.edu/ France: http://hpux.cict.fr/
Canada: http://hpux.ee.ualberta.ca/ Netherlands: http://hpux.ced.tudelft.nl/
Italy: http://hpux.dsi.unimi.it/ South Africa: http://hpux.petech.ac.za/
Japan: http://hpux.u-aizu.ac.jp/ US (UT): http://hpux.cs.utah.edu/

The software has been tested on Series 700 machines with at least one
of these versions of HP-UX: 8.07, 9.01, 9.03, 9.05 or 10.10. Most packages will
work on Series 300, 400 and 800 machines running the same release of HP-UX.
