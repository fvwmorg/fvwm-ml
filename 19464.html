Quoting Harold H. Baillie (baillie_at_c2i2.com):
> I am getting a [FVWM]{ExecuteModule] error about unknown module
> odulePath. I've tried eliminating blank lines around my ModulePath
> entry in my .fvwmrc file, moving the line to the top - the message comes
> and goes. I am invoking fvwm from .xsession with fvwm -f system.fvwmrc.

I experienced a similar error on my system (HP-UX 10.10) when I
compiled fvwm2-2.0.45 with gcc -O2. Switching to HP-UX's native c
compiler and removing all optimizations made the problem go away for
me.
