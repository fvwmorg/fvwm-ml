Having gotten tired of always going through a menu of choices to get to the
"Window Ident" option, I designed a button-style I could bind to FvwmIdent:

ButtonStyle 5 13 25x20_at_1 75x20_at_1 75x38_at_0 60x38_at_0 60x62_at_0 75x62_at_1 75x80_at_0 \
        25x80_at_0 25x62_at_1 40x62_at_1 40x38_at_1 25x38_at_0 25x20_at_1

(spacing is for readability only, of course).

It's a stylized "I" that is the 3rd button in my left grouping (button 1 brings
up my menu of window operations, and button 3 brings up a transient window list
(my Perl/Tk windowlist, in this case)).
