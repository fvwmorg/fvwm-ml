On Sun, 8 Mar 1998, Brian Hauber wrote:

: Is there a way to have icons of minimized windows go to a speicifc
: place on the screen ala mwm? I haven't been able to locate anything
: in the documents and would appreciate any info you could provide me
: with.
:

I believe you are looking for IconBox .. I'm using something
[correctly || incorrectly ] like this:

Style "*" DecorateTransient,IconBox 920 90 980 440,SloppyFocus

To place icons in a region defined by the four numbers. Read the
FvwmIconBox (1) man page for more details.
