Hi.

I thought I'd tell you folks about this hack I've been using for a few
years. I use fvwm because it doesn't force me to make undue use of the mouse.
I've gone a few steps further and wrote this program which maps each quadrant
of the screen to the keys on the keypad. Pressing a keypad key warps the
pointer to the most representative window in that quadrant of the screen.

For example, pressing KP_7 warps the pointer to the most salient window of
the upper left of the screen. Pressing KP_3, warps the point to the window
in the lower right corner of the screen. The program is simple and is
documented at the top of the source:

   http://www.xcf.berkeley.edu/~ali/wpointer.cc

Hope you find it as useful as I have.
Enjoy!

 Ali.
