Kai Grossjohann wrote:
>
> The first click in a window does two things:
>
> (1) it gives the window focus
> (2) it is passed on to the application
>
> How do I turn off (2)? I don't want my Emacs to do strange things
> just because I click in it to give it focus!

The Motif Window Manager has the properties passButtons and
passSelectButton to enable/disable this feature. It seems to me like
fvwm should have a similar Style or keyword.

I use ClickToFocus all the time with fvwm and have learned (the hard
way!) to click on an "appropriate" area of a window that I want to
raise. This is especially important in text editors where I could
possibly lose my cursor position [frown].
