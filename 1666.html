This kind of suggestion should go the fvwm-workers list, but its not
completely off topic here.

I think most users .fvwm2rc's are way too big to post, and no one
would want them anyway. I know mine is. Besides that, mine is in about
15 pieces.

Notice that on the Vector buttons page, the config files ARE included.
I think that targetted approach is the way to go. Ie., what config file
lines to you need to get a certain appearance.

We could have FvwmPager configs, FvwmButton configs, Border configs,
Menu configs, you get the idea.

There shouldn't be anything copyrighted on the screenshots page.
Thats come up a few times, (in the context of the background images)
and when there was doubt, we've changed it.

If you have anything you think should be on the web site, submit it
to the fvwm-workers list.
