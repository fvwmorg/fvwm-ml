  Alan> Unfortunately, while the 1.x Windowlist warped the pointer to

Here's what I have in .fvwm2rc, and it's been working just fine for
quite a while now. Still works fine with 2.0.43.

Key m A 3 WindowList

I hit Alt-m, then move around with j and k or CursorUp and CursorDown,
then select a window with Space or Return. Note: you might need to
hit a key twice for it to take effect. Especially if you select
windows using the given number.
