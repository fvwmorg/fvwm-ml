On 17 Apr 2004 01:32:58 +0800, xiangbing wrote:
>
> I sometimes click the close button and suddenly find that I do not
> really want to close this window.Not loosing the mouse,I move the
> cursor elsewhere,then loose it.Still,the window closed.

You may solve this by creating this safety feel (taken from fvwm-themes):

  DestroyFunc DeleteOrDestroy
  AddToFunc DeleteOrDestroy
  + H Nop
  + M Nop
  + C Delete
  + D Destroy

Then clicking or double clicking close the window. Just holding the mouse
button (with or without moving) does nothing.
