On 24 Jul 2002 19:17:15 -0400, Timothy Groves wrote:
>
> 1) How can I bind a keystroke to act as though I'd just clicked on the
> Start button? (Meta_L comes to mind...)

Something like (press left windows key):

  Key Super_L A A Menu StartMenu Root 0 o100-25p

> 2) Is it still possible to have a quick-launch button on the TaskBar?

No, FvwmTaskBar has 3 fixed parts: start menu, window list and clock+mail.
FvwmButtons has no such limitation.
