  Grant> One question - what is "warping"?

Warping is moving the mouse pointer by software control (ie without
the phyical pointing device acutally being moved). Warping is
extensively done by OpenLook style programs. Fvwm also does some
warping (with the Focus function, for instance, or if you choose a
window from WinList).
