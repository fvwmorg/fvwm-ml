I'm not sure I understand this, but to find out if its the PNG module
causing problems you can simply remove "Tk::PNG" from the use line in
the FvwmTabs source code. FvwmTabs will spit out lots of errors every
time it tries to load a .png image, but it should still function.

What does FvwmTabs output on stdout/stderr?
