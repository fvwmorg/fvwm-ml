"Leo deck" <Leo.Deck_at_trw.com> writes:
> Is there any interaction between fvwm95 and .Xresources/.Xdefaults files?

This is the fvwm/fvwm2 mailing list. If by "fvwm95" you mean fvwm2
with config files that make it look like "w95", then read on, or at
least take these answers as answers you would get if you were using
fvwm2.

fvwm2 itself doesn't read or change x-resources.

> It is my understanding that resources defined in .Xresources or .Xdefaults ge
> t loaded into the resource database by the Resource Manager (RM). How do I g
> et access to the resource database from fvwm95? Do you know of a simple way
> to that the RM has loaded the .Xresources/.Xdefaults information into the res
> ource database?

The resource database is read or displayed by the xrdb command.
Try "man xrdb" for starters.
