I have to admit that it's been ages since I last used fvwm. However,
I'm thinking of switching back, mostly because I'm getting tired of a
lot of the bloat that's going around in the newer window managers and
because I'm intrigued by some of fvwm's new features.

So, I'm hoping that some kind soul(s) will be able to answer some of my
questions.

1. Is it possible for desktops to remember which of their windows was in
focus? That way if I jump from desktop to desktop, the window which had
focus before will have focus again, regardless of where the mouse is.

2. I've noticed support for gtk and imlib in the lastest snapshots. Is
it possible to have "more graphical" titlebars now? Something like one
that is only half or a third as wide as the window.

Thanks in advance...
