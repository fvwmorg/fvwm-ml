On Fri, May 28, 1999 at 12:05:55AM +0100, Benjamin Rusholme wrote:
>
> Hi. I'm trying to write the value of a string to the FvwmPager title (the
> one internal to the window which defaults to "Desk 0"). Putting:
>
> *FvwmPagerLabel 0 $blah
>
> in my .fvwm2rc prints the literal title "$blah". Any ideas on the
> correct notation? I'm running 2.2.

This is not possible. You may be able to do something with the
preprocessor modules (FvwmCpp and FvwmM4).
 
> Also is it possible to turn the internal title off?

Yes:

*FvwmPagerFont none
