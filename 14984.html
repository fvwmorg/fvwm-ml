> I'm an Fvwm newbie running version 2.0.39 on an SGI IRIX 5.3 system.
> Most things work, but I can't get FvwmForm to do a QuitVerify box.
> I've copied Example 1 from the FvwmForm man page into my .fvwmrc file,
> and I invoke it in a menu that looks like this:
>
> AddToMenu "Preferences" "Fvwm Control" Title
> + "Modules" Popup Module-Popup
> + "Backgrounds" Popup Backgrounds
> + "" Nop
> + "Window Functions" Popup WindowFunctions
> + "" Nop
> + "Exit FVWM" Module FvwmForm QuitVerify
>
> When I do it, nothing happens. How do I debug this?

Your menu is essentially the same as one I use without any problem, so
may be the problem is in your definition of QuitVerify. Here's what I
use, FWIW:

##########################QuitVerify###################################
*QuitVerifyGrabServer
*QuitVerifyWarpPointer
*QuitVerifyFont -adobe-helvetica-bold-r-*-*-12-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
*QuitVerifyButtonFont -adobe-helvetica-bold-r-*-*-12-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
*QuitVerifyFore Black
*QuitVerifyBack #a0a0a0
*QuitVerifyItemFore Black
*QuitVerifyItemBack #a0a0a0
*QuitVerifyCommand Beep
*QuitVerifyLine center
*QuitVerifyText "Do you really want to logout?"
*QuitVerifyLine expand
*QuitVerifyButton quit "Logout" ^M
*QuitVerifyCommand Quit
*QuitVerifyButton quit "Cancel" ^[
*QuitVerifyCommand Nop
