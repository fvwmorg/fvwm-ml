Gregg Dameron <gregg.dameron_at_lmco.com> writes:
> I'm a longtime fvwm95 user converting to fvwm2. I have 2.4.5 installed
> on a Solaris 7 box. One thing I've noticed with fvwm2 -- a FrameMaker
> document window doesn't redraw its interior when I use the Pager to
> switch to another desk, then switch back. I have to refresh the screen
> or window (or minimize then restore it, or drag another window across
> it) to see the document's contents. I tried
>
> Style * BackingStore
> Style * SaveUnder
>
> and their "Off" counterparts, with no luck. The problem doesn't occur
> under fvwm95. Any suggestions welcome.

Thats a known problem.

The window refreshes OK when you change pages, but not desks.
The easiest workaround until someone fixes it is to hit
Control L in Framemaker.
