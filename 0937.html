Dominik Vogt wrote:
> On Wed, May 31, 2000 at 05:13:21PM -0700, Peter Bismuti wrote:
> > I'm having a problem with the WebLogic Admin console, one
> > of the dialogue boxs pops up blank and cannot be killed
> > without killing the whole app.
>
> This all looks like the dialog box crashes the whole
> application. Try using fvwm's 'Close' command to close
> the window. My guess is that the application does not
> do anything in reply. A 'Destroy' will certainly kill
> the whole application.

Better yet use fvwm's 'Delete' command. If nothing happens it means the
application doesn't support the delete protocol and consequently the
fvwm 'Close' command will revert to doing a 'Destroy'.
