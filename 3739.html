Christian Lyra <lyra_at_pop-pr.rnp.br> writes:
> Hi,
>
> I just got one of those "multimedia" usb keyboards, and want to bind the
> multimedia keys to actions in FVWM. I can get the scancodes of those keys
> using xev, but how can I use it on my .fvwmrc?

xev will tell you the scan code and the keysym.
The keysym is what you would use in your .fvwm2rc.

If the key doesn't have a keysym, or if the keysym is not unique,
use xmodmap to assign a keysym to the key.
