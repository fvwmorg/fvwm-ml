On Thu, Jul 08, 2004 at 08:52:32AM -0500, Shay wrote:
> Really just one question. Is there a way to remove iconified windows
> from the WinList?

Depending on what you mean with "winlist":

 * The command "WindowList": yes, use the NoIcons option.
 * The module FvwmIconMan: yes, use the ShowNoIcons option.
 * The module FvwmWinList: no.
