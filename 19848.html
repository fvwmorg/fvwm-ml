On Sun, 20 Apr 1997, cobal wrote:

> I'm using FVWM 1.xx that came with my distribution. It's throwing
> windows taller and wider than the current page. In netscape this is
> particulary bad - the mail window is so tall - I have to jump to the
> adjoining page and shrink the mail window (usually 3 or 4 inches) with
> the title bar.
>
> Am I doing somthing wrong? Am I overlooking a fix? Thanks.

No, you're not doing anything wrong. You just need a little information
you haven't found yet.

Most applications will allow you to specify the size and placement of
their windows. I don't have the information handy right now, but I think
if you man fvwm and look at the section on geometry you'll find what you
need.

Also take a look at the Netscape app-defaults file. I think there are
geometry entries in there -- just edit them to taste.
