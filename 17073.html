I think I may have another possible bugger here.

Swallow FvwmPager into FvwmButtons and set the "Respawn" attribute.

i.e.

*FvwmButtons(1x1 Size 64 38 Swallow(Respawn) "xmailbox" 'Exec xmailbox')
*FvwmButtons(1x1 Icon x.xbm Action 'Exec "" xgo')
*FvwmButtons(1x1 Swallow(Respawn) "xdaliclock" 'Exec xdaliclock')
*FvwmButtons(1x2 Swallow(Respawn) "xcmap" 'Exec xcmap')
*FvwmButtons(1x1 Swallow(Respawn) "xload" 'Exec xload')
*FvwmButtons(1x4 Swallow(Respawn) "FvwmPager" 'FvwmPager 0 3')

Now, drag a window on the pager around (you may have to take it to
another Desk, I'm not 100% sure right now.

Anyway. . . Buttons seems to kill (Pager dies?) and Swallow and then
Respawn a new Pager.

At least the system is usable but really annoying. Can anyone duplicate
this or is my system peculiar? Incidentally, the list above is the only
Buttons I have in my bar, nothing else.
