I was curious about support for D&D on fvwm2. I saw that FvwmWharf supports
the OffiX protocol. On the linux-announcements awhile ago there was an
announcement of the standardization of D&D on X. I believe the
specification for the protocol is at http://www.cco.caltech.edu/~jafl/xdnd.

What's interesting about it is that the toolkits Gtk+,Qt, and most
importantly to me, Xemacs, will be supporting the protocol. They have a
sample implementation that looks pretty easy to modify.

Does Fvwm have a D&D protocol? Are there any plans to implement one?
