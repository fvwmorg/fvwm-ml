Tkgoodstuff's Biff client doesn't do animations (check out "coolmail" for
that), but does do multiple mailboxes (including MH and IMAP mailboxes),
listing and reading new messages, different "alert levels" assigning different
xpm icons to different mailboxes, all with GUI configuration.

http://www-personal.umich.edu/~markcrim/tkgoodstuff

Tkgoodstuff (a button bar with a lot of extra functionality, some of it
connected with fvwm) is just about finalized for a "major release" (a
once-a-year sort of thing). So comments are very welcome.
