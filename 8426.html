I got it!

DestroyModuleConfig FvwmEvent: *
Module FvwmEvent

*FvwmEvent: Cmd "Exec exec rplay "

*FvwmEvent: StartDelay 3
*FvwmEvent: Delay 1

*FvwmEvent: new_page "~/sounds/new_page.au"
*FvwmEvent: new_desk "~/sounds/new_page.au"

Thanks for getting me on the right path!
