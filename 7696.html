Yes, simply add a section "ServerFlags" like this:

Section "ServerFlags"
       Option "AllowMouseOpenFail" "True"
EndSection

and unplug your mouse. You will still have a pointer on the display because
X11 allow to move it with the keyboard.

Roland.
