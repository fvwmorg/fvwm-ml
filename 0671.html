> I configured FvwmWinList to geometry +0-0 and TitleAtBottom. But it refuses
> to stay there and wants approximatively +0-16. Without TitleAtBotton, in fact with
> NoTitle, all is correct. Not a great deal.

Yes, this is a bug. As a temporary workaround, you can execute
'Style FvwmWinList TitleAtBottom' *after* FvwmWinList is on the screen
by adding '+ I Wait FvwmWinList' to StartFunction.

> I experienced some very strange behavior with some kind of windows. I use

> server. All other windows work well but these appear with random geometries. Sometime
> they flicker before ending with very small sizes... They doesn't act like this in v2.2.2.

You should supply more info. What does 'random geometries' mean?
Consider to report this to fvwm-workers list.
Probably output of 'xwininfo -size' on these windows may help.
