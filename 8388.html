> And, last question, is it possible to have, in the title bar, a title
> bigger than the bar-height. (Ok, it is not clear, but think of Keramik...
> An active window has his title bigger than the rest). I don't think it is

You can't currently have different font/title-height/border-width for
active and inactive windows. But you may try to have different
RightOfText/UnderText/LeftOfText images to create a similar effect.
