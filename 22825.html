On 14 Sep 1998, Lee Willis wrote:

> > 42) Why do NumLock and CapsLock interfere with ClickToFocus and/or my
> > mouse bindings?
> >
> > A: Because they are treated as modifiers.

That's why I didn't find it. When I loose keybindings, I'm not going
to search through a FAQ for words like "Numlock" or "Modifier".
Hmm, "mouse bindings".... Forgot that. Maybe the question should be
rephrased to something like:

42) How can I restore keybindings or mousebindings when they don't work
    anymore for no apparent reason.

A: Probably you pressed Caps-Lock or Num-Lock, which X (and fvwm
also, then), treat like modifiers. Press 'm again and your bindings
will work again.
