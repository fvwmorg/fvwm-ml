I would also like something like this,where I swallow IconMan and use
it for a task bar. I can get FvwmButtons to swallow it, but the only
thing my clicks to is iconify stuff. Here are my FvwmIconMan setup
scripts and the swallow command:

 *FvwmIconMan*nummanagers 1
    *FvwmIconMan*background Black
    *FvwmIconMan*foreground Green
    *FvwmIconMan*drawicons true
    *FvwmIconMan*format "%t"
    *FvwmIconMan*buttongeometry 1024x18
    *FvwmIconMan*managergeometry 1x1
    *FvwmIconMan*drawicons true
    *FvwmIconMan*resolution global
    *FvwmIconMan*followfocus true
    *FvwmIconMan*focusbutton down black Green
    *FvwmIconMan*focusandselectbutton down black Green
    *FvwmIconMan*selectbutton up black Green
    *FvwmIconMan*dontshow class=FvwmButtons resource=FvwmPager
    *FvwmIconMan*action Mouse 1 N sendcommand Focus, \
                        sendcommand "Iconify -1"
    *FvwmIconMan*action Mouse 2 N sendcommand "Iconify"

me.
