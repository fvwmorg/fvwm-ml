While themes.org is still in the process of upgrading their
hardware and software a temporary site has been placed at
http://fvwm.themes.org which introduces the fvwm-themes
enhancement package for fvwm.

In the next month or so, fvwm will be incorporated into the
main themes.org software design like all the other window
managers.

Comments, opinions and suggestions regarding fvwm.themes.org
can be directed to fvwm_at_themes.org or via Internet Relay Chat
on irc.openprojects.net and #themes.org

Volunteers to assist in the creation and maintenance of the
new site are welcome to apply.
