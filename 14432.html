I use fvwm-pre-2.0-pl35. On a window's left button (button 0), I have bound
the usual menu (mwm-style). But the menu doesn't fit "snug" attached to the
title bar, but leaves a one-pixel row to the left and top free; the original
window contents are visible in this area. I think it would look "more
professional" (or rather "more mwm-ish") if the menu would attach directly
to the title bar (and left resize bar, respectively).

    Martin
__
Here's my config:

    Mouse 0 1 A Menu Window-Corner Close

with the menu

    AddToMenu Window-Corner "Restore Alt+F5" Nop
    + "&Move Alt+F7" Move-or-Raise
    + "&Size Alt+F8" Resize-or-Raise
    + "&Lower Alt+F3" Lower
    + "Raise" Raise
    ....
and
    # Keyboard accelerators
    Key F3 A M Lower
    Key F4 A M Destroy
    ...
    Key F10 A M Maximize
