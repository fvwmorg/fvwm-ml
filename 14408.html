Hi!

I have been running FVWM on all (UN*X) machines I have ever worked
on (HP, SGI, SUN, Linux) and it has always been the best window
manager I know of.

A few days ago I switched to a SGI with a 1280x1024 display. (the
graphics board is a GR2-Elan).

On this machine FVWM crashes every time that I log in: Everything
starts up as usual, but after I have moved the mouse around the
screen for a short time, everything that is related with the
window manager just freezes. Using cut&paste with the mouse I
can kill and restart FVWM. Usually it works without any problems
after I have killed and restarted it once. On some occasions I
had to repeat this process several times.

This is really strange, and I have no explanation for it. I even
recompiled FVWM on *this* machine, but no luck.

If it is of any help, I have included the output of FvwmDebug
(started from .fvwmrc) and my .fvwmrc at the end of this mailing.
(gzipped and uuencoded).

I would really appreciate help. Even a simple work around would
help me a lot!

Thanks a lot in advance,
 Andy.
