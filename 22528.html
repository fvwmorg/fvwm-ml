"unmanaged" means "the WM doesn't know about these windows". Such windows, of
of course, will behave as if you set "Sticky" -- fvwm doesn't unmap -- and
"StaysOnTop" -- apparently such windows are always left at the top of the
window stack...

As for the class/resource names, is there a difference if you start
"wine -managed"?
