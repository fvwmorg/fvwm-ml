Yesterday i thought the same way. But the truth is that this make things worse.
You should instead of this use the Mail-Followup-To: fvwm <fvwm_at_fvwm.org>. The
problem with this is that only a few eMail Clients are aware of it. And I think
mozilla isn't one of them. :-( Also have a look at Sven's signature.
