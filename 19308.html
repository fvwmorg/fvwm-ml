>>>>> Charles Karney writes:

  Charles> Mouse 1 TS A Move-or-Raise

  Charles> AddToFunc Move-or-Raise "I" Raise
  Charles> + "M" Move
  Charles> + "D" Lower

  Charles> My problem is that a click of Mouse 1 on the title bar (to
  Charles> bring the window up) causes the window to lose focus for
  Charles> about a second.

Fvwm waits to see if the click is a double click. There's a timeout
for that which is by default set to 750 ms, I believe. You can try to
click then immediately move the mouse a few pixels, focus should
return to the window quickly.

I think.
