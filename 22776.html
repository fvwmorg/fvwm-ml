Hello,

I use Fvwm with the Raise, Lower, RaiseLower options to perform the obvious
window operations.

Say I have 10 windows on the desktop and one window (theone with the focus)
is on top of another 6.
Clicking lower or raiselower (by selecting the window operation from a menu)
will place the window below all other 6 windows.
It would be nice if there was a StepLower function that placed a window only
below the one right below it. That way things get a little easier on cluttered desktops, especially when using the top three of six windows all at a time on
a small screen.
