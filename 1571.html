On 31 Oct 2000 13:53:01 -0800, Hyper Faze wrote:
>
> I'm trying to get a bigger button bar on my desktop

[...]

> *FvwmButtons(2x1, Swallow "FvwmPager" "FvwmPager 0 0 &")

You can replace this line with something like:

  *FvwmButtons(2x1, Size 150x80, Swallow "FvwmPager" "FvwmPager * *")

Or specify geometry for the full button bar:

  *FvwmButtonsGeometry 500x400-0+0
