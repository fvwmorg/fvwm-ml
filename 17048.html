Well, I figured out some of my icon problems from a few days ago and
now I am able to attach the nice GNU icon to my EMACS session. Now I
am trying to work out how to make the nasty text labels go away.

Specifically, I have the following attributes in my .fvwm2rc

Style "*" NoIcon
Style "emacs" Icon gnu.xpm

This gives me the gnu where I expect it. The problem comes when I fire
up xterms. I would like to have no iconified form on the screen. That
way, I could have those xterms which are uniconified visible and those
which are iconified would appear only in the winList. What I get is
an icon with no picture but with a title bar. Can I make this go
away?
