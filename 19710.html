Hi!

I working on SunOs with Fvwm 2.45...
But our department is going to migrate to Solaris.

After successfuly instalation Fvwm on Solaris I missed FvwmButtons.
Altrough it's work just nice on SunOs.

What's wrong? I didn't receive neither error messages nor any
notifications...:(

And another problem...

In SunOs I have the Decoration defined like this

### Xterm Decor ###
DestroyDecor XTerm
AddToDecor XTerm
+ TitleStyle LeftJustified
+ ButtonStyle 1 ActiveUp( Pixmap mini-display.xpm) Inactive ( Pixmap
mini.xterm.xpm )
#+ ButtonStyle 1 Pixmap mini-display.xpm ActiveUp
#+ ButtonStyle 1 Pixmap mini.xterm.xpm ActiveDown
+ ButtonStyle 2 Pixmap win95-close-full.xpm ActiveUp
+ ButtonStyle 4 Pixmap win95-maximize-full.xpm ActiveUp
+ ButtonStyle 6 Pixmap win95-minimize-full.xpm ActiveUp
+ BorderStyle Active -- HiddenHandles NoInset
+ BorderStyle Inactive -- HiddenHandles NoInset
+ HilightColor blue PaleGreen
+ TitleStyle ActiveUp (VGradient 40 2 PaleGreen 60 #60c0a0 40 #60c0a0) -- flat
+ TitleStyle ActiveDown (VGradient 40 2 PaleGreen 60 #60c0a0 40 #60c0a0) --
flat
+ TitleStyle Inactive (VGradient 40 2 white 60 grey 40 grey) -- flat

Have a look at this line:
+ ButtonStyle 1 ActiveUp( Pixmap mini-display.xpm) Inactive ( Pixmap
mini.xterm.xpm )

It doesn't work on Solaris at all!!! Fvwm stopped on this line and doesn't
move.

So this line I replaced to one of commented in Decor definition section.

Why it doesn't work?

I compile the same code on both Solaris and SunOs, what's difference?
