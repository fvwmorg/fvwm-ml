And Cliff Trapp spake, saying:
>
> >
> > > When I exit fvwm, it kills the window manager, but does not exit me from
> > > my session.
> > Try making your own .xinitrc or .xsession file with no & after the
> > fvwm2 command
> >
>
> Thanks everyone. Ordinarily I could edit either one of those, but I am
> running CDE which is either misconfigured or configured NOT to use either
> my own .xinitrc or my .xsession.

I missed what OS you're running on, but I know that under the HPUX
CDE you can create a two-line file called ~/.dtprofile containing:

<blank line>
SESSIONTYPE=xdm

And it will pass control to your .xsession after you log in. Give it
a try, perhaps it will work on your system.
