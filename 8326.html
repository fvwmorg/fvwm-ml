Hi Rob.

I don't know who called you. I've been waiting for them to
respond, but I guess its not going to happen.

Your name comes up from time to time on this list.
It came up recently when we were discussing Fvwm's 10th
birthday.

I haven't been paying a lot of attention to this, (I forget
birthdays all the time), but I believe we figured out that
the 10th anniversary of Fvwm's first release is June 1 of this year.

You appear in the birthday plans like this:

  C. Find Robert J. Nation

       Priority: medium
       Responsible: Dominik
       Status: ongoing

       Everybody is asked to help. Hopefully we can find him, but we won't
       cancel the event if we do not.

Also this:

  B. Pictures of the developers

       Goal: Make a web page with pictures of the fvwm developers.
       Priority: low, nice to have
       Responsible: volunteer needed!
       Status: planning schedule

       Everybody who ever made a patch or helped designing any feature is
       welcome to provide a picture.

       20-May
        * Collect pictures of the developers.
          [pending]
       25-May
        * Make a web page with the pictures of the developers.
          [pending]

Thanks for getting back to us and I hope things are going well for you.
We've been enjoying working on Fvwm over the years and we owe you thanks
for getting this started.
