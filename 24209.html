"brian McC" <bnm1972_at_hotmail.com> writes:
>
> Where do my minimized windows go? With fvwm, they would iconize and end up
> in the corner of the screen. Since I've switched to fvwm2, they just
> disappear and the only way to summon them back is to use the
> right-button-on-desktop window menu.
>
> Can someone tell me how to change this behavior so that my minimized
> windows
> become iconized?

Look for "Style * NoIcon" somewhere in your configuration. Remove
the "NoIcon" part.

There are differences between fvwm and fvwm2 as to how the iconbox(es)
are defined. Search the fvwm2 man page for "iconbox", "icongrid",
"iconfill".
