I have a fvwm-2.0.45 setup with lots of toolbars, somewhat along the
lines of redhat. The different toolbars are constructed automatically
by m4 from the menus, and all this works just fine.

What I miss is a delete toolbar button to add to the constructed
toolbars. The problem is which FVWM command to associate with the
command.

- the 'KillModule' command doesn't work as the toolbars are started
  with the FVVWM command 'Module FvwmButtons <toolbar-name>'. The
  'KillModule' command doesn't know about aliases as used above.

- the 'Delete' and 'Close' commands doesn't work either as they means
  one have to double-click the delete toolbar icon.

What can be used instead? Any options on any of the above commands
that will make them work?
