> I'd like to keep my background on restart.
> Any reason, why fvwm2 sets the background to black and then to this grey
> pattern?

Remove the following line from InitFunction and RestartFunction.

+ "I" exec xsetroot -mod 2 2 -fg \#554055 -bg \#705070

It is in the default .fvwm2rc. If you copied it over, you probably have
this line.
