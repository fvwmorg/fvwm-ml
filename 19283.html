Romano Giannetti <romano_at_iet.unipi.it> writes:

> I used the hack proposed by albrecht (thanks*1000!) and it works for me:
>
> AddToFunc raise_if_exist "I" WindowId $w raise
> AddToFunc "InitFunction" "I" Module FvwmAuto 1500 raise_if_exist
>
> I have no more black dots since then. Hope this helps you.

Thanks to all who answered.

Well, I tried this and is working so far.
That "WindowId" trick is really slick, Albrecht.

Now, if one could have functions as arguments, we could say:
AddToFunc do_if_exist "I" WindowId $w $0 $1...$9
and call:
do_if_exist iconify -1
