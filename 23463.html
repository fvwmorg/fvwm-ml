Denis Kholodar wrote:
> commands like:
> "xterm -geometry 72x40+0+0"
> "xemacs -geometry 72x40+0+0"
> work fine, but when i try to place my netscape window at 0:0 with
> "netscape -geometry 863x625+0+0"
> it comes up somewhere at 0:25 or 10:50 etc.

  What version of fvwm?


  Under fvwm2 [all I've tried], having (e.g.):

Netscape.MailThread.geometry: 643x690+426+140
Netscape.Composition.geometry: 540x799+10+10
Netscape.Navigator.geometry: 758x735+10+10

  ... in my app-defaults/Netscape works a treat.


  We do now know that, due a Netscape funny [not fvwm], you won't be able
to apply StartsIconic to Netscape - you have to use -iconic, and doing
this on pre 2.1.7 stopped StartsOnPage working, but I think that's all
fixed now. *I* don't have any problems any more.
