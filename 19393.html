>Also, I saw that people who istalled the Xaw95
> widgets xterm title bar looked like Win95's title bar. How do I get
> mine like that? You know, with the icon of the app in the upper left
> corner

To get the "mini-icon" at the upper left conner.
  First you have to have something similar to this in your .fvwm2rc
        Style "emacs*" MiniIcon mini-edit.xpm,Icon emacs.xpm
  Then put
        ButtonStyle 1 MiniIcon
   and you have to comment out the old ButtonStyle for button 1.
> the "-" and "x" in the upper right hand corner. PLEASE HELP
> ME OUT!!! Thanks in advance!

I haven't done this before but you can use pixmap for each button style.
