I'm a BIG fan of FVWM
 (the Father-of-all Virtual Window Managers - isn't that what the F meant :-)

Here are a few things that I've noticed (bugs ?) using 2.0.45

1. FlipFocus seems to disappear when you Restart your fvwm session (via
   'Restart fvwm2' in the .fvwm2rc file) - this happened on both 2.0.45 &
   2.0.44. By disappearing I mean I get the following error when I try to
   invoke the function after a restart


        [FVWM][executeModule]: <<ERROR>> No such module /download/fvwm-2.0.45/modules/ALLmodules FlipFocus


   I've also noticed that FlipFocus goes awry sometimes and when
   asked to FlipFocus it flips between my xterm and FvwmButtons (even
   though I wasn't focused on FvwmButtons - it ignores the other window
   that I _was_ focused in).

        Key F1 A C Prev [!iconic] FlipFocus

   I've informed Dan Niles (FlipFocus' author) about the Restart problem.


2. I have seen the same problem that Lance Brown (labrown_at_splat.niehs.nih.gov)
   mentioned in his email about FvwmAuto (not being able to focus); I've been
   at times unable to focus on xterms when I was previously focused in some
   application window such as Netscape/emacs. I would have to leave the
   xterm and enter it a few times before it finally focuses. I know this is
   not much of a description, but I can't really place it myself - it seems
   rather random.

   I've also seen a problem with rxvt losing/recapturing focus. If you
   overlap your rxvt with any other window - say by 10% and briefly go to
   the rxvt and return back, you'll notice that the focus keeps shifting
   from the rxvt to the other window until you move your mouse deeper into
   one window or the other (if you don't move the mouse this shifting stays
   indefinitely). Since I haven't been able to replicate this problem on
   two normal xterms (ie. not involving rxvt), I'm assuming this is more a
   problem with rxvt rather than FvwmAuto

        + "I" Module FvwmAuto 200


3. I liked (still do) an old fvwm-1.24 function - 'TogglePage' and would love
   to see it come back. If it does not, no harm done. I've been able to
   replicate its behavior by including the following segment in my .fvwm2rc
   file

        AddToMenu MyTogglePage
        + "ON" EdgeScroll 100 100
        + "OFF" EdgeScroll 0 0

   The 'EdgeScroll 0 0' works fine UNLESS you are moving a window in which
   case the scrolling is enabled. I am not sure if this enabling of scrolling
   upon moving windows is intentional or whether this is a bug (I tend to
   believe its the later).


Lastly, a request; I read the archived version of the emails
(www.hpc.uh.edu/fvwm/archive/) and it would be much nicer to remove the
FVWM background/wallpaper (with yellowish tint) from the archived directory
since it makes it rather difficult to see anything within Netscape.

Many Thanks,

 - Nadim
