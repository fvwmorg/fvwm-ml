FVWM list gets far fewer spams than, say, the openssh or xfree86
mailing lists. Yes, the host runs spamassassin. Yes, it has many
hand-tuned filters. Yes, they work; thousands of spams are stopped

any spam that accidentally makes it through the filters, I suggest you
leave the list because those filters will simply never be perfect.
