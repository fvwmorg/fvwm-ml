On Mon, Feb 24, 2003 at 03:00:57AM -0800, Prabu Subroto wrote:
> Dear my friends...
>
> I just succeeded to make XWindows Kiosk with FVWM2. On
> FVWM2 it runs perfectly and very easy to imlement.
>
> My boss want the monitor of each computer is on for 24
> hours (around the clock). I don't know how to do that.
> Must I switch off the screen saver? But How?
> Today the monitor is off every 3 minutes.

You probably have the screen blanking feature in the X server
enabled. Use

  $ xset s off

to turn it off. If that does not help. look for processes named
xlock, xlockmore, xscreensaver, etc. and prevent that they are
started.
