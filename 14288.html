>> I am peeping at fvwm 2.0 as it develops. I wonder wether the function
>> "OpaqueResize" from fvwm 1.x has been renamed, accidently removed or
>> permanently removed?

ericding> It's now called OpaqueMoveSize.

It is not right. OpaqueMoveSize in fvwm-2 is equivalent to OpaqueMove
in fvwm-1.

it may have some error (because I made this list when pre2.0p1? ...).
