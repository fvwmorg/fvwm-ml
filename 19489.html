Hi all,

I think that MouseFocusClickRaises is the best thing since sliced
bread :-). And I think that it could be made even better if there was a
new option MouseFocusRaiseClickDoesntPassClick or something,
analogously to ClickToFocusDoesntPassClick.

Ie if I click into a window in MouseFocus or SloppyFocus mode said
window is raised but the click isn't passed to the window.

Whatcha think?
