 --- Mikko Heiskanen <mikko_at_whitecortex.net> wrote:
> Yes, I dug that up from the manpages, the StrippledTitle(-s). :)
> But, for some reason, I can not get the stripes on windows.
> I am a bit lost in the configuration file, it could most probably
> be because of that (http://whitecortex.net/~mikko/dot.fvwm2rc)
> Although, I've picked most of it from system.fvwm2rc and manpages.

I looked at your config, and I saw this line:

Style * SloppyFocus, ClickToFocusPassesClick, ClickToFocusRaises, \
        MouseFocusClickRaises, GrabFocusOff, \
                NoPPosition, NoIcon


You therefore just need to add "StippledTitle" to the list, thus:

Style * SloppyFocus, ClickToFocusPassesClick, ClickToFocusRaises, \
        MouseFocusClickRaises, GrabFocusOff, \
                NoPPosition, NoIcon, StippledTitle
