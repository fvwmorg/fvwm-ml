> I am confused which config file should I use. I have installed the RedHat 5.1 and
> configured the XF86.
> I find that .fvwmrc is for ver.1.xx and .fvwm2rc is for ver.2.xx, right?

right

> How can I find out which version I am running (beside looking at xinitrc because I cannot
> interpret it properly)?
> Do I then use the appropriate configure file in my home directory?

go ahead and look at your .xinitrc. At the bottom there should be a
line saying "fvwm &" or maybe "fvwm2 &". To be sure of which
version your running, find the shortcut ("fvwm" or "fvwm2") and do an
"ls -l". That'll tell you where the link points. I'm afraid i can't be any
more specific 'cause i'm in windoze right now. I'll get Linux configured
during Christmas break. Right now exams are my major priority.

Since your running RH 5.1, i'd wager to guess that you are running
fvwm 2.0.xx (i've got 2.0.46 around here somewhere...haven't gotten
around to installing it yet). If u get a "windoze-ish" interface, then
your probably running fvwm95 (a hacked config of 2.0.45...i think).

bon chance ("good luck")
