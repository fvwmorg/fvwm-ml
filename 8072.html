Thomas Glanzmann <sithglan_at_stud.uni-erlangen.de> writes:
> I am looking for a way to announce Workspaces like in wmaker. Is there a X
> Application which I could use together with FvwmBacker?

I guess you want to play an audio clip on a desk change?
If you have a shell named "playsound", arg 1 will be the desk number:

Heres how to test it:

KillModule FvwmEvent
DestroyModuleConfig FvwmEvent:*
*FvwmEvent: PassID
*FvwmEvent: Cmd Exec
*FvwmEvent: new_desk playsound
Module FvwmEvent

Put FvwmEvent in your StartFunction when done.
