On Fri, Jul 04, 2003 at 01:17:45PM +0800, Razvan Musaloiu-E. wrote:
> Hi!
>
> I'm running fvwm 2.4.15 and I don't know how to make Gaim (ver 0.64) not
> to popup at every received message. I try using:
> Style "Gaim*" GrabFocusOff GrabFocusTransientOff
> but didn't work. :-(

According to what FvwmIdent says about gaim, it seems to take the
focus itself. In this case, there is little you can do. Identify
the exact name of the annoying popups (with FvwmIdent if
necessary) and try

  Style <window_name> GrabFocusOff, GrabFocusTransientOff, NeverFocus
