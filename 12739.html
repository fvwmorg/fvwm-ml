> I want to have iconize button (4) on the
> left side

In FVWM button 4 is actually the 2nd button (from the left) on the right
side of the title bar. Read the "Mouse" command in the "fvwm" man page.
What you are asking for is actually:

Mouse 1 7 A Iconify

> while it always go to the right side.

The "IconBox" command defines where iconified windows will appear.
See the "fvwm" man page.

> Also how to get a button to close a window and put
> it on the right side of the title bar.

Use a "Mouse" binding (like above) & use the "Close" or "Destroy"
as the mouse function/action.
