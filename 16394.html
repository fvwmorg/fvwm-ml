Both fvwm 1 and 2 can use more modifiers. I've been using them since
1.22. Ensure that the key in question is bound (via xmodmap - but get
xkeycaps from ftp.x.org - you'll be glad you did) to mod2, mod3, mod4
or mod5. mod1 is typically the Meta key. Then use the modifier
number in .fvwmrc. For example, on my Sun keyboard, the "diamond" keys
(left and right) are meta and I've bound the alt and compose keys to
mod2. In .fvwm2rc I have:

Key Down A 2M Popup "mainmenu"

So when I press the down arrow in combination with Alt (that's the 2)
and the Meta keys I get my menu popup.
