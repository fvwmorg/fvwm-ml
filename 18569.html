I like exmh. This is a tcl/tk program that sits on top of the mh mail
utilites. Therefore, there is more to setting things up when compared to
openwindows mailtool. You have to install tcl/tk and the mh utilities, but
I believe that its worth it.

get exmh from ftp.sunlabs.com or http://www.sunlabs.com/~bwelch/exmh/index.html
get tcl/tk from http://www.sun.com/960710/cover/dltcl.html
get mh from ftp://ftp.ics.uci.edu/pub/mh
