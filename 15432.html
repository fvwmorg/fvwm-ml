You, Robert Nation 885-9815 write:

> I can't actually look at these because they're encoded base64 instead of
> uuencode, (how do you decode a base64 message?)

You need munpack and mpack. They're on most mime support sites, so you
should be able to find them with archie or one of the web search
engines.


? but remember that to do

> have always seemed EXTREMELY slow to me. You might get away with it,
> beause they are all going to rectangular shapes. To get a feel for this,
> try resizing an oclock, and see how long it takes to complete (almost
> a second for me).
>

(uuencoded) and it's all square, so I don't think the shape extensions
