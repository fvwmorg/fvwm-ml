Hi all,

There are 3 new transparent themes in my collection: Glass, Water, and
Wine.

There's a screenshot
<http://www.twobarleycorns.net/fvwm/glass_decor_screen.png>
showing the Glass decor. I'm using FvwmPager and making a toolbar out of
a FvwmButtons swallowing a FvwmIconMan. (FvwmTaskBar is too crashy with
transparency, especially on Sun.) The FvwmIconBox module is being used
to control the clutter of the Gimp's many icons. The relevant configs
are there. Fvwm workers, you can consider that a screenshot submission :-)

A tarball of the whole thing except for screen wallpapers is available
as before. You can get it all at
<http://www.twobarleycorns.net/fvwm-decors.html>
