With the recent talk of restart problems, I though I'd add that
I cannot Restart FVWM2 V2.0.41 at all. Whenever I do, I get returned
to the shell (X-Windows exits).

I'm running FVWM on top of Openwin 3.0. Has anyone else had this problem
at all?

-Wolf


I have:

AddToFunc RestartFunction "I" Module FvwmButtons
 
-and-

AddToMenu Quit-Verify "Really Quit Fvwm?" Title
+ "Yes, Really Quit%mini.exit.xpm%" Quit
+ "Start twm" Restart twm
+ "Restart Fvwm" Restart /home/cwolf/FVWM2/bin/fvwm2
+ "Start mwm" Restart mwm
+ "Start olwm" Restart /usr/openwin/bin/olwm
+ "No, Don't Quit" Nop
