Hi!
I started using 2.3.12 and now .13, for the 2.2.* series FvwmAuto module
seems to have a bug fixed in 2.3.* with not bringing to top e.g. netscape
middle clicked (open in new window) windows.

Now 2.3.13 works fine, except that sometimes the FvwmAuto module isn't
started. When I do a restart Fvwm2 then it is. Also some of my Applets which
should be swallowed and started by FvwmButtons aren't executed.
Here is my Init section:

+ "I" exec Esetroot -scale /root/tolkien/movie/ringsart6.jpg
+ "I" Module FvwmAuto 555 Raise Nop
+ "I" Module FvwmWinList
+ "I" Exec astime -bg "#266294" -fg yellow -day white -date white -ampm white -sec lightgreen -fh -nh -geometry -8-8 -exe xcalendar
+ "I" Exec ascpu -u 1 -bg "#266294" -fg yellow -position -5-69 -exe xcalendar
+ "I" Exec asmem -used -bg "#266294" -fg white -position -5-133
+ "I" Exec aterm -sh 66 -sl 333 -geometry 90x14-70-0 +vb -tr -trsb -fg grey84 -T "X Messages" -fn "-b&h-lucidatypewriter-medium-r-*-*-13-*-*-*-*-*-*-*" -e tail -c 16384 -f /var/log/messages

Note that the exact same init section works fine with 2.2.*
