Actually, it does look for the file in the system area though. It
just doesn't look for the file with the word 'system' in front of it.

Here is the search method:

  $HOME/.fvwm2rc
  FvwmDir/.fvwm2rc

where FvwmDir is specified in the Fvwm.tmpl file.
