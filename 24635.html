Lee Willis <lee_at_gbdirect.co.uk> writes:
> Are there any good resources for screenshots and associated .fvwm2rc
> files. I've checked the site at www.fvwm.org but I can't seem to find
> any up to date information ...

There is a "screenshots" section under construction. Right now, its
mostly screenshots (not many .fvwm2rc files), and its only available
for CVS download.

Hopefully it will appear on our web site soon.

Anyone can download it and display it locally:

cvs -d :pserver:anonymous_at_cvs.fvwm.org:/home/cvs/fvwm checkout fvwm-web

(if CVS asks you to login, the password is "guest".)

You can then display it locally, using "file:".

If anyone would like to contribute, send your desktop as a "gif" to me
along with the caption you would like.
I'll take care of the thumbnail.
