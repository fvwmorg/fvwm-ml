Activating is quite simple.

In file definitions.inc line 270 needs to be changed from

if( get_user_setting("no_software_patents") || ! get_user_setting("aswp") ) {

to

if( get_user_setting("no_software_patents") ) {

to activate the protest site by default.
To see the fvwm sites the string ?no_software_patents=1
needs to be added to the fvwm urls. The links at the protest
site do that automatically.

I could activate it Sunday or Monday. If there are no
protest I will activate the protest at Monday at latest. I
will also insert www.fvwm.org to the protest wiki.

I am for leaving the site closed till Thursday or Friday.
