Ali Faour <afaour_at_newbridge.com> writes:
> Hi,
>
> I installed fvwm-2.2.4 and xpm packages. How can I use the xpm icons?
>
> e.g.: when I iconifiy XTERM, I get a small barre at the top right of the
> screen with
> the word XTERM written on it. I would like also to use other icons for
> netscape, cmdtool, ... etc.
> my system is SunOS Generic_105181-17 sun4u sparc
> SUNW,UltraSPARC-IIi-Engine (sol 2.6)
>
> Where are the default icons stored? and how they are called?

You have to install the icons in a place of your own choosing.
You can get some icons at http://www.fvwm.org.

Lets say you installed the icons in /usr/local/icons.

Add to your .fvwm2rc:

ImagePath /usr/local/icons:+
Style * Icon default.xpm
Style XTerm Icon xterm.xpm
Style cmdtool Icon toolbox.xpm
