I have compiled a small collection of colour xpm icons for use in
the fvwm (2.0) menus. If you are interrested, here they are, uuencoded,
tarred and gzip'ed. Their size are 14x14 pixels and they use the same 8
colours, all of them.
 It would be nice to get icons for even more applications, like xv and emacs,
in that small size. (I think)
Enjoy, if you want, and have a nice day, wherever you are.


/ Ludvig A. Norin
