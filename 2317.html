Martin Maciaszek writes:
> While reading the xterm man-page I stumbled over a cool feature of
> xterm called active icons. When minimized, the icon of xterm schows
> a miniature view of the xterm. The man-page says further that the
> minimized xterms can receive key strokes if the window manager
> supports it. What settings are needed to let the minimized xterms
> receive the key strokes? I couldn't find any hint on that.

You can get the same effect by setting the font to a size of 1 pixel.
This may be available on cntrl-Button3 as "Unreadable". If not, use
"Selection" to set the font to nil2.
