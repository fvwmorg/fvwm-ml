On Wed, 15 May 2002, Mattias Holmstroem wrote:

> hi all, got a question...
> when I remove my mouse pointer from my xterm(unfocus it) I want the
> window color to change to black window and black text, how do I do this?
>
> I have found the section where I can change the HiForeColor to #000000
> and HiBackColor to #000000... whats is the name for the unfocused
> varibel? I have tried StdForeColor and StdBackColor, they didnt work...
>
> MvH Mattias "Nallen" Holmström

I don't think xterm is going to do this for you. If you use aterm
instead, it has a "-fade" command line option.

Starting an aterm as "aterm -fade 0" will cause it to go black
when unfocussed.
