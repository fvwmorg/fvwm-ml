On 16 Aug 2004 23:38:11 +0200, Julian Leyh wrote:
>
> Andreas Zimmermann <And.Zimmermann_at_web.de> wrote:
>
> > i got problems setting geometry on my pager. This is my config:
> >
> > *FvwmPager: Geometry 71x53+0-0
>
> are you shure, this is for your version of FvwmPager ?
> look at FvwmPager(1)
>
> for fvwm 2.2.5, for example, you'd have to use this:
> *FvwmPagerGeometry geometry
>
> for fvwm 2.5.10, your config should work..

2.2.x is not supported. The colon-space syntax works since 2.3.x and this
is the only valid syntax guaranteed to continue to work in the future.

Returning to the original problem. Is it possible you call a pager alias,
like "FvwmPager MyPager"? To see it works, start FvwmConsole and type:

  *FvwmPager: Geometry 71x53+0-0
  FvwmPager
