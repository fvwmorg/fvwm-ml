I use xclock with fvwm-2.0.43

*FvwmButtons(5x1, Swallow (UseOld, NoKill, NoClose) "xclock" \
        'Exec xclock -g 206x33+279+0 -fg NavyBlue -bg gray90 -digital -update 1\
        -fn -*-courier-medium-r-normal--17-*-75-75-m-90-iso8859-1 &')
