On 22 Oct 2002 13:37:54 -0400, Victor Eijkhout wrote:
>
> Style options seem to be ORred together. That means that if I reread
> my rc file after editing out some option I don't get rid of it. Is
> there a style analogon to DestroyFunc and such? Can I zero out all
> styles at the top of the file?

You may DestroyStyle *, but this is not recommended.

All style options without exceptions have their opposites, so you may undo
any style option on the fly. But, of course, the Style line order matters.

Just open the man page and search (using "/" key) for the style option
that you want to undo, you will see an opposite style option.
