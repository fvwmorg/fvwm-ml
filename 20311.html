My .fvwm2rc contains the lines:

PixmapPath /home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps:/home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps/Icons/reduced:/home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps/Buttons

As this line is long and wraps off the screen, I tried to split it across lines like this:

PixmapPath \
        /home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps: \
        /home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps/Icons/reduced: \
        /home/bjs/Documents/Video/Pictures/Pixmaps/Buttons

But my pixmaps were no longer found! So did I do something wrong, or is this in fact something missing from Fvwm? If it is missing, I think it would be a really great thing to implement.
