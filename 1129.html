On Fri, Jul 14, 2000 at 05:00:17PM +0100, Martin Eyles wrote:
> Is there any way to swallow the component bar of netscape into the
> button bar
>
> I tried this
> *FvwmButtons(1x6,Swallow "Netscape" "Exec /usr/local/bin/netscape
> -component-bar")
> but just got netscape to behave very bizzarely

Maybe you ought to try changing the "Hangon" value to
"mozillaComponentBar"?
 
> Alternatively, is there a way to get netscapes componet bar to apear
> without any title bar next to the button bar on all desktops (ie - not
> swallowed, but looks and feels like it)

You can certainly do this. You just need to feed explicit geometry
(try setting this as an X resource in the Netscape.ad file) and then
match "mozillaComponentBar" for the appropriate decoration-less
behavior, like:

Style "mozillaComponentBar" BorderWidth 0, NoHandles, NoTitle
