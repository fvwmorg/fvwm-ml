On Thu, 16 Jan 1997, Lionel Cons wrote:
> I have a simple question. Some users report that you can't change the
> focus when you use ClickToFocus and when the NumLock key has been
> pressed. Do you know how to make the ClickToFocus ignore the state of
> NumLock?

It's not just click to focus. All mouse and key bindings look at num-lock
state. It seems that only bindings with the Any modifer flag work with
numlock on.
