On 22 May 2002 23:41:59 -0400, Eduardo Gargiulo wrote:
>
> I'd customized .fvwm2rc, but now, when I hit F4 to show up WindowList,
> the process list disappear when i release the key and next time it
> appear in the upper left corner of the window focused. How can I freeze
> the WindowList so i could choose any process?

I assume you press Alt-F4 not F4 and WindowList disappears when you
release Alt. Add SelectOnRelease option with no parameter.

  Key F4 A M WindowList Root c c NoDeskSort, SelectOnRelease

> Another question: how can I customize (eg: desktop #) the text of each
> process in windowlist?

I am not sure what do you mean here. There are several options that affect
the menu item format and several options that affect the sorting. Try:

  WindowList Geometry
  WindowList NoGeometry
  WindowList NoGeometryWithInfo
  WindowList NoDeskNum, NoNumInDeskTitle
  WindowList NoCurrentDeskTitle, TitleForAllDesks

The options in the last two lines are only available in 2.5.x.
Please contact the man page for the description of WindowList options.
