On 17 Nov 2004 10:07:51 -0500, Phil Stracchino wrote:
>
> First of all, sorry if I've missed something in the documentation
> explaining how to do this, but I'm not quite certain what I should be
> looking for.
>
> Is there any way in fvwm2 that I can set things up such that a specific
> window uses SmartPlacement if there is sufficient clear space available
> on the desktop, but if it cannot be placed without overlapping another
> window, then it is placed at a specified position (possibly its own
> requested geometry)?
>
> Can anyone suggest ways of accomplishing this?

There is no currently way to do this. You may try TileCascadePlacement
and TileManualPlacement, these are close to what you want. The first
works pretty well.
