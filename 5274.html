Tim Hanson <tjhanson_at_attbi.com> writes:
> I confess that I'm using the standard FVWM2 that came with SuSE 7.1, so
> I don't have the current program, but I have resisted moving to either
> the standard GNOME or KDE desktops because of familiarity and the small
> footprint of FVWM. SUSE 8 is on the way in the mail.
>
> Is there a utility which would create menus (either Buttons or the
> standard mouse menus) from a directory of links on the fly? It sure
> would be nice if I could alter my menu structure simply by moving links
> and icons to a specific "~/FVWM_Menu" directory, rather than dealing
> with .fvwm2rc.

There are 2 shells in the utils directory:

fvwm_make_browse_menu
fvwm_make_directory_menu

that demonstrate how to make a menu from directories.

Maybe, those shells will give you some ideas.
