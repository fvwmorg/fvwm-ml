> Hint: Did you look at the error message generated by the binding?

[FVWM.1][ParseBinding]: <<WARNING>> Got mouse button 6 when the maximum
is 5.
You can't bind complex functions to this button. To suppress this
warning, use: Silent Mouse 6 R A DynMenu



>
> In general, fvwm supports all button numbers up to 9, but X
> supports any button > 5 only partially. There are no bit masks
> for these buttons in X, so dragging with these buttons or running
> complex functions does not work. However, you should be able to
> bind a simple function like
>
> Mouse 6 R A Raise
>
> or
>
> Mouse 6 R A Schedule 0 DynMenu
>
> If this does not work, it's a bug in fvwm.
