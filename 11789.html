> > > works. Could you point me to the FAQ for this? :)

> > The FAQ? This is simply in the manual:
> > Style "mutt" MiniIcon mutt.mini.png
> > (the same for moneydance)
> >
> > cheers,
> > Remko
>
> Thanks, That isn't working for me. I believe this is correct but for
> some reason it won't show the mini's on some apps. Other apps that
> run in xterms show the proper icons but mutt and moneydance, among a
> couple of others, don't. Here is what's in my .fvwm2rc-wm-icons:
> Style "mutt" Icon norm/mutt_button.xpm MiniIcon mini/mutt-mini.xpm

Open FvwmConsole (bind it to a menu or a keypress), then paste into it:

  Style "mutt" MiniIcon mini/mutt-mini.xpm

If the mini icon of mutt is not changed, then either the window has a
different class string, or this mini icon can't be loaded.

If the mini icon of mutt is changed, then either you have an incorrect
order of Style lines (some XTerm overwrittes your mutt), or this window
was not always named "mutt", at least on the capture time.
