Previously...
>I am having trouble with getting borders around windows which are spawned
>by other windows. For example, if I open a netscape browser it has a window
>but if I then click on preferences then the window that pops up has no
>border. The same is true of other programs such as xgopher, xboard,
>and so on...
>
>Can anyone tell me how I can fix this, perhaps by giving me the relevant
>snippet of their fvwm2rc file?

The windows you refer to are generally called transients. Try the
DecorateTransient option. It's a Style, so you could try:

Style * DecorateTransient

Enjoy.
