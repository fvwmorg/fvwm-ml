On Sun, Feb 22, 2004 at 12:06:11PM +0100, Guido Berhoerster wrote:
> Hi,
> I have some trouble with FvwmWharf, it appears to have a problem
> with transparent colorsets which cause it to crash with an error
> message similar to this:
>
> ----snip----
> X Error of failed request: BadWindow (invalid Window parameter)
> Major opcode of failed request: 40 (X_TranslateCoords)
> Resource id in failed request: 0x800005
> Serial number of failed request: 17
> Current serial number in output stream: 17
> ----snap----
>
> Here is a minimal .fvwm2rc to reproduce the problem:
>
> ----snip----
> ImagePath /usr/X11R6/share/fvwm2/pixmaps:+
>
> Colorset 10 RootTransparent, fg black, bg rgb:dcdc/dada/d5d5, hi white, sh\
> rgb:9e9e/9a9a/9191, Tint rgb:dcdc/dada/d5d5 50
>
> DestroyFunc InitFunction
> AddToFunc InitFunction
> + I Module FvwmWharf
> + I Module FvwmConsole
> + I Exec xterm
> #+ I Exec fvwm-root -r background.png
>
> Style FvwmWharf !Title
> Style FvwmWharf !Borders
> Style FvwmWharf ParentalRelativity
>
> *FvwmWharfColorset 10
> *FvwmWharfFullPush
> *FvwmWharfNoBorder
> *FvwmWharf xterm xterm.xpm Exec xterm
> ----snap----
>
> FvwmWharf starts fine but as soon as one sets a background image
> e.g. via fvwm-root -r background.png FvwmWharf crashes with above
> error message.

Should be fixed now.

> I know FvwmWharf is not widely used any more because of
> FvwmButtons but I just tried out FvwmWharf because it has this
> nice feature called 'FullPush' which moves the icon of a button a
> few pixels down and to the right when pressed. That way one can
> get some visual feedback even if the button has no relief around
> it, something that FvwmButtons cannot do.
> I'm using fvwm 2.5.8 compiled on Feb 9 2004 at 20:54:15 with
> support for: ReadLine, XPM, PNG, Shape, XShm, SM, Xinerama,
> XRender, XFT on FreeBSD.
