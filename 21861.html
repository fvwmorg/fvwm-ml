On Sun, 8 Mar 1998, Scott Scriven wrote:

: I remember reading something about windows rolling up into just their
: title bars when minimized, but I can't find it now. Can fvwm do this?
: How?

I think you mean windowshading. In my .fvwm2rc, I have:

AddToFunc "Move-or-Raise" "M" Move
+ "M" Raise
+ "C" Raise
+ "D" Windowshade

and the last line means that double-clicking the title bar make the
rest of the window disappear or re-appear. I also added

+ "&Windowshade" Windowshade

into the AddToMenu "Window-Ops2" section.

Hope this helps. I use it all the time, and don't minimize windows
any more.
