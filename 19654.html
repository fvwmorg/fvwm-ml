don't remember who, but someone recently posted about a title-bar button
for FvwmIdent in fvwm2. try this one---it looks better (don't forget to
figure out which button number to use; this one's button 3, the second
one from the edge on the lefthand side of the title-bar):

ButtonStyle 3 20 50x35_at_0 40x25_at_0 50x15_at_0 60x25_at_1 50x35_at_1 50x40_at_1
\ 60x40_at_0 30x40_at_0 30x50_at_0 40x50_at_1 40x70_at_0 30x70_at_0 30x80_at_0 70x80_at_1
\ 70x70_at_1 60x70_at_0 60x40_at_1 60x70_at_1 70x70_at_0 70x80_at_1
