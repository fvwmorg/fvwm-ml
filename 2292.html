On 21 Mar 2001 13:29:47 -0300, Christian Lyra wrote:
>
> Can anyone plz help me with stroke+fvwm? This is my current fvwm:
>
> FVWM version 2.3.29 compiled on Mar 21 2001 at 11:53:02
> with support for: ReadLine, Stroke, XPM, GNOME WM hints, SM
>
> And I put this at .fvwm2rc:
>
> Mouse 3 A M StrokeFunc
>
> and
>
> Stroke 248 0 R N Exec exec xterm
>
> When I press the Alt key plus 3rd mouse button the cursor change to what I
> supose to be the "stroke" mode. Then I tried to "stroke" but nothing helps!

248 is not a reproducible sequence; 258 or 32489 are reproducible.
Try these commands instead if you want "U", top-right to top-left:

  Mouse 3 A M StrokeFunc FeedBack DrawMotion StrokeWidth 2
  Stroke N9632147 0 A M Exec exec xterm
