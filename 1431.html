On Tue, Sep 19, 2000 at 10:28:47PM -0400, Jerry Alexandratos wrote:
> I've got two configuration questions that I can't seem to find answers
> for.
>
> First, how do I change the shadow color for borders? Ideally, I'd like
> make it the same color as the rest of the borders (thus giving a
> flat/non-bevelled look).

In 2.2.x you can not choose the shadowe colour individually. But
in your case you can use flat borders. Look for the 'flat' option
of the BorderStyle command.
In the 2.3.x beta releases you can control shadow and hilight
colours by using colour sets (look for 'colorset' in the man page).
 
> Second, when using sloppy focus is there any way to auto-raise a window
> without using FvwmAuto?

No. That is what FvwmAuto was made for. Well, you could use FvwmEvent,
but this is even more complex and will work much less reliably.
