Rick Hatton <hattonr_at_aug.com> writes:
> I am having trouble changing the color of hte inactive windos. I have used:
>
> Style "*" ForeColor black
> Style "*" BackColor aquamarine4
>
> and -
>
> Style "*" Color black/aquamarine4
>
> But the inactive windo frame and tile bar remains drkish grey with some very
> light lavenderish/pinkish font color. I as successful in changing the active
> winow frame and title bar colors.
>
> how can I change the inactive window frame/titlebar colors?

Try this:

DestroyDecor MyDecor
AddToDecor MyDecor
+ TitleStyle LeftJustified ActiveUp (Solid Navy -- flat)\
  ActiveDown (Solid Navy -- flat) Inactive (Solid grey51 -- flat)
+ BorderStyle Active (Solid cyan -- HiddenHandles NoInset)\
  Inactive (Solid white -- HiddenHandles NoInset)
Style * UseDecor MyDecor
