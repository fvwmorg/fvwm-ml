Scott Scriven wrote:
> Yep. I tried it, and it does the opposite of what I want.
> "EdgeThickness 0" prevents me from dragging windows across pages (bad),
> but keeps the root menus at screen edges (also bad). :(

EdgeThickness is improved in the current CVS source and will be in the
next beta. It only works once at the moment but you will be able to get
what you want with a function:

DestroyFunc paging_move
AddToFunc paging_move I EdgeThickness 2 # or 1 if it works for your
server
+ I Move
+ I Edgethickness 0
