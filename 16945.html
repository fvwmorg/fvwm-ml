After a nice holiday weekend I'm back to announce that I've created the
fvwm-workers mailing list as promised. To subscribe, send the line

subscribe fvwm-workers

to majordomo_at_hpc.uh.edu and follow the directions that will be returned to
you. You will be required to confirm the subscription at the address you
are trying to subscribe. (This prevents someone from forging a message and
subscribing president_at_whitehouse.gov.)

The list is set up pretty much like this one; I don't have web archives up
yet but I will after there is enough in the archives. The fvwm list and
the fvwm-workers lists are not nested; I can see many instances where
someone would want traffic from fvwm-workers but not fvwm.

That's basically it. All interested parties should subscribe to the new
list but wait a few days before starting up discussion so nobody is left
out. I hope to move all development talk away from the fvwm list in the
next week or so.
