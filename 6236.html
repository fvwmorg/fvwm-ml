On 13 Sep 2002 15:56:45 -0700, Ashok Venkatachar wrote:
>
> I was unable to find xlock in /usr/bin/X11 or elsewhere.
>
> Does fvwm allow screen locks? If so, can anyone tell me where i
> can find it?

You may install either xlockmore package (enhanced xlock)
or xscreensaver package.

Then bind xlock a menu item.
Or run "xscreensaver -no-splash &" in ~/.xinitrc or from InitFunction
and it may lock automatically.
