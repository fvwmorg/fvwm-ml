On 19 Apr 2003 23:53:58 +0200, Marc Dirix wrote:
>
> I was wondering, if there is an option to fvwm to get the title bar
> from the top to the right of the screen.

Yes, it is possible in 2.5.x version:

  Style * TitleAtRight

You may also want (probably not) to use:

  Style * RightTitleRotatedCCW # CCW = counter-clock-wise, default: CW
  Style * !UseTitleDecorRotation # don't rotate titlebar pixmaps/gradients
