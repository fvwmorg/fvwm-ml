Hi there, just downloaded 'snap-20030228' and I'm noticing extra
slight delays in focus specifically on FvwmWinList and FvwmPager.
Seems like the window first gets focus and then the modules get
its indication (its like a 2-step process and the delay is rather
noticeable - wasn't there before).

Not sure what to send in terms of debug or how to go about things,
but I thought I'd mention it.

Maybe this will help -- have FvwmWinList running, start an xterm
and maximize it on a particular desk/page then enter that xterm.
Now switch page and/or desk and notice how WinList gets refreshed
(notice the delay ?). Everything gets displayed, then its WinList's
turn (again, its delayed and its rather noticeable).

BTW: was using 'snap-20021218' before.
