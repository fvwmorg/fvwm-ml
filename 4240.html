>
> I get an error message (not literal but close)- sh: cannot find wmconfig

You probably don't have wmconfig package installed. Run "rpm -q wmconfig".

  http://rpmfind.net/linux/rpm2html/search.php?query=wmconfig

Or maybe you don't have /usr/X11R6/bin in your path.
