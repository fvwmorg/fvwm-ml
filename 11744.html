On Thu, Mar 18, 2004 at 10:04:02AM +0100, Dominik Vogt fvwm-at-fvwm.org |fvwm| wrote:
> On Wed, Mar 17, 2004 at 01:17:38PM -0500, Chris wrote:
>>
>> The really strange thing is that last night, for a time Alt-F1 was
>> working as desired, then it stopped. Control or Shift combinations
>> were fine. Now I can't get any of them to work, even without a
>> modifier.
>
> Make sure your NumLock, ScrollLock or CapsLock key isn't pressed.

 That did it. As soon as I turned Numlock off, it works fine. (In
retrospect, that's not surprising, since Numlock is mod2.) I created
additional keybindings to cover both Meta and Meta+mod2 cases, and now
it works beautifully regardless of Numlock. Even the Alt-Tab behaviour
is now working!

 Unsurprisingly, what I thought might be a problem turns out to be an
error on my part and a lot more power and functionality than expected in
FVWM. Sometimes, it's good to be wrong. :)
