On 09 May 2004 12:30:41 +0200, Xavier Maillard wrote:
>
> How can i have window decorations on the right/left side of any window ?
>
> I just don't want to have 'em on top of my windows.

In 2.5.x:

  Style * TitleAtLeft
  Style XTerm TitleAtLeft, LeftTitleRotatedCW
  Style XLoad TitleAtRight
