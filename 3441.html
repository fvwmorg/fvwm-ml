On Sat, 20 Oct 2001, Neil Zanella wrote:

>
> Hello,
>
> To enable selection of fvwm2 from the kdm login it is sufficient to add an
> entry to add fvwm2 to the SessionTypes line in /etc/X11/xdm/kdmrc and also
> add the lines
>
> fvwm2)
> exec /usr/local/bin/fvwm2
> ;;
>

A little word of warning: Don't forget the ";;", because then it's not
possible to log in even failsafe. The check for failsafe shouldn't really
be in switch block but rather in an if statement before....

> in the /etc/X11/xdm/Xsession file.
>
> For some reason the .xsession file in my home directory was not being
> detected by the startup scripts so I opted for this alternative.
>
