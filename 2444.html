> On Thu, May 03, 2001 at 10:36:43AM -0400, Dan Espen wrote:
> > Martin Maciaszek <mmaciaszek_at_gmx.net> writes:
> > > I want to swallow a date-app, similar to the app in the cde
> > > panel, into my FvwmButtons panel. So far I couldn't find a
> > > suitable app for this. wmdate looked promising but it somehow
> > > doesn't like being swallowed. it's always displayed in a separate
> > > window. Does someone know a date app? (No, I don't want xclock in
> > > digital mode. I already swallowed xclock in a separate button)
> >
> > The only clock like app I see in the CDE panel is dtcm in its iconic
> > state. You should be able to get FvwmButtons to swallow that.
>
> The Problem with dtcm is that I don't have CDE installed :( I'm
> trying to mimic the look of a CDE Desktop on my fvwm system.
> (fvwm-themes looks good but is way too complex for me. So I just
> take the good ideas. *g*)

Ok, then you already have fvwm-themes installed, it enhances your fvwm
instalation even if you don't use it. Try the following from FvwmConsole:

  FvwmScript FvwmApplet-Day
  FvwmScript FvwmApplet-DayClock
  FvwmScript FvwmApplet-DigitalClock

If you use colorsets, you may also give 2 colorset numbers as parameters.

Other 64x64 applets (including Date/Time) as candidates for swallowing:

  http://www.bensinclair.com/dockapp/showstyle.php3?show=name
