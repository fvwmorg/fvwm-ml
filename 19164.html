I have recently upgraded to 2.0.45, and gotten most all of my old look-and-feel
back. But one minor quirk of the old pager that I miss-- when I clicked mouse1
on an app window in the pager, it not only jumped to that quadrant, but it also
gave the focus to that window (I use ClickToFocus on my windows). I have not
found a way to re-create this behavior with the module FvwmPager. As a bonus,
I'd kind of like to have it use something other than "Desk 0" for the title--
I don't use virtual desks, just one 3x3 desktop.

Randy
