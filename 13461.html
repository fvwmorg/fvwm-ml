Hi Frank,

> another strange effect then a windows style is BorderWidth 0

If you unshade before you remove the title it appears to work ok:

  DestroyFunc ToggleShade
  AddToFunc ToggleShade
  + I ThisWindow (Shaded) WindowShade false
  + I TestRc (Match) WindowStyle NoTitle
  + I TestRc (Match) UpdateStyles
  + I TestRc (Match) Break
  + I WindowStyle TitleAt$0
  + I UpdateStyles
  + I WindowShade
  + I WindowStyle Title

SCoTT. :)
