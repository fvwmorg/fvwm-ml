Ever since I switched to Fvwm2 2.0.46 from 2.0.45, my FvwmButtons has stopped
working. I changed nothing in my config file pertaining to it. Everything
compiled without any trouble (on my SunOS 4.1.3 with gcc 2.7.2 and X11R6, not
OpenWIndows).

I used the sample file that came with the module source, and it starts the
clokc, but the buttons never appear. I've watched my processes with 'top' on a
1 second resolutoin, and I am some times lucky enough to see FvwmButtons pop in
for one cycle (I think I hear it laughin at me too...). I don't seem to get any
error messages or anything. I looked through the source, and I didn't see any

or where its exiting.

Has anyone has similar trouble? Does anyone have a suggestion on how I can
track this problem down?
