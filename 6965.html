This is a suggestion from a delighted user, to make an excellent new
fvwm2 feature even better - when used by geriatrics whose shaky hands
cannot be trusted to draw a simple straight line with any degree of
consistency.

Would it be possible to call "stroke" (with Alt + Mouse 2?) from a
window that displays a 3x3 grid? This would ensure that my wavery
diagonal lines and untrue right angles are close enough to the intended
"strokes".
