> I have a question about some of the linked libraries. Specifically XPM.
> I'm trying to install fvwm2.042 but I think my libraries are all screwed
> up. This is the message I'm dying on (I tried taking the whole statement
> out of the source ... but then ran into other problems):
> "Picture.c", line 67: undefined struct/union member: closeness
> "Picture.c", line 69: undefined symbol: XpmCloseness
>
> I've tried compiling on a freshly built sunos 4.1.4 system and also on
> solaris 2.4. I can't seem to get this thing installed because of missing
> libraries and as a side note .. where do I find missing X libraries?

Your problem is that the XPM library doesn't come with the system.

>From the FAQ:

13) I'm trying to build FVWM, but the linker is complaining about
    missing functions like 'XpmReadFileToPixmap' or the compiler can't
    find the include file X11/xpm.h. Why is that?

A: You need to have the Xpm library on your system. You can find the
   source for it at ftp.x.org in /contrib/libraries. Or you can
   compile without Xpm support (see the configure.h file) and lose out
   on all the pretty color icons.
