i love the command line, but i also love saving keystrokes.
last night i got enlightened.

just added:

Mouse 4 R A Scroll +0 -100
Mouse 5 R A Scroll +0 +100

to my .fvwm2rc

and arranged my pager pages vertically

DeskTopSize 1x4

(just one desk)

and my mouse wheel on the root window now takes me to
other pages quickly and cleanly.

i warned it was a silly tip on the subject, so don't flame me, please.
