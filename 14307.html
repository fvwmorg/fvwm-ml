I have 2 things to propose for fvwm.
1. would it be possible to make the window-titles selectable?
This is a feature of gwm. I miss it especially when I have the path of an xterm
window in the title, or while configuring the fvwmrc.
2. Sometimes I start a program, and it takes several seconds to open the
window. If I want the window to open on a particular desktop, I wait there,
till the window comes up. Could it be possible to leave 'markers' on desktops
saying 'I want the next window to open here' ?
