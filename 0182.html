On Wed, 26 Jan 2000, Jos wrote:

> Fvwm understands some 'intuitive' color names like 'yellow'
> or 'grey90' etc. Is it possible to somehow convert these to
> rgb values? I looked in the cpan archive, but couldn't find
> any perl scripts that do this. Does anyone know if there is
> a program around that can?

If you've got gnome around, then there's a "color browser" in the Gnome
Panel "Utilities" menu. It'll do what you want (play with the
options). You can also just look at the flat file /usr/lib/X11/rgb.txt (or
wherever location it may be on your system). It should have the decimal
values there, it's a flat file.
