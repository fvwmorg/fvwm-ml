On Thu, 25 Jan 1996, John Junior Dawkins wrote:

> i am assuming that
>
> AddToFunc "InitFunction" "I" Module FvwmAuto 250
>
> is equivalent to the AutoRaise function in fvwm1.2. but my windows don't
> automatically come to the top when i enter them. i have to click on the borders
> to get them to come to the top. what am i doing wrong?

I use the same thing in this manner:

AddToFunc InitFunction "I" Module FvwmButtons
                           Module FvwmAuto 50

Note that there's no "I" before Module for FvwmAuto...

Hope that helps...
