On 12 Nov 2002 18:55:51 -0600, Norvell Spearman wrote:
>
> fvwm-2.5.4
> kcalc, from kdeutils-2.2.1
>
> Before switching to fvwm, I got used to kcalc in kde. In kde kcalc has
> buttons like non-transients (minimize, close). In fvwm, however, kcalc
> gets a title bar but no minimize button --- even with DecorateTransient
> in my ~/.fvwm2rc. I've noticed this same behavior with kcalc and xfce
> (which is and/or was based on fvwm code). Is there any way to force
> kcalc to appear as non-transient to fvwm?

This is because you have either MwmFunctions or MwmDecor in your config,
both disable a corresponding button if an application asks for this. Do:

  Style kcalc NoFuncHint, NoDecorHint

You may report to kcalc developers that marking kcalc transient and
setting mwm hints is unjustified and bad for a user. How is it in KDE 3?
