On Tue, Feb 26, 2002 at 07:58:35AM -0500, Adam Courchesne wrote:
> I'm running fvwm2 ver 2.4.3 on AIX.
> I have scrolling turned off in the following manner:
>
>
> EdgeScroll 0 0
> EdgeResistance 100000 100000
> EdgeThickness 0
>
> When I drag my cursor to any side of the screen other than the right
> side, it stops at the edge, as if it hit a brick wall. (This is what I
> would expect to hapen). But when I drag it off the right side, it keeps
> going (I.E. disappears) until I drag it back. What dos this happen?
> Can I disable it?

I can think of two possible explanations:

 - The X screen is wider than the resolution of the monitor. No
   idea if this is possible with AIX, and if it is, how to turn it
   off.

 - You can't see the whole X screen on the monitor. Use the
   wheels, buttons, ... on the monitor's case to move the image
   to the left and shrink it if necessary.

BTW, what you describe has nothing to do with the fvwm settings
above.
