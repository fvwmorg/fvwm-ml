There was some dicussion of button styles here recently. Here is one
that I made to bind to the "stick" function. It is three horizonal lines
like those that appear in the title bar when a window is sticky.

ButtonStyle 4 12 10x23_at_0 90x23_at_0 90x28_at_0 10x28_at_1 10x48_at_1 90x48_at_0 90x53_at_0 10x53_at_1 10x71_at_2 90x72_at_0 90x77_at_0 10x77_at_1
