On Tue, Nov 14, 2000 at 11:27:15AM -0800, Perry Hutchison wrote:
> This seems like it ought to be an FAQ, but I haven't been able to
> find the answer. Is there a simple method of causing the FvwmConsole
> window to have a title bar so it can be iconified?
>
> Fvwm Version 2.2.4 compiled on Feb 4 2000 at 03:41:44
> FvwmConsole Ver. 1.3, Red Hat Linux 6.2

You'll want to see FAQ entry 3.1, on configuring FVWM on RH systems.
But what you're looking to disable is probably a line in the config
like:

Style "Fvwm*" NoTitle

Or, an additional line like:

Style "FvwmConsole" Title

after the notitle line.
