> To avoid page flipping when you open the window you could try to
> de-iconify first and then warp to the window:
>
> + I iconify off
> + I warptowindow 50 50

  I *want* it to page flip! I was just remarking that although the icon
had migrated to (1,0), FVWM still [correctly] identified the window with
(0,0). Mind you, this is true of most apps: I've often had an icon
mysteriously appear on the wrong page, only for it to deiconise back into
the correct page.


  Here's something related to try: bind a key to 'Iconise' (Sun's "Open"
FKey for me). *Double* click/hit the key over an open window. Usually, the
window iconises, then deiconises (natch) ... but the window's transparent!
You only get the FVWM decor, not the window itself.

  I've just done this again to check it's still there - and I can't
remember how to get it back!
