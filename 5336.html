On 17 Jun 2002 12:03:16 +0200, Christian MICHON wrote:
>
> when I try to use xpm cursors, I can't have any shade of gray in it, like
> in windoze. I understand that, due to the standard way of defining cursors
> and their mask in b&w, this may not be possible.
>
> If I migrate to 2.5.1+ and PNG support, will the cursors also be black and
> white ?

This is not related to xpm vus png.

Cursors have 2 colors, inner and outer, or fg and bg. By default these
colors are black and white. But you may choose any other 2 colors:

  CursorStyle MENU left_ptr yellow black

The same syntax applies to the pixmap cursors.

  CursorStyle MENU myimage.xpm red darkred

If X supports more than 2 colors, we may support this too.
