Most of you probably know this already, but I thought I'd mention it
anyway, as I haven't really seen any references to it.

I noticed the other day that when you run fvwm 2.0.42 under Xnest, at
least on my machine (slow Tseng 4k video card), it runs rather
sluggishly. I believe an older one like 2.0.38 runs a bit better.
In any case, if anyone out there feels like optimizing fvwm, or I
believe some people were trying to debug some "undocumented"
blinking... This might be a way to see it more easily. And for those
poor soles who have insanely fast hardware, running a couple nested
Xnest's should be able to slow X down to human readable speeds. :-) I
believe, however, Xnest is an X11R6 program, and I have no idea if
there is an R5 version (or if that's even feasable).

Just a thought,
