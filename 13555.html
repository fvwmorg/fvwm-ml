I was asked to publish my .fvwm2rc. So I decided to pack some edited
editions of my ~/gtk configs, ~/fvwm files and some additional ~/X configs
into a tarball. They're available here:
<http://www.wolfgangpfeiffer.com/fvwm.pfeiffer.tar.gz>

Almost certainly there will be bugs in these configs. They work
here. But as one knows: The fact something works does not
necessarily mean it's right. So please make the usual backups before
running these configs.
