On Fri, 17 Apr 1998, Frank Wilcox, Jr. wrote:

> my right mouse button keeps "locking." The terminal behaves as if the right
> mouse button is being held down. This prevents me from being do
> anything...except reboot.

This is strange and I don't really know what's wrong. May not even be a
FVWM problem.

>
> Also, is it possible to place the button bar vertically on the side of the
> screen than horizontally on the bottom? (I'm using version
> 2.somethin'-or-other)

Yes, use:
*FvwmButtonsRows 1
instead of defining *FvwmButtonColumns,
and, of course, choose the right position be setting *FvwmButtonsGeometry.
