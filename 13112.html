It may be time consuming to learn and fix 30MB or 140KB of complex
configurations, you should not expect this.

Please make a minimal config file (10-20 lines) showing the problem with
"fvwm -f my-minimal-config". It is possible you will be able to reproduce
this problem without any images and even fix it yourself once you nail it.

> Is there a possibility to upload this package to a dedicated FTP server ?

I think you may find a free web hosting, so you could post just urls of
images and simple configs to the mailing list. Large tarballs are not
the best thing to attach.
