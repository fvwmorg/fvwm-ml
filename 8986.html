Bubblemon is the wrong color on my Solaris box(en) too. Instead of blue sky and
water, the background is red. It looks more like a bloody duck-hunting scene
than an idyllic pond. I took a quick look at the code and I think it's directly
fiddling the bits of the color specification, so I suspect it assumes
little-endian x86 byte order and doesn't do the right thing on the big-endian
sparc. See <http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/b/big_endian.html> I'd probably learn
something if I tried to fix it, but don't see when I'll ever get time.
