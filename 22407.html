Neil Zanella <nzanella_at_cs.mun.ca> writes:

> Are the entries you get with ctrl-mouse buttons(s) on an xterm
> configurable?

I think so.

> Are they configurable vi the .fvwm2rc file? (those that say Main
> Options, VT Options, VT Fonts)?

No. You need to set these using X resources (in your .Xdefaults file,
or perhaps somewhere else, depending). Look for a file XTerm, in
/usr/openwin/lib/app-defaults, /usr/X11/lib/app-defaults, or somewhere
similar.
