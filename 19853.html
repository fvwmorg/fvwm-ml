On 21 Apr 1997, Graeme McCaffery (Graeme.McCaffery_at_astro.cf.ac.uk) wrote:
> does anyone have a function that on a mouse click will either
> (on a single click) raise/lower a window or windowshade (on a
> double click)

Try the following. Clicking on the titlebar will raise a window, double
clicking will WindowShade the window:

Mouse 1 T A Move-or-Raise
AddToFunc Move-or-Raise "C" Raise
+ "M" Move
+ "D" WindowShade

Hope this helps,
